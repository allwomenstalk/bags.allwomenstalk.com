# 9 Classic Looking Chain Strap Purses ...

Chain strap purse styles are quite timeless. No doubt made popular by the classic Chanel bags, this is a look that’s often emulated by other designers and department store brands. Chain strap purses make a great addition to any wardrobe and look great worn during the day or night. Take a look at just a few purses with chain straps, below.

## 1. Forever21 Quilted Chain Strap Purse

_ **Price:** $26.80 at forever21.com_
This faux leather purse has a classic quilted design with a woven chain shoulder strap. It also has a fold-over flap front and twist lock closure. The chain strap is detachable so this purse can also double as a clutch. This purse would look great worn with a chic black and white outfit.

## 2. ASOS Panelled Chain Bag

![ASOS Panelled Chain Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/04/2_asos-panelled-chain-bag_300x470.jpg)

**Price:** $59.00 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/K7t1FV)
This medium sized handbag is ideal for carrying your daily essentials. It has a short chain strap with a leatherette panel at the top. This bag has a fairly basic rectangular design, but the panelling on the fold-over flap adds some interesting detail.

## 3. Lottie D’or Quilted Chain Strap Purse

![Lottie D’or Quilted Chain Strap Purse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/04/3_lottie-dor-quilted-chain-strap-purse_300x183.jpg)

**Price:** $69.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Choose a lighter coloured handbag, such as a cream one, as an alternative to basic black. The look is still classic, but better suited to wearing with lighter colour palettes. This purse has classic quilted detailing and a long woven chain strap. You can easily alternate between wearing this purse across the body or on one shoulder.

## 4. Mulberry Lily Leather Shoulder Bag

![Mulberry Lily Leather Shoulder Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/04/4_mulberry-lily-leather-shoulder-bag_300x229.jpg)

**Price:** $755.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/uEXPcV)
If you can afford designer chain strap purses, then the Mulberry ‘Lily’ is a stylish choice. The bag is a contemporary take on the more classic Mulberry designs. It has a woven chain shoulder strap and features a turnlock fastening on the front flap. Wear it with anything and everything, and you’ll definitely get your money’s worth.

## 5. DKNY Quilted Leather Chain Strap Purse

![DKNY Quilted Leather Chain Strap Purse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/04/5_dkny-quilted-leather-chain-strap-purse_300x209.jpg)

**Price:** $155.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/xM5qwP)
This beige quilted leather purse is perfect for taking from day to night. It has a simple rectangular design and features a zip top fastening. It also comes with two retractable chain straps. Wear it over your shoulder during the day and retract the straps in at night to use it as a clutch.

## 6. Forever21 Padlock Heart Crossbody Bag

_ **Price:** $19.80 at forever21.com_
This cute handbag features a heart shaped padlock at the front. It also has a long combination chain link and leatherette shoulder strap. This bag would be perfect for taking with you for a night out on the town.

## 7. ASOS Quilted Lock across Body Bag

![ASOS Quilted Lock across Body Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/04/7_asos-quilted-lock-across-body-bag_300x220.jpg)

**Price:** $30.00 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/gHdDvL)
In case you haven’t realised by now, quilted leather and chain straps go hand-in-hand. The look pretty much sums up classic, sophisticated style. Work a masculine/feminine look by teaming this chain strap purse with cigarette style pants, button-up blouse, and patent lace-up shoes.

## 8. Mango Quilted Tote Bag

![Mango Quilted Tote Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/04/8_mango-quilted-tote-bag_300x281.jpg)

For a chain strap purse that can carry a bit more, take a look at this quilted tote. It has a quilted base, zip top fastening, and long chain handle. It’d make a stylish everyday bag and look great teamed with a range of different outfits.

## 9. Tory Burch Robinson Leather Shoulder Bag

![Tory Burch Robinson Leather Shoulder Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/04/9_tory-burch-robinson-leather-shoulder-bag_300x189.jpg)

If you want to add a timeless and sophisticated touch to your look, a chain strap purse is just what you need. Emulate the essence of Coco Chanel with a quilted purse or go for a smooth leather purse for something more modern and streamlined. Which of these chain strap purses do you like the most?
