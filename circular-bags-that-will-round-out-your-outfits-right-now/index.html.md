# 7 Circular Bags That Will round out Your Outfits Right Now ...

Circular bags are a fun way to change up your outfits this season. They’re a little bit quirky and are a far cry from your ordinary day to day tote bags and satchels. From basic black to playful prints and kooky colour combinations, there’s a style of circular bag to suit all tastes. If you like to think outside of the square, so to speak, then check out these cool circular bags.

## 1. Whipstitch Shoulder Bag

![Whipstitch Shoulder Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x9/ix/wjdyulx854e45831c2ee1725192646_600x527.jpg)

Accessory trends sometimes have a short lifespan but you can hardly go wrong with basic black bags, right? This cute as a button circular bag is a versatile number which you could style with either the most basic or most flamboyant outfits. The whipstitch details and large tassel add that little something extra to the design while an adjustable shoulder strap is purely functional. At over $1000, this bag by Wendy Nichol is definitely in the ‘way too expensive’ basket but if you keep your eyes peeled you’re sure to find something similar on the high street.

## 2. Fringed Circle Bag

![Fringed Circle Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rc/rs/oba050ml54e4582e86d4b805318374_600x356.jpg)

Is navy the new black? We’re not sure, but we do know that navy is a classic colour. This navy circular shoulder bag features fringed detailing around the edges as well as gold tone hardware and a chain link shoulder strap. Team it with a crisp white collared shirts, cropped cigarette trousers, and patent shoes for an elegantly understated look. You can nab yourself one of these bags from 6pm.com for under $50.

## 3. Round Crossbody Bag

![Round Crossbody Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xi/tf/cojnqpib54e458316d744747375246_600x530.jpg)

If you like your accessories to really pop then check out this bright red circular bag by JJ Winters. It has a slightly slouchy feel to it and is complemented by a gold-tone chain link strap. It’s perfect for adding a splash of colour a basic or neutral outfit.Try teaming it with a striped shirt, full skirt, and ballet flats for a retro-inspired look.

## 4. Round Shoulder Bag

![Round Shoulder Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pt/ke/lcr0xdbx54e4582db11f0285286877_600x766.jpg)

Alexander Wang knows his trends and this military-inspired round bag is sure to be a hit with fashionistas. It’s made from a shiny black leather and has surplus style pockets to the front. Keep the military-theme going by teaming this sleek bag with a khaki shirt dress and ankle boots.

## 5. Logo Canvas Shoulder Bag

![Logo Canvas Shoulder Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hj/d6/e29wcmlz54e458308fe28711752400_600x593.jpg)

Kenzo is another cult brand and this blue and white number is their take on the circular bag trend. This circular shoulder bag comes emblazoned with their logo and features gold-tone hardware and an adjustable shoulder strap. If you’re obsessed with on-trend and cult brands then this bag would make a worthy addition to your wardrobe.

## 6. Phone Dial Clutch

![Phone Dial Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oa/mc/ol011apo54e4583167686292478904_600x393.jpg)

Would you like a side of kitsch with your circular bag? This faux leather clutch by Betsey Johnson features a cute number dial detail to the front. It also has a removable chain strap so you can either wear it across the body or use it as a clutch. Team it with gingham prints during the day for a playful look or dress it up at night with some tailored evening-wear.

## 7. Glitter Perspex Bag

![Glitter Perspex Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kj/b4/bwrne4pw54e9a0c6ce862535810991_600x221.jpg)

This glitter perspex bag by Edie Parker is perfect for a glam night out. It has a glittery gold perspex body with a clasp closure and chain link strap. It would look great worn with a little black or white party dress and some strappy heels. At over $1000, however, this bag is definitely the stuff of dreams.

Sure, they might not be able to hold much, but these circular bags are bound to add a different dimension to your outfits this season. Are you a fan of the circular bag trend? Which of these bags do you like best?
