# 7 Ravishing Ruffle Detail Handbags from Ruche ...

I have a thing for handbags… what girl doesn’t? I especially love handbags that have a pretty detail, something that sets them apart from other bags, and those details, of course, include lush ruffles. I’ve recently found a whole lot of them on one of my favorite fashion sites, Ruche, and I can’t wait to share them with you! Here are 7 ravishing ruffle detail handbags from Ruche!

## 1. Blissful Swirls Black Clutch

![Blissful Swirls Black Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v6/fm/tf74xypu54c8eb2e8fe1c379193410_450x193.jpg)

**Price:** $34.99 at [shopruche.com](http://www.shopruche.com/maintenance.html)
This sweet little clutch is the perfect accompaniment to your favorite little black dress, and comes with a detachable chain. It’s roomy enough to hold all of your goodies for a girl’s night out, date night, or for a special party. It’s covered in swirls of gorgeously pleated ruffles, and even has a second interior pocket to keep your lip gloss and ID at your fingertips.

## 2. Cappuccino Carnation Clutch

![Cappuccino Carnation Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s6/re/esifzyv954c8eb2fa5b77384050838_450x217.jpg)

**Price:** $30.99 at [shopruche.com](http://www.shopruche.com/maintenance.html)
This pretty little wristlet is small enough to slip into your messenger back or work bag, but big enough to carry all of the little things you want to access with speed and ease. The front features a dainty flower with ruffles around the edges, and is a lovely, latte brown.

## 3. Waves in the Field Green Ruffle Purse

![Waves in the Field Green Ruffle Purse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/np/sj/jgnhw1et54c8eb30a560c781104135_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $50.99 at [shopruche.com](http://www.shopruche.com/maintenance.html)
This bag is the color of spring, fresh green grass and budding leaves. I love the waves of ripples and the contrasting brown piping… I’d carry this bag with an earth-tone outfit, like high-waisted khaki trousers and a plain white blouse with a wood bead necklace and bracelet.

## 4. Annabell Blossom Handbag

![Annabell Blossom Handbag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c5/il/bfdxrzyd54c8eb31af25b275772269_450x386.jpg)

**Price:** $84.99 at [shopruche.com](http://www.shopruche.com/maintenance.html)
Ah, another lush ruffled handbag! Made by Melie Bianco, it features a sweet ruffled rosette on the front and a pretty polka-dotted liner. There’s an adjustable, detachable shoulder strap, or you can use the top handles. Either way, you’ll be carrying style!

## 5. All in One Silhouette Petals Purse

![All in One Silhouette Petals Purse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v1/o8/nxiah3lx54c8eb32a586d667406079_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $35.99 at [shopruche.com](http://www.shopruche.com/maintenance.html)
Choose from black or grey, a cross-body or shoulder strap or a clutch, this bag is the ultimate in versatility! It features a pretty ruffled flower on the front and is made of faux leather. I’d wear this with a menswear inspired outfit and pretty peep-toe pumps.

## 6. Silhouette Flowers Purse

![Silhouette Flowers Purse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qh/q4/f6c6g9tr54c8eb33b8354050150582_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $54.99 at [shopruche.com](http://www.shopruche.com/maintenance.html)
Looking for a slightly larger bag, one that’s big enough to function as an office or school carry-all, but still has oodles of style? This is your bag! It’s available in pale grey or marigold, with contrasting piping and interior pockets to keep all of your goodies organized.

## 7. Ruffles and Ripples Ivory Purse

![Ruffles and Ripples Ivory Purse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bb/zi/f0b1ge4d54c8eb34d732e935064771_450x389.jpg)

**Price:** $59.99 at [shopruche.com](http://www.shopruche.com/maintenance.html)
Slouchy and scrunchy and lusciously ruffled, this soft white bag has something these other bags don’t - a unique drawstring closure at the top. The two ruffle details are also unique, and wouldn’t this bag look pretty with a soft grey belted trench?

Those are just a few of the gorgeous ruffled bags available at the Ruche website, and there are so many more of them in all of my other favorite shops! I especially love the big green one, and the last white one. So pretty! Which of these ruffled bags do you like best, and what would you wear it with?
