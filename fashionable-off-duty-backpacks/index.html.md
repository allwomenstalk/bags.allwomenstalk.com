# 7 Fashionable off\-Duty Backpacks ...

**Fashionable backpacks**, believe it or not, do exist. Once thought of as an un-trendy fashion item strictly for the use of school kids, the backpack is making a fashion comeback. What started off as a bit of a ‘so ugly it’s cool’ trend, there’s now a range of quite stylish backpacks on the market. They might not be to everyone’s tastes, but a fashionable backpack is a functional and on-trend item. From fashionable designer backpacks to more affordable options, take a look at a few fashionable backpacks below.

## 1. Topshop Taupe Heart Trim Backpack

![Topshop Taupe Heart Trim Backpack](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/03/1_topshop-taupe-heart-trim-backpack_400x381.jpg)

**Price:** $60.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/XPQDcX)
Most backpacks tend to have a sporty vibe about them, but this Topshop backpack is quite refined. It features vintage-inspired quilting details and chain-trim shoulder straps. It also has a fold-over flap with a sweet metal heart trim. This fashionable backpack would look great teamed with a Peter Pan collared dress and Mary Jane style shoes.

## 2. Modcloth Canvas Backpack

![Modcloth Canvas Backpack](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/03/2_modcloth-canvas-backpack_342x336.jpg)

**Price:** $31.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you’re keen on working this season’s sporty chic trend, then give this canvas backpack a go. It’s made from 100% recycled cotton and comes in a great pale orange colour. The fuss-free style of the backpack is what I find most appealing. Wear it on the weekends with an outfit of a striped tee, denim jeans, and lace-up shoes.

## 3. Topshop Leather Backpack

![Topshop Leather Backpack](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/03/3_topshop-leather-backpack_400x441.jpg)

**Price:** $80.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/qkoF2E)
When looking for **fashionable backpacks, stick to** simpler styles that won’t date. Black leather is a classic and this particular backpack has a fairly minimalist design. It has a clip lock fastening, front zip pocket, and slim shoulder straps. This backpack could be quite versatile and worn with a range of outfits.

## 4. The Row Leather Backpack

![The Row Leather Backpack](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/03/4_the-row-leather-backpack_400x529.jpg)

**Price:** $3900.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/1nAkg8)
Let’s ignore the price tag for a minute. This bag definitely takes the cake for **fashionable designer backpacks**. Despite only debuting last year, this backpack by The Row has already become a cult fashion item. The minimalist design is elegant and sophisticated, which are two words I bet you thought you’d never see being used to describe a backpack.

## 5. Alexander Wang Marti Leather Backpack

![Alexander Wang Marti Leather Backpack](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/03/5_alexander-wang-marti-leather-backpack_400x529.jpg)

**Price:** $850.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/hHGcGh)
Alexander Wang has mastered the art of off-duty cool. This **fashionable backpack has a slouchy** design and comes in a nude leather. It features an array of zip detailing as well as five external pockets. The great thing about this backpack is that it comes with a detachable cross-body strap so can be worn as a normal handbag if desired.

## 6. ASOS Scallop Edge Backpack

![ASOS Scallop Edge Backpack](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/03/6_asos-scallop-edge-backpack_400x410.jpg)

**Price:** $62.67 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/FwcC7C).
For something completely different, take a look at this quirky briefcase style backpack. The design is a bit vintage inspired and features feminine detailing such as scalloped edges. Complete the look by teaming this backpack with your favourite vintage inspired dress and a pair of brogue shoes.

## 7. Topshop Unique Leather Rucksack

![Topshop Unique Leather Rucksack](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/03/7_topshop-unique-leather-rucksack_400x378.jpg)

**Price:** $360.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/im06rx)
I’m guessing that this fashionable backpack was somewhat inspired by The Row backpacks. It has a clean, rectangular silhouette and features gold rope straps. It has a single internal compartment and magnetic fastening top. The neutral colour of this backpack would make it quite easy to style.

From super minimalist designs to the humble canvas backpack, there are a range of styles to choose from when it comes to fashionable backpacks. When looking for a fashionable backpack, stick to more minimal designs that won’t date. What do you think of the backpack? Fashionable or not?
