# 7 Funky Fringed Bags ...

While the head to toe boho trend seems to be well in the past, boho accents are still a popular choice. Items such as fringed bags are an ideal way of incorporating a boho or luxe hippie twist to an outfit. Most come in a slouchy, over the shoulder design. Below are just a few funky fringed bags to consider.

## 1. Meli Melo Bon Bon Fringed Bag

![Meli Melo Bon Bon Fringed Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b9/rq/mt13th7d54c8fc376efa9520137713_300x450.jpg)

**Price:** $430.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/r1QFYk)
This bright red leather bag is a modern interpretation of the bohemian trend. It has a circular body, chain shoulder strap, and fringed trim. It also has a zip fastening at the top and a satin lined interior. This would look great paired with a summery white dress, or with an outfit of indigo blue jeans and nude blouse.

## 2. Topshop Red Suede Fringed Eyelet Bag

![Topshop Red Suede Fringed Eyelet Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ar/ns/dgxczrly54c8fc384fbf1006906075_300x448.jpg)

**Price:** $60.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/bb8j5h)
Funky fringed bags often give off a casual, relaxed vibe. This red suede bag would make an excellent choice. The fold over flap design is simple and streamlined. The long fringe detailing definitely adds interest. This bag also has an interior zip pocket for all those important bits and pieces.

## 3. Topshop Macramé Fringed Cross Body Bag

![Topshop Macramé Fringed Cross Body Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xk/bg/p3e4mb7v54c8fc3945ba6689579552_300x447.jpg)

**Price:** $60.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/vEYn9l)
Macramé details are enjoying renewed popularity in light of the 70s dressing trend. This macramé cross body bag has a zip top fastening and long fringing along the bottom. It also has an interior magnetic button closure. If you want to channel your inner 70s hippie, try slinging this bag over an outfit of bleached flared jeans and flowing blouse.

## 4. Modcloth Safe Travels Bag

![Modcloth Safe Travels Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uc/cf/w1wv2p1a54c8fc3a52ccf416321820_300x390.jpg)

**Price:** $249.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This bag seems to have a lot going on. This large fringed bag has a zip top fastening, exterior pocket, and two interior pockets. It also comes with two choices of shoulder straps – a chain link strap and an adjustable leather shoulder strap. It comes in a neutral grey colour which could accompany a variety of different outfits.

## 5. Topshop Black Leather Lazer Cut Fringed Bag

![Topshop Black Leather Lazer Cut Fringed Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hq/ul/em8dar2854c8fc3b58a85731456076_300x399.jpg)

**Price:** $135.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/fXePNr)
Funky fringed bags may not be your first pick for an everyday bag, but this black one could certainly do the job. It comes in a simple envelope style design and features laser cut detailing along the front. It also has a cross body strap and fringed flap. This bag would add a boho twist to any outfit. I could picture it with a cream cable knit sweater and burgundy cuffed shorts.

## 6. Jerome Dreyfuss Igor Mini Messenger Bag

![Jerome Dreyfuss Igor Mini Messenger Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/og/og/xpynqvnn54c8fc3c7a0ee138073976_300x387.jpg)

**Price:** $848.00 at [matchesfashion.com](http://www.matchesfashion.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This gold leather bag is a luxe take on the trend. It has a taupe leather base and metallic gold finish. The fringing is detachable, so if you don’t feel like partaking in the funky fringed bags trend, you can simply remove them. This bag would look great worn during the day or at night.

## 7. Paul & Joe Perlee Beaded Handmade Cross Body Bag

![Paul & Joe Perlee Beaded Handmade Cross Body Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/aj/wa/ynxr8zhm54c8fc3d7b533985885403_300x337.jpg)

**Price:** $315.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/9EFdvE)
This beaded bag is a quirky little number. It has a beaded handmade body and features tassel fringing at the sides. It also has a braided white shoulder strap. This funky little bag would definitely add a playful touch to an outfit. It’s worth noting that since these bags are handmade, each will differ slightly in appearance.
