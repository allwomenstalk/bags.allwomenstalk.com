# Louis Vuitton Neverfull GM Bag

**What’s the bag like:** Timeless yet resolutely modern, the Neverfull is ideal both for traveling and in town. Its large format can be adjusted using its two side straps, which makes it very practical.

**What’s it made of:**
-Monogram canvas, striped textile lining and natural cowhide trimmings
- Golden brass pieces
- Hook closure
- Zippered inside pocket with a D-ring (for attaching a purse or key holder)
- Side straps for changing the capacity of the bag
- Carried on the shoulder
- Natural cowhide straps
- Inside patch with the Louis Vuitton signature echoing the very first travel items of the House

**Price:** $750.00

**Size:**

## 15. 5" X 12.8" X 7.9"

**My Opinion:** I am always looking for a stylish bag that is big enough to carry everything I need to take with me, and this one is perfect. There is a very retro feel to the inside styling, which reminds me of the glamour of the 50's and beach holidays! The outside pattern is gorgeous, and the bag feels and looks high quality. This is a definite hit bag, wherever you are going. The bag also comes in PM and MM styles, so you really can adjust your bag to meet your needs! A divine bag range.

**My Rating:** Big and Beautiful!
