# 7 Modern Clutches for Any Occasion That Are Versatile and Stunning ...

Even though I spend my days with my little boy, I still carry around clutches for any occasion! My favorite one is a bright yellow leather clutch that can double as an iPad or tablet case. I got it from GAP (one of my favorite stores!). It's so versatile, like many other clutches, that it makes for the perfect accessory! So here you go, here are 7 clutches for any occasion that will make you want to add one to your collection.

## 1. Mix No. 6 Color Block Envelope Clutch

![Mix No. 6 Color Block Envelope Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/11044949_5596_599x327.jpg)

Using my own yellow clutch as inspiration, this chic envelope clutch is ideal for that pop of color and complement to any outfit! You can pair it with jeans and a blouse or a dress and heels with simple matching jewelry! I love that it's structured as well. DSW has a great selection of handbags and clutches for any occasion, but are mainly known for their fabulous and great-priced footwear! This bag is also only $29.95!

## 2. Steve Madden Bcantina Flap Clutch

![Steve Madden Bcantina Flap Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/11044949_7168_477x238.jpg)

Another DSW love, this faux-leather, two toned clutch literally goes with any outfit! Neutral tones are always great to have in accessories because then you don't have to worry about switching out your bags from day to night. And this Steve Madden clutch is decent in size- enough to carry all your essentials! It's stylish and affordable too, at $39.95.

## 3. Green Flip Lock Clutch by Topshop

![Green Flip Lock Clutch by Topshop](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/11044947_1511_459x266.jpg)

Isn't this mint green leather clutch a beauty? It doubles as a cross body bag and a clutch to suit your preferences. I love the color and style of this gorgeous bag! I would definitely recommend pairing it with colors that complement and don't overwhelm, so that it stands out as a focal piece. Try neutral clothing with pastel jewelry or even pops of red for a fresh flare! This bag is currently on sale at Nordstrom for $50.92.

## 4. Maison Scotch Quilted Soft Leather Clutch Bag

![Maison Scotch Quilted Soft Leather Clutch Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/12091950_4180_255x167.jpg)

I have been dying for the perfect red leather clutch! This one in particular is so bold and full of texture, but is on the more expensive side at $227.74. If it's worth the investment, then definitely make the plunge and purchase this fabulous eye candy. I actually have an orange-red cross body bag and surprisingly it goes with so many outfits! I could see this ASOS clutch working the same way in its versatility.

## 5. Fashion Fan Club Clutch by Modcloth

![Fashion Fan Club Clutch by Modcloth](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/11044948_7643_299x207.jpg)

Don't you just love an accessory piece that's a conversion starter? This little fan clutch is just that kind of accessory! It's adorable and unique, sure to turn some heads and receive many compliments. I could see it working well for a cocktail party and a LBD! This beauty can be purchased for $44.99.

## 6. ZAC Zac Posen Posen Clutch

![ZAC Zac Posen Posen Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/11044948_0706_479x238.jpg)

I actually had to re-read the name of this clutch to be sure I got it correct! This is the priciest bag on the list at $395.00, and it just screams personality and modern brilliance. The colors are perfect for fall and the design is unique! I would definitely be thrilled to carry this beauty around because it would work so well with everyday life!

## 7. Hat Attack Raffia Crochet Clutch with Braid Trim

![Hat Attack Raffia Crochet Clutch with Braid Trim](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/11044949_2792_479x311.jpg)

This fun straw clutch has some serious flair! I love the neural shade which pairs with just about anything, but most of all I am in love with the texture it has! The leather tassel works so well with it also, and gives it more contrast. This clutch would look fab with some brown leather boots! The price of this one is $88.00.

As you can see, there is a wide variety of styles and price points here! Tell me, which one was your favorite from this list? Where do you love to shop for cute bags and accessories?
