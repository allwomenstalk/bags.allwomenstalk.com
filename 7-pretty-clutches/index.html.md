# 7 Pretty Clutches ...

I love clutch handbags, especially for formal occasions. They look great with everything from your best sequined LBD to skinny jeans and a pretty tunic to leggings with tall boots. I’m always on the lookout for a new clutch, something with character, charm, and style, and I’ve found a bunch of them on my favorite fashion site, ModCloth! Here are 7 pretty clutches…

## 1. Hanging out with My Ecru Clutch

![Hanging out with My Ecru Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ay/yy/ecfkxa6n54c8eabdc8f80983587812_350x208.jpg)

**Price:** $49.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This is a fabulous casual clutch, perfect for a day of shopping and lunch with the girls. It’s roomy enough for the essentials - wallet, keys, lip gloss, and sunglasses - with a detachable shoulder or cross-body strap. The neutral color is ideal for pairing with just about anything, including basic black.

## 2. Purple Plumage Clutch

![Purple Plumage Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wx/b5/c4k228yr54c8eabe8c632046436968_350x222.jpg)

**Price:** $79.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
It’s hard to say which detail I like more, the vintage-inspired feathery pattern, or the gorgeous gold hardware. I suppose it doesn’t matter, since this clutch comes with both. Pair with an LBD, colorful heels, and gold jewelry for a night on the town, or with a pretty tunic, patterned leggings, and ankle booties with gold buckles.

## 3. Glitter with Glamour Clutch

![Glitter with Glamour Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jq/km/kex825p754c8eabf72a44286154006_350x240.jpg)

**Price:** $41.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If ever there was a clutch designed for the night life, this is it. It’s made of gorgeous pinky gold, with a ruffled edge and removable wrist strap. Wear with anything in your closet, from punk sass to high class. Gorgeous!

## 4. Cue the Montage Bag

![Cue the Montage Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i6/bn/rgi8sjy254c8eac052417454749451_350x192.jpg)

**Price:** $68.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Eek! So cute! This charming clutch by shoe mavens Irregular Choice is so rich in details, you’ll be distracted for hours just trying to choose your favorite. The bright blue fabric? The perky cherry red gingham trim? Or the polka-dot bow? Pair with skinny jeans and tall boots with a long-sleeved tee and vest or cardi… and enjoy all of the details!

## 5. Kinda Busy Clutch

![Kinda Busy Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/es/ju/tfs69qd254c8eac134e56858436559_350x194.jpg)

**Price:** $41.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Metallics are hot again this season, and a peek at this clutch will tell you why. The color is sharp and sassy, the woven pattern eye-catching. Pair with an LBD and silvery stilettos, or with studded leggings and a soft tunic. Make sure your jewelry and accents match the silvery chain, though, and you’re ready for a night out!

## 6. Chitty Chitty Bag Bag

![Chitty Chitty Bag Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kw/li/m41autgx54c8eac21a92b945923866_350x254.jpg)

**Price:** $64.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I didn’t know a clutch could be slouchy, so casual! It’s made of salmon-pink faux leather with a tasstle and accents in shiny (though phony) raspberry snakeskin. The gold chain strap is detachable, for extra versatility, and the interior is charmingly polka-dotted. Don’t try to match this clutch to an outfit, just add it. Trust me!

## 7. Through the Post Clutch

![Through the Post Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ud/h6/hcf4j75m54c8eac2ede5e409834643_350x294.jpg)

**Price:** $29.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Boast of your world travels, or just pretend, with this sweet faux-leather clutch. Adore the postage-stamp details and the fitting message, “Make up for lost time.” Add this bag to any office-wear outfit or to a pretty summer dress.

Those are my new must-have clutches, all from my favorite online shop, and I can’t wait for my order to arrive! I had to have the postcard clutch, and I can’t decide on the others yet. Which of these pretty clutches do you like best, and why? How would you wear it?
