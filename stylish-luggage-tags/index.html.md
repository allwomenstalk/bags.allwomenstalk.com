# 7 Stylish Luggage Tags ...

When travelling, a luggage tag is an easy way of identifying your luggage. Choose one in a design that reflects your personality and tastes. From high end designers to high street chain stores, there’s a luggage tag to suit all tastes and budgets. Take a look at a few stylish luggage tags below.

## 1. Jimmy Choo Teddy Leather and Calf Hair Luggage Tag

![Jimmy Choo Teddy Leather and Calf Hair Luggage Tag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f8/qn/aqrlpr5o54c92be47a452879805766_296x299.jpg)

**Price:** $250.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/sSNKhv)
The Jimmy Choo brand is synonymous with style. This luggage tag features dark brown leather, animal-print calf hair, and a press-stud fastening flap at the plastic window. It has a designer stamped gold rivet and buckle-fastening strap. At this price tag, however, I’d definitely be keeping this luggage tag close at all times during travelling.

## 2. Topshop Leather Leopard Print Luggage Tag

![Topshop Leather Leopard Print Luggage Tag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vt/rm/ws4lxcms54c92be5403eb413286549_300x119.jpg)

**Price:** $20.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/6rw6Pf)
If you are after affordable and stylish luggage tags then try a chain store like Topshop to get your fix. This leopard print luggage tag would make a great alternative to the previously mention Jimmy Choo luggage tag. It has an all over leopard print design, buckle fastening, and clear plastic window. This would look especially striking set against a jet black suitcase.

## 3. Want Les Essentials De La Vie Changi Luggage Tag

_ **Price:** $49.00 at barneys.com_
This luggage tag comes in a chic ecru and black colour combination. It is made from leather and features a fabric lined interior. It has a buckle fastening, snap closure, and I.D. holder. Its minimalist, sleek design makes it an extremely stylish choice.

## 4. Cath Kidston Luggage Tag

![Cath Kidston Luggage Tag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vx/f8/p0u4nr1w54c92be6cea0a891113928_300x146.jpg)

**Price:** $9.23 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/E51i27)
There are no precise rules as to what constitutes stylish luggage tags. This Cath Kidston luggage tag features an all over floral print with contrasting trims. Some people may find it a bit too cutesy and kitsch while others will just adore it. As for functionality, this luggage tag has a buckle fastening and transparent cover and card. Add a touch of whimsy to your luggage with this adorable tag.

## 5. Jimmy Choo Teddy Glitter and Leather Luggage Tag

![Jimmy Choo Teddy Glitter and Leather Luggage Tag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ux/g2/p18vrj8j54c92be795902748544180_215x299.jpg)

**Price:** $165.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/Uj2yHT)
This leather luggage tag has a glitzy glitter finish. It is made from champagne pebbled leather and has a silver glitter finish. It has a press-stud fastening, clear plastic window, and buckle fastening strap. This leather luggage tag would be a chic way to add some glamour to your luggage set.

## 6. Diane by Diane Von Furstenberg Luggage Tag

![Diane by Diane Von Furstenberg Luggage Tag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/un/x0/lorgrlot54c92be85ce6b450395811_131x300.jpg)

**Price:** $50.00 at [matchesfashion.com](http://www.matchesfashion.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This luggage tag features a stylish black and white geometric print. The overall design is very retro in appearance. This rectangular luggage tag features a buckle fastening strap and I.D. holder. The black and white print would look great against brightly coloured luggage.

## 7. Smythson Luggage Tag

_ **Price:** $105.00 at barneys.com_
This Smythson luggage tag is timeless. The most stylish luggage tags often are. This one is made from crocodile stamped leather and has gold hardware and stamping. It features ‘home’ and ‘destination’ pockets, and also has an adjustable buckle strap. This luggage tag will be sure to see you through plenty of travelling.

Luggage tags are not only functional, but can be quite personal too. Let your individuality shine through in a luggage tag that reflects your personality. Which of these stylish luggage tags do you like best?
