# Best and Cutest  Backpacks for College Girls ...

You've got to have a bag for college, which is why you need expert guidance on **how to choose a backpack for college.**

This post will tell you everything you need to know about choosing the **best backpacks for college**, no matter what your needs are.

Going to college often means having a laptop in your hand. You'll also need to carry books, paper and many other items.

No more being stuck without enough space for your essentials.

This handy **college** **backpack buying guide** will help you find your perfect backpack for college.

## 1. Consider the Size

One of the first things to think about when learning **how to choose a backpack for college** is size. Before you go to the store and buy a random laptop bag, find out how big your laptop is. The typical laptop is anywhere from 11 to 17 inches. However, you might find it hard to locate a backpack that accommodates a larger laptop. Be sure you know how roomy you need the bag to be so you get the right one the first time.

## 2. Consider the Materials

Material is another important factor that you have to look at when finding the best college backpacks. You need something that is durable because you're going to be using your backpack every single day. If you're carrying a laptop or other device, look for something that provides some cushioning for protection. Look for sturdy stitching and wide straps, which offer additional support so you backpack stays in good shape for many years.

## 3. Consider Your Own Comfort

The best college backpack is one that allows you to carry everything you need, but without putting any strain on your back. Test the bags before you buy and be sure you can adjust the straps for a perfect fit.

## 4. Consider the Design

The best college backpack is one that complements your fashion sense. If you are cheerful person, there are plenty of colorful backpacks that you can buy. Likewise, if you like something neutral, there are tons of choices. When you love your backpack, you're more likely to use it and that's one way to be successful in college.
