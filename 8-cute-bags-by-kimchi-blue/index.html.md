# 8 Cute Bags by Kimchi Blue ...

Aside from my admitted love affair with Kimchi Blue shoes, I must also come clean about my semi-addiction to their adorable bags, as well. They’re so stylish, so unique, it’s hard not to love them, covet them, and ultimately buy a few. Here are the ones that top my “I want” list right now… here are 8 cute bags by Kimchi Blue.

## 1. Kimchi Blue Velvet Bow Clutch

![Kimchi Blue Velvet Bow Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oo/je/g1l9jyer54c8ecbdac88a537232500_499x308.jpg)

**Price:** $9.99 at [urbanoutfitters.com](http://www.urbanoutfitters.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
We all need a little clutch, something we can carry with our best LBD, or even just with skinny jeans and a sequined tunic for a night out with the girls. This one’s perfect, made of soft crushed velvet, with a skinny detachable shoulder strap and two neutral colors to choose from, mauve or black. And the price simply cannot be beat!

## 2. Kimchi Blue Front Pocket Lady Satchel

![Kimchi Blue Front Pocket Lady Satchel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hq/ap/jm2gv3oz54c8ecbe9fb70669455950_499x381.jpg)

**Price:** $48.00 at [urbanoutfitters.com](http://www.urbanoutfitters.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This faux leather satchel is roomy enough to hold all of your work-day or school-day necessities, with style to boot. Its pebbled texture is soft and weathered-looking, and it comes in three must-have colors (brown, grey, and black).

## 3. Kimchi Blue Straw Doctor Bag

![Kimchi Blue Straw Doctor Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ds/v6/ob84vh2r54c8ecbfcc49d116204051_499x497.jpg)

**Price:** $58.00 at [urbanoutfitters.com](http://www.urbanoutfitters.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Carry it by the padded handles, or wear it cross-body with the detachable, adjustable strap - either way this structured straw bag will look equally gorgeous. I love the contrast of the faux leather details, and the choice between tan or black… wouldn’t this look perfect with strappy wedges and cropped cigarette pants?

## 4. Kimchi Blue Small Bow Crossbody Bag

![Kimchi Blue Small Bow Crossbody Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vh/n8/zage95ev54c8ecc0e32d7410725749_499x321.jpg)

**Price:** $38.00 at [urbanoutfitters.com](http://www.urbanoutfitters.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I love the convenience and look of cross-body bags, especially this one, available in bright red, basic black, or chocolate brown. All three are made of the same pebbled faux leather, and feature an adjustable shoulder strap, stud detailing, and sweet bow on the front flap.

## 5. Kimchi Blue Printed Weekender Luggage

![Kimchi Blue Printed Weekender Luggage](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/if/s0/atzgwxpa54c8ecc20400a129267243_498x354.jpg)

**Price:** $78.00 at [urbanoutfitters.com](http://www.urbanoutfitters.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Stash all the things you’ll need for a weekend away in this pretty floral-print weekender. It’s made of PVC-coated cotton with contrasting faux leather handles and a detachable shoulder strap.

## 6. Kimchi Blue Slouchy Bucket with Tassels Bag

![Kimchi Blue Slouchy Bucket with Tassels Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e6/f8/wzympmii54c8ecc33f19f688442163_499x609.jpg)

**Price:** $58.00 at [urbanoutfitters.com](http://www.urbanoutfitters.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This super-soft slouchy bag is ideal for adding to your favorite Boho outfit, especially the dove grey version (it comes in inky black, too). I love the tassel details, and the drawstring top. So pretty!

## 7. Kimchi Blue Suede Buckle Bag

![Kimchi Blue Suede Buckle Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h6/vz/wnivjpc454c8ecc426b83992919000_499x422.jpg)

**Price:** $68.00 at [urbanoutfitters.com](http://www.urbanoutfitters.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This bag is far more mature and chic that most in the Kimchi Blue line, but I love it! It’s ideal for work-day office use, and is made of soft, lush suede with a contrasting faux-leather flap and turn-lock closure. I love it in brown… why don’t they make it in black?

## 8. Kimchi Blue Barrel Bag

![Kimchi Blue Barrel Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ee/gx/soidjkez54c8ecc51fc64489255382_498x343.jpg)

**Price:** $78.00 at [urbanoutfitters.com](http://www.urbanoutfitters.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I don’t normally like barrel bags, because they look too hard and structured, but this one’s gorgeous! It’s made of pliable, soft woven leather and features a shiny brass turn-lock clasp. The tan is neutral enough to wear with anything, and I love how it looks with vintage plaid or floral prints.

These cute little bags are the reason my closet is full of everything Kimchi Blue… how can I resist? They’re relatively inexpensive - did you see the price on that sweet clutch? - and are so stylish! Which of these bags do you like best, and why? What would you wear it with? Do share!
