# 7 Starry Printed Pieces to Add to Your Wardrobes This Season ...

Take your cue from the recent 2014 designer runways and add some starry printed pieces to your wardrobe this season. It could be a casual printed top or a more sophisticated printed dress, but one thing’s for sure and that is that star prints are going to be enjoying renewed popularity over the next couple of seasons. Big bold prints are perfect for making a statement while smaller star prints are perfect for a subtler take on the trend. Keen to try out this trend? Then check out these starry printed pieces.

## 1. Star Print Top

![Star Print Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/06203113_0535_599x667.jpg)

Starry printed pieces don’t always have to be statement making. This relaxed star print top by MiH Jeans would make a casual addition to your wardrobe. It’s made from a soft cotton-jersey and features faded blue stars. Work a casual look in faded boyfriend jeans and sneakers or dress it up with a full skirt and heels. It is pretty expensive at around $100 but you can check out Gap and ASOS for similar tops at a fraction of the cost.

## 2. Star Print Dress

![Star Print Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/06203111_6260_600x900.jpg)

This black and white star print dress is a versatile wardrobe piece. Wear it alone with ballet flats and a structured handbag for a simple summery look, or layer it up with tights, boots, and a chic coat for a stylish transeasonal look. This Chinti and Parker dress is pretty pricey at $220 but you can always check out ASOS and Modcloth for similar star print dresses that are easier on the wallet.

## 3. Star Print Scarf

![Star Print Scarf](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/06203112_9129_406x497.jpg)

If you’re after an affordable way to commit to the star print trend this season, then stick to accessories. This star print scarf will cost you around $30 Mango and you can find plenty of similar, affordable styles at a range of stores this season. Pop it on over your favourite fall outfit to add a little something extra to your look.

## 4. Star Print Jeans

![Star Print Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/06203111_9120_336x596.jpg)

The rose gold star details on these jeans add a playful twist to your classic skinny jean. Wear these jeans with a chunky knit top, ankle boots, and floppy fedora for an on-trend fall look. While you might love the look of these jeans, you might not love the price. These jeans will set you back around $250 but be sure to check out ASOS and Forever 21 for more affordable styles.

## 5. Star Print Silk Shirt

Here, the classic white silk shirt has been given a modern twist in the form of a bold star print. This classic collared shirt by Equipment has a loose fit and would look great teamed with skinny black jeans and patent loafers for a menswear inspired look. Equipment shirts are notoriously pricey, though, and this one will set you back around $260.

## 6. Star Print Slippers

Add a whimsical touch to your outfit with some star print shoes. These smoking slippers are from JC Penney and are a steal at around $16. Wear them with a pair of chic cigarette pants and silky shirt for a stylish daytime look.

## 7. Star Print Backpack

![Star Print Backpack](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/06203113_5588_599x610.jpg)

If you like your prints a bit louder, then take a look at this star print backpack. It’s perfect for those who want to work a casual street look. The backpack has a classic design and features leather-look trims. It’ll set you back around $40 from ASOS and you can also a range of star print backpacks by Nasty Gal and Volcom.

Star prints are a great way to add a playful twist your outfits this season. What do you think of the star print trend? Which of these items are you most starry-eyed over?
