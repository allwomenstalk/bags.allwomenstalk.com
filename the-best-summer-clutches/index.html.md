# The 13 Best Summer Clutches ...

If you have to go out and about when the weather is warm, but can’t leave behind all your necessities, consider getting yourself a couple of summer clutches. They’re perfect for the bare minimum of stuff if you’re going to a show or a party. Summer clutches are also cute and fun to carry so you can look awesome at the same time.

## 1. Vince Camuto Horn Clutch

![Vince Camuto Horn Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/09042329_9689_336x252.jpg)

The Vince Camuto Horn Clutch is one of the summer clutches made from stylish woven leather that will pair well with anything in your wardrobe. This is one of the easiest to carry summer clutches because it has a small handle and a removable chain strap. You simply can’t go wrong!

**Price:** $105 at [neimanmarcus.com](http://www.neimanmarcus.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 2. Michael by Michael Kors Tilda Clutch

![Michael by Michael Kors Tilda Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/09042358_6247_600x322.jpg)

It’s shiny and silver, so how could you possibly go wrong? This clutch from Michael Kors also has a logo design, a shoulder strap and a convenient snap that make this awesome bag stylish and functional at the same time.

**Price:** $218 at [bloomingdales.com](http://www1.bloomingdales.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 3. Kayu Jen Straw Minaudiere

![Kayu Jen Straw Minaudiere](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/09042411_4184_350x294.jpg)

This Kayu Jen clutch is reminiscent of those cool straw bags from your childhood, only in a grown-up way. This one is a stand out choice because it comes in a bright pink color and has a cute closure. It’s also available in white, black and turquoise so you can pair it with virtually anything you own.

**Price:** $148 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 4. Clare Vivier Fold over Clutch

![Clare Vivier Fold over Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/09042436_0660_336x252.jpg)

This fold over clutch from Clare Vivier is perfect for parties because it’s roomy enough for the necessities, but it’s small enough to be discreet and cute too. Made from luxurious calfskin and featuring a cotton lining, this clutch meets all your needs.

**Price:** $156 at [neimanmarcus.com](http://www.neimanmarcus.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 5. Tory Burch Amanda Logo Clutch

![Tory Burch Amanda Logo Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/09042448_0057_350x204.jpg)

If logos are your favorite part about shopping for bags, this Tory Burch clutch fits the bill. It’s black with a gold logo and chain strap so you can carry it anytime, no matter what you’re wearing. It also comes in three other fun colors if you want to be a bit more daring.

**Price:** $350 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 6. Diane Von Furstenberg Carolina Lips Clutch Bag

![Diane Von Furstenberg Carolina Lips Clutch Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/10050540_7185_459x285.jpg)

The Carolina Lips Clutch Bag from Diane von Furstenberg is a fun mixture of whimsy with the lip shaped closure and beauty with the metallic body. This clutch folds over and has enough room to house most anything you must have with you.

**Price:** $241 at [bergdorfgoodman.com](http://www.bergdorfgoodman.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 7. Loeffler Randall “Lock” Leather Clutch

![Loeffler Randall “Lock” Leather Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/09042530_2979_350x338.jpg)

Two-toned leather meets a double interior compartment with this clutch from Loeffler Randall. The closure is a push lock one and is easy to open and close. We're showing the black one but the bright pink and red color options mean this clutch is sure to stand out anywhere you carry it.

**Price:** $295 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 8. Rebecca Minkoff Mini M.a.C

![Rebecca Minkoff Mini M.a.C](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/09042602_3672_350x304.jpg)

The Mini M.A.C. by Rebecca Minkoff is the perfect choice if you love a clutch, but need a bit more room. It looks like a clutch but is much roomier and has fun hardware. It’s also available in several colors so you can find just the right one for you.

**Price:** $195 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 9. BCBG Thea Clutch

![BCBG Thea Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/09042632_9686_336x252.jpg)

If you’re looking to make a statement, this clutch from BCBG is your go-to choice. It combines a fun geometric pattern with a removable strap for convenience and style at the same time.

**Price:** $148 at [neimanmarcus.com](http://www.neimanmarcus.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 10. Milly Gwen Clutch

![Milly Gwen Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/09042650_3048_335x176.jpg)

If rocker chic is your style, this Milly Gwen clutch is definitely for you. It’s a fantastic turquoise color with great studs that give it an edge you’ll love.

**Price:** $195 at [ru.shopbop.com](http://ru.shopbop.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 11. Asos Leather Beaded Tile Effect Clutch

![Asos Leather Beaded Tile Effect Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/09042232_0849_599x320.jpg)

If a unique clutch you won’t see anywhere else is your main goal, this one from Asos is your best bet. It features a lovely beaded outer shell with 100 percent leather construction. It’ll hold up and look great for years.

**Price:** $37 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/rjk813)

## 12. Sweet Pandora Clutch

![Sweet Pandora Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/09042720_9128_570x280.jpg)

If you’re looking for a splurge, this is it! It’s not cheap, but this beautiful turquoise clutch is hard on the outside, so you have protection for your stuff. The inside has a zippered closure and the outside has a wonderful closure. Start saving!

**Price:** $1,255 at [marissacollections.com](http://www.marissacollections.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 13. Loft Macrame Zip Pouch

![Loft Macrame Zip Pouch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/09042753_0470_484x363.jpg)

If all you need to carry is an ID and a little bit of cash, this tiny clutch from Loft is affordable and perfect. It’s beautiful outer design and leather wrist loop combine for the perfect accessory for any outfit.

**Price:** $10 at [loft.com](https://goo.gl/mxlJv3)
