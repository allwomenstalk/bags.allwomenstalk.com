# 8 Best Mulberry Bags ...

The Mulberry brand was established in 1971 in England. Since then, the brand has established itself as one of the most iconic British lifestyle brands. From timeless classics such as the Bayswater, to more modern ‘It-bags’ such as the Alexa, a variety of Mulberry bags have achieved iconic status. Take a look at some of the best Mulberry bags below.

## 1. Bayswater Leather Bag

![Bayswater Leather Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wt/cl/bhn5od7m54c8fce277ff9166823881_300x387.jpg)

**Price:** $950.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/v9tSVc)
The Mulberry Bayswater is timeless and iconic. It’s a medium to large sized carryall and features a flap front, two top handles, and concealed buckles at the sides. It also has a designer stamped padlock to the front. The Bayswater comes in a range of finishes from classic oak leather to electric blue ostrich leather. This bag is definitely an investment piece.

## 2. Oversized Alexa Bag

![Oversized Alexa Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gg/s5/ug9qbazz54c8fce359ac1079585246_300x261.jpg)

**Price:** $1250.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/f6xSnZ)
The Alexa bag first burst onto the scene during the Spring Summer 2010 season. It has since achieved ‘It-bag’ status. Named after the British fashion icon Alexa Chung, the bag is an elegant and modern reinvention of the classic Bayswater and Elkington bags. The oversized Alexa in oak leather is one of the most popular choices.

## 3. Piccadilly Leather Weekend Bag

_ **Price:** $1450.00 at lagarconne.com_
The Piccadilly is actually the largest of the Bayswater family. It features Mulberry’s iconic postman’s lock fastening to the front, and has a hidden padlock in the handle pouch. This large leather bag could be used as a weekend bag or as carry on luggage. It is designed for use by both men and women.

## 4. Edie Large Shoulder Bag

![Edie Large Shoulder Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fy/s9/vam0182v54c8fce50e6ed795729457_300x228.jpg)

**Price:** $1175.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
The Edie is reminiscent of many of the best Mulberry bags, but it comes in a sleeker and more modern design. It has a removable shoulder strap, top handle, and internal patch and pouch pockets. The Edie comes in a range of finishes from pebbled leather to metallic tweed. It also ranges in size from small to large.

## 5. Lily Shoulder Bag

![Lily Shoulder Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xz/pg/i17zisuq54c8fce5d63be491904150_300x379.jpg)

**Price:** $650.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
The Lily is a small shoulder bag that comes with an adjustable chain strap. It has a flap front that features the iconic Mulberry hardware. The chain strap can be doubled up and worn over the shoulder, or extended and worn across the body. The design is both youthful and elegant.

## 6. Tillie Leather Shoulder Bag

![Tillie Leather Shoulder Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dg/he/az3q934754c8fce6a357e343664605_300x246.jpg)

**Price:** $1175.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/G4hiJy)
One of the newest and best Mulberry bags to launch recently is, in my opinion, the Tillie. It is based on the classic satchel style, but with a more contemporary shape. It does not feature the classic postman’s lock but instead features a D-ring and Trompe l’oeil buckle. Like most Mulberry bags, it comes in a range of sizes and finishes.

## 7. Clipper Small Weekend Bag

![Clipper Small Weekend Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l7/p8/gf4nvy4s54c8fce772131792922780_300x247.jpg)

**Price:** $1450.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/UAm5Qv)
Even in a small, the Clipper travel bag is substantial in size. This bag has two top handles and a detachable shoulder strap. It features a hidden padlock and double ended zip closure. Much like the Piccadilly, this bag is well suited for weekend getaways and carry on luggage.

## 8. Sofia Tote

![Sofia Tote](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vw/d2/o4i1xyg454c8fce865954249204608_300x407.jpg)

**Price:** $750.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
The Sofia range is one of Mulberry’s newest. This hobo style tote is sure to become a fashion favourite. While previous Mulberry bags have a classic satchel design, this slouchy hobo is a bit more modern and directional. It comes in pebbled leather and features a drawstring closure and removable should strap. The Sofia has a certain model-off-duty vibe about it.

To have at least one of these bags in your repertoire would be quite special. Which of these do you think are the best Mulberry bags?
