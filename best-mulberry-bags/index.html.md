# 8 Best Mulberry Bags to Buy Right Now: The Ultimate Guide

The Mulberry brand was established in 1971 in England. Since then, the brand has firmly established itself as one of the most iconic British lifestyle brands. From timeless classics such as the Bayswater to more modern silhouettes, a variety of Mulberry leather bags have achieved true "It-bag" status. Whether you are browsing the PurseForum Mulberry threads for recommendations or looking for a women's Mulberry bag for everyday wear, finding the most popular Mulberry bag can feel overwhelming. Take a look at our curated list of the best Mulberry handbags below to help you decide on your next investment.

- Timeless classics like the Bayswater remain a staple in many collections.

- Modern reinventions offer fresh takes on iconic British designs.

- There are multiple options, from large travel bags to small evening styles.

## 1. Bayswater Leather Bag

![Mulberry Bayswater Leather Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wt/cl/bhn5od7m54c8fce277ff9166823881_300x387.jpg)

The Mulberry Bayswater is a timeless and [iconic Mulberry bag](#5). It is a medium to large sized carryall that features a flap front, two top handle bags, and concealed buckles at the sides. It also has a designer stamped padlock to the front. The Bayswater comes in a range of finishes from classic oak leather to electric blue ostrich leather. This piece is definitely an investment, making it arguably the best Mulberry bag to buy for long-term style. You can view the current Bayswater collection on the [official Mulberry website](https://www.mulberry.com/us/shop/bayswater).

## 2. Oversized Alexa Bag

![Mulberry Oversized Alexa Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gg/s5/ug9qbazz54c8fce359ac1079585246_300x261.jpg)

The Alexa bag first burst onto the scene during the Spring Summer 2010 season. It has since achieved sought-after "It-bag" status and remains a favorite [investment bag](https://bags.allwomenstalk.com/investment-bags-every-woman-should-own/) for many. Named after the British fashion icon Alexa Chung, the bag is an elegant and modern reinvention of the classic Bayswater and Elkington styles. The oversized Alexa in oak leather remains one of the most popular choices for those who love a relaxed, slouchy silhouette. It often serves as a fantastic alternative to the more structured [Bayswater carryall](#1).

## 3. Piccadilly Leather Weekend Bag

![Mulberry Piccadilly Leather Weekend Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uh/k1/mefv2dbg54c8fce432f6e955654310_300x299.jpg)

If you are searching for the largest Mulberry bag, the Piccadilly is actually the most spacious member of the Bayswater family. It features Mulberry’s signature postman’s lock fastening to the front, and it has a hidden padlock in the handle pouch. This large leather bag functions beautifully as a Mulberry travel bag for weekends away or as stylish carry-on luggage. It is thoughtfully designed for use by both men and women who appreciate luxury travel accessories.

| Feature | Details | Ideal Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Size | Largest in Bayswater family | Travel, weekend trips |
| Hardware | Signature postman's lock | Secure carry-on |
| Material | Classic leather | Durable travel companion |

## 4. Edie Large Shoulder Bag

![Mulberry Edie Large Shoulder Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fy/s9/vam0182v54c8fce50e6ed795729457_300x228.jpg)

The Edie is reminiscent of many classic Mulberry designs, but it comes in a sleeker and more modern shape. It has a removable shoulder strap, a top handle, and practical internal patch and pouch pockets. The Edie comes in a range of finishes from beautiful pebbled leather to textured metallic tweed. It also ranges in size from small to large, making it versatile enough for daily errands or days at the office.

## 5. Lily Shoulder Bag

![Mulberry Lily Shoulder Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xz/pg/i17zisuq54c8fce5d63be491904150_300x379.jpg)

The Lily is a charming small shoulder bag that comes with an adjustable woven chain strap. It has a classic flap front that features the iconic Mulberry hardware, similar to what you might find on a [larger weekend bag](#3). The chain strap can be doubled up and worn over the shoulder, or it can be extended and worn across the body for a hands-free option. The overall design is both youthful and elegantly refined, ideal for evenings out.

- Perfect for evening events or when you only need to carry essentials.

- The versatile chain strap allows for multiple styling options to suit your look.

## 6. Tillie Leather Shoulder Bag

![Mulberry Tillie Leather Shoulder Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dg/he/az3q934754c8fce6a357e343664605_300x246.jpg)

A fantastic option that has gained attention among Mulberry fans is the Tillie. It is based on the traditional satchel style, but it incorporates a more contemporary shape. It does not feature the familiar postman’s lock; instead, it showcases a sleek D-ring and a Trompe l’oeil buckle detail. Like most items in their lineup, it comes in a range of sizes and beautiful finishes to suit any wardrobe preference.

## 7. Clipper Small Weekend Bag

![Mulberry Clipper Small Weekend Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l7/p8/gf4nvy4s54c8fce772131792922780_300x247.jpg)

Even when designated as a small, the Clipper serves excellently as a Mulberry luggage piece and is quite substantial in size. This bag has two sturdy top handles and a convenient detachable shoulder strap. It features a hidden padlock and a secure double-ended zip closure. Much like the [Piccadilly design](#3), this bag is exceptionally well suited for spontaneous weekend getaways and functions perfectly as reliable carry-on luggage. You can check out more luxury luggage options at premium retailers like [Net-a-Porter](https://www.net-a-porter.com/).

## 8. Sofia Tote

![Mulberry Sofia Tote](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vw/d2/o4i1xyg454c8fce865954249204608_300x407.jpg)

The Sofia range is a wonderful addition to the brand's offerings. This hobo-style tote provides a relaxed silhouette that easily becomes a daily fashion favorite, much like other [casual everyday bags](https://bags.allwomenstalk.com/casual-everyday-bags/). While previous Mulberry classic bags often feature a more structured satchel design, this slouchy hobo is a bit more modern and fluid. It comes in rich pebbled leather and features a chic drawstring closure alongside a removable shoulder strap. The Sofia carries a distinct model-off-duty vibe that works effortlessly with casual wear.

Having at least one of these high-quality pieces in your personal collection would be quite special, which might explain why people often wonder, "Why is Mulberry so expensive?" The craftsmanship and timeless appeal certainly provide the answer. Which of these designs do you think is the best Mulberry bag for your style?
