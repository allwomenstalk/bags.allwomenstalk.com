Cross-body bags exude a street-smart kind of aesthetic. Larger, slouchy bags are an ideal way to top off a casual everyday outfit. Smaller cross-body bags look great paired with a dressy evening outfit. There are a range of styles and sizes available. Take a look at just a few versatile cross-body bags below.

1. Michael Michael Kors Hamilton Leather Corss-Body Bag

Price: $155.00 at net-a-porter.com
This black quilted bag is a timeless wardrobe piece. It has a quilted leather body and top zip fastening. The timeless design is given a contemporary twist in the form of the silver chain shoulder strap. Exercise a utilitarian style and sling this bag over an outfit of indigo jeans, white v-neck t-shirt, and black blazer.

2. Topshop Black Patent Lock Contrast Handle Bag

Price: $65.00 at us.topshop.com
This black patent bag has a stylish design. It features a front flap with a lock fastening, contrasting straps, and interior pouch and zip pockets. Like most cross-body bags, the shoulder strap is adjustable. You could wear this bag over the shoulder or simply by carrying it by the grab top handles.

3. Modcloth Case in Point Bag

Price: $69.99 at modcloth.com
This tan bag would make an excellent everyday handbag. It is of a medium size and could fit most of your daily essential items. It is made from faux leather and features decorative buckle details at the front. It has a zip top fastening, single interior compartment, detachable shoulder strap, and grab top handle. Pair this bag with a vintage-inspired outfit.

Inject a dash of colour into your outfit with one of these cross body bags. This petite bag has a framed design and comes in a summery coral colour. It has a gold-tone flat lock fastening, grab top handle, and adjustable shoulder strap. Wear this bag with a cropped cream top and A-line denim midi skirt for an effortless summer look.

Price: $36.93 at asos.com
This black and white bag has a ladylike, structured frame. It has a zip top fastening, outer flap pocket with gold-tone clasp, and a long chain and leather-effect shoulder strap. The overall design is elegant and sleek. Keep it monochrome and pair this bag with a black and white outfit.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are cross-body bags?
Cross-body bags are handbags designed with a long strap to be worn across the body for hands-free convenience.
Why are cross-body bags popular among women?
They offer comfort, security, and style, making them perfect for busy, on-the-go lifestyles.
Are cross-body bags suitable for formal occasions?
Yes, sleek and minimalist cross-body bags can complement formal outfits beautifully.
How much can a typical cross-body bag hold?
Most can comfortably carry essentials like a phone, wallet, keys, and a few small personal items.
What materials are cross-body bags made from?
They come in various materials, including leather, canvas, nylon, and synthetic fabrics.
Can cross-body bags fit larger items like tablets?
Some larger styles can fit tablets, but most are designed for smaller everyday essentials.
How do I choose the right size cross-body bag?
Consider what you need to carry daily and choose a size that fits those items without being bulky.
Are cross-body bags safe for travel?
Yes, worn close to the body, they reduce the risk of theft and keep valuables secure.
Can cross-body bags be worn on both shoulders?
Absolutely! The adjustable strap allows you to comfortably switch sides.
How do I care for my cross-body bag?
Follow care instructions based on the material, such as wiping leather with a damp cloth or spot cleaning canvas.
