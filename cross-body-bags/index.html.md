# 8 Cross\-Body Bags ...

Cross-body bags exude a street-smart kind of aesthetic. Larger, slouchy bags are an ideal way to top off a casual everyday outfit. Smaller cross-body bags look great paired with a dressy evening outfit. There are a range of styles and sizes available. Take a look at just a few versatile cross-body bags below.

This black quilted bag is a timeless wardrobe piece. It has a quilted leather body and top zip fastening. Exercise a utilitarian style and sling this bag over an outfit of indigo jeans, white v-neck t-shirt, and black blazer.
