# 15 Trendy Summer Totes ...

Though I can’t live without the summer sun and surf, a good tote bag is my number one season essential. From sunnies to a stash of magazines to read on the beach, summer totes can hold just about everything you need to enjoy the warm weather. Here are 15 totally trendy totes you’ll want to keep by your side (unlike that spring fling). I’ve got an option for just about every gal!
