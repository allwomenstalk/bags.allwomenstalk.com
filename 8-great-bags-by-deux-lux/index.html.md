# 8 Great Bags by Deux Lux ...

I’m a sucker for a pretty bag, always on the look-out for something new and exciting. Right now, I’m in love with anything by Deux Lux, one of the ultra-hip brands carried at Urban Outfitters. There are so many I want! Here are 8 great bags by Deux Lux… they top my “I want” list right now…

## 1. Deux Lux Bejeweled Crossbody Purse

![Deux Lux Bejeweled Crossbody Purse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z5/e4/r0gbtp0a54c8ecab52a46477373749_400x266.jpg)

**Price:** $59.99 at [urbanoutfitters.com](http://www.urbanoutfitters.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Oh, the sparkle and shine! This tiny bag combines two of my favorite trends: cross-body bags and super-glittery details. The front flap is encrusted with dozens of shiny faux gemstones, and finished with a chain-link shoulder strap.

## 2. Deux Lux Carryall Satchel

![Deux Lux Carryall Satchel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lq/a3/zts14c0y54c8ecac8bf1e844319572_400x381.jpg)

**Price:** $68.00 at [urbanoutfitters.com](http://www.urbanoutfitters.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Really, the name of this bag says it all: it’s an oversized, slouchy satchel, ideal for carrying all of your daily work or school things. It’s made of faux leather in three colors (black, brown, and pale peach) and has top handles and an adjustable, detachable shoulder strap for endless carrying possibilities.

## 3. Deux Lux Studded Pouch

![Deux Lux Studded Pouch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gz/rq/w82e76ah54c8ecad8736b000797870_400x366.jpg)

**Price:** $88.00 at [urbanoutfitters.com](http://www.urbanoutfitters.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This faux leather bag is decorated all over with rows and rows of shiny metal studs in all sizes and colors, with a soft sating lining and lots of storage room. Pair with skinny jeans, a graphic tunic, and sky-high platforms for a night out!

## 4. Deux Lux Wave Pleated Satchel

![Deux Lux Wave Pleated Satchel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rj/um/iyuurv1k54c8ecae83b4b912595968_400x378.jpg)

**Price:** $39.99 at [urbanoutfitters.com](http://www.urbanoutfitters.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I love the mixed textures of this bag! It comes in three colors (black, brown, and yellow) and features a smooth faux leather background, braided handles, and a very pretty pleated texture on the front. There are twin top handles, and an adjustable shoulder strap. So chic!

## 5. Deux Lux Messenger Backpack

![Deux Lux Messenger Backpack](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wf/wf/yvl8edbs54c8ecaf7afa9663343815_400x526.jpg)

**Price:** $49.99 at [urbanoutfitters.com](http://www.urbanoutfitters.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
It’s a roomy messenger bag and a roomy backpack, all in one! It’s made of black faux leather with lots of antiqued-looking zippers and hardware, ideal for use as an overnight or school bag.

## 6. Deux Lux Crossbody Bag

![Deux Lux Crossbody Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pj/wl/tf450w7t54c8ecb074ca2640590731_400x241.jpg)

**Price:** $29.99 at [urbanoutfitters.com](http://www.urbanoutfitters.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Be pretty in pink with this soft, slouchy shoulder or crossbody bag. It’s exclusive to Urban Outfitters and features a small front flap decorated with a row of shiny studs and an adjustable shoulder strap. I love the color… it’s so unique!

## 7. Deux Lux Quilted Front Pocket Satchel

![Deux Lux Quilted Front Pocket Satchel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ij/ln/c6mzvnp154c8ecb167657912704418_400x412.jpg)

**Price:** $29.99 at [urbanoutfitters.com](http://www.urbanoutfitters.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This may be one of the best bag bargains I’ve seen in ages. It’s an oversized satchel made of soft faux leather with antiqued hardware and two outside pockets with a subtle quilted pattern. There are handles at the top for carrying, and a detachable, adjustable shoulder strap, too. It comes in three colors, but I like the turquoise best, though the black and grey are nice, too.

That’s just a sampling of the marvelous line of bags by Deux Lux… and I love them all, especially that pink one! Which of these pretty bags do you like best? How would you wear it? Or is there another brand or bag you like more?
