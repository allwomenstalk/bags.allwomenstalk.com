# 8 Happening Day Bags to Take Anywhere ...

Even if you don't want a closet full of purses, it really does pay to have at least one handbag for every occasion. A beach bag, a weekend bag, an evening bag, a casual bag, and a day bag – you know, when you know you're going to be out all day, and need to carry a lot with you, but you don't want anything too bulky. It's a fine line to straddle, but here are some happening day bags to take anywhere that will help you see the spectrum for the perfect daytime travel bag!

## 1. Sonia Rykiel Polka Dot Reversible Day Bag

![Sonia Rykiel Polka Dot Reversible Day Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/va/yl/bgzj5vyh54c92737ef257878426058_300x380.jpg)

**Price:** $276.98 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/r8zJ2p)
Although this bag is a bit on the pricey side, I think it's a happening day bag to take anywhere primarily because of the polka dots. It just looks so happy! The inside features some contrasting twist designs on the handles and reversed colors with the dots, which is always cool. It's like you get twice the bag for the price. More importantly for the topic at hand, there's plenty of space and lots of pockets inside and out, so you have tons of room.

## 2. City-Wide Style Handbag

![City-Wide Style Handbag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wi/k5/o47yma9l54c92738edf5a659238636_300x428.jpg)

**Price:** $99.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you're looking for something a little more sophisticated, this is a really great choice. I'm not even a fan of the whole gold/brown palette, but I'd get this in an instant. It's that gorgeous red and the way it complements both the brown on the body and the yellow handles that really gets to me. Plus, that additional wristlet is great! This bag, too, has plenty of pockets, so you can easily carry your entire life in it.

## 3. Juicy Couture “Daydreamer” Floral Print Tote

![Juicy Couture “Daydreamer” Floral Print Tote](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2011/06/daydreamer1_600x667.jpg)

**Price:** $268.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Again, although it's a little costly, this is definitely a happening day back to take anywhere. As I've said before, I really love Juicy Couture's bags and accessories, and this one is no exception. It's bright and colorful, so your day wear doesn't have to be boring, but there are plenty of pockets, the size is great, and you even get a handy – heart-shaped – little mirror inside!

## 4. Sharon Eisenhauer Haiku Hobo

![Sharon Eisenhauer Haiku Hobo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vd/qj/z8vcsriv54c92739d8f9f300008792_300x235.jpg)

**Price:** $78.00 at [zappos.com](http://www.zappos.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
You don't always want something too awfully bright, though, so you can try a day bag like this instead. It's saved from being basic black thanks to both the pebbled design and that pretty flower on the front. Meant for shopping, it has lots of additional pockets hidden inside, and they vary in size so you can fit just about anything, from cell phones to wallets to makeup kits.

## 5. ASOS Faux Croc Bar Day Bag

![ASOS Faux Croc Bar Day Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tp/q4/x0mmnln554c9273aa6014036369130_300x300.jpg)

**Price:** $64.63 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/7JpnQG)
I'm not usually a fan of yellow – or ochre, I should perhaps say. However, something about this bag just jumped out at me. The color is actually part of it, as is the faux croc texture. Mostly, though, I think it's the sturdiness of the bag. It looks sturdy, like it's functional and highly fashionable, and that's always the mark of a really great day bag.

## 6. Enviable Status Handbag

![Enviable Status Handbag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x7/ai/xol8sc7j54c9273b99d18148309520_300x438.jpg)

**Price:** $109.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Once upon a time, I was just wild about peach. I got out of the phase a little bit, but this gorgeous bag brought me back in no time at all. Isn't this pretty? On one hand, it's chic simplicity itself, but there's little touches – like the color, and that gorgeous strap – make it both hot and daintily feminine. Again, there's tons of hidden pockets and zippers – inside a lining that's mauve and silky to the touch. Precious!

## 7. Adidas by Stella McCartney Pack Away Bag

![Adidas by Stella McCartney Pack Away Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hb/vp/vd3y9dvv54c9273c97a6d387291550_300x450.jpg)

**Price:** $36.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/VKJjiQ)
Here's another happening day bag to take anywhere, and not just because it's ridiculously on sale, either. For one thing, I adore this bright coral color – it's perfect for summer! For another, the bag is incredibly roomy, with plenty of space to keep all the things you need throughout your day.

## 8. Kavu Hip Zip Bag

![Kavu Hip Zip Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r2/p3/bvicc6m954c9273db3a10668868911_300x312.jpg)

**Price:** $36.00 at [zappos.com](http://www.zappos.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I don't know why I like this bag, except that it just looks fun, you know? It's unquestionably roomy enough to carry throughout the day, with pockets inside and out. Plus, it's unique – you don't see a bag like this every day.

I hope you agree that these are some happening day bags to take anywhere, no matter what you need to bring along with you. No one wants to be lugged down while you're exploring a city or running a lot of errands, but as you can see, sometimes you need a lot of room. What do you do when you know you're going to be out all day?
