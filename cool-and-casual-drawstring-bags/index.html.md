# 9 Cool and Casual Drawstring Bags ...

Drawstring bags are the perfect accessory for working this season’s luxe casual looks. Usually in the form of a bucket or duffel, the drawstring bag’s slouchy style is a big winner right now. A drawstring bag looks super stylish paired with anything from a relaxed t-shirt dress to a classic combo of jeans and a crisp shirt. If you’re thinking of updating your off-duty look, then take a look at the following stylish drawstring bags.

## 1. Leather Drawstring Bag

![Leather Drawstring Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/09/1_leather-drawstring-bag_600x899.jpg)

For a basic everyday drawstring bag, you can’t go past one in tan leather (or leather-look material). It’ll look great paired with neutrals, pastels, brights, and even a few busy prints. Look out for any extra details like embossing or tassel attachments for a fun twist.

## 2. Berry Drawstring Bag

![Berry Drawstring Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/09/2_berry-drawstring-bag_600x702.jpg)

Berry is one of the hottest colours of the moment, so keep an eye out for drawstring bags in this super sophisticated hue. Wear your berry bag with a crisp shirt dress, ballet flats, and oversized sunglasses for a glamorous daytime look.

## 3. Leather and Suede Drawstring Bag

![Leather and Suede Drawstring Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/09/3_leather-and-suede-drawstring-bag_600x659.jpg)

A suede bag is a quick way to add some lush accents to your outfit. Sleek leather trims and gold-tone hardware also adds some polish to what would otherwise be a pretty casual style of bag. It’d look great teamed with a silk blouse, cigarette pants, and patent pumps.

## 4. Studded Drawstring Bag

![Studded Drawstring Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/09/4_studded-drawstring-bag_600x899.jpg)

Got a love of studs? Then make sure your drawstring bags have plenty of studded embellishments. If you can’t buy one, then get crafty and punch some studs onto a plain drawstring bag. Rock them with waxed jeans, biker jackets, and edgy ankle boots.

## 5. Perspex Mix Drawstring Bag

![Perspex Mix Drawstring Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/09/5_perspex-mix-drawstring-bag_600x490.jpg)

Clear bags and clutches are all the rage right now, and drawstring bags haven’t gotten off lightly either. Stay on-trend by choosing a bag with a clear body or panels. Online stores such as ASOS are doing a lot of clear accessories, so be sure to check them out if this is a look you like.

## 6. Perforated Drawstring Bag

![Perforated Drawstring Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/09/6_perforated-drawstring-bag_600x570.jpg)

You can’t go wrong with a combination of black and white. For a more minimal look when it comes to drawstring bags, stick to ones that are free of any unnecessary hardware or embellishments. Keep the rest of your outfit monochrome for a cool, high fashion look.

## 7. Colored Drawstring Bag

![Colored Drawstring Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/09/7_colored-drawstring-bag_600x417.jpg)

Keep it feminine by wearing a blush toned drawstring with your outfit. Look out for ones with daintier features such as slimmer straps and lighter hardware. Keep the feminine theme going by wearing a pretty blouse, full skirt, and peeptoe shoes.

## 8. Embellished Drawstring Bag

![Embellished Drawstring Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/09/8_embellished-drawstring-bag_600x377.jpg)

Want something a little bit eclectic when it comes to drawstring bags? Then the answer is all in the embellishments, my friend! It could be some colourful embroidery, mirrored panels, or even intricate beading. Such bags may be harder to find in your typical fashion chain store so be prepared to hunt them down in places like markets or even on your next exotic holiday.

## 9. Sporty Leather Drawstring Bag

![Sporty Leather Drawstring Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/09/9_sporty-leather-drawstring-bag_600x899.jpg)

Drawstring bags are perfect for accessorizing a sport luxe outfit. Ones in darker, washed leathers are great if you’re after something a bit more versatile. Things like exposed zipper details also help add a bit of edge. They’d look great teamed with chambray denim, a leather skirt or shorts, and ankle boots.

You can pick up many of these drawstring bags from stores such as Topshop, ASOS and H&M, and at fairly reasonable prices too. What do you think of these drawstring bags for a cool and casual look?
