# Delightful Little Lunch Bags You'll Be Proud to Carry ...

A brown bag to carry your lunch might be eco-friendly but it’s hardly stylish is it? You spend ages choosing your outfit, bag and shoes, so why shouldn’t you carry a great lunch bag too? I found some lunch bags I’d be happy to carry – hope you like them too.

## 1. You Can’t Go Wrong with Black and White

![You Can’t Go Wrong with Black and White](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uy/ko/yxd1iv7p5571ab6e32637002501785_450x450.jpg)

Want something a little bit funky but that will also not stand out whatever your outfit? This Fit and Fresh Downtown Designer Lunch Bag in Zebra with Ice is just the job. I love the shape and that it has a useful little pocket on the outside.
Price: $18.20 + shipping
[walmart.com](http://www.walmart.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 2. Great if You’ve Got a Passion for Pink

![Great if You’ve Got a Passion for Pink](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fb/bk/waftzjq55571ab6e8aef8949913980_450x450.jpg)

The Gaiam Lunch Tote has a pretty pattern called wildflower. This is a nice roomy lunch bag so if you like to pack your lunch in little containers and keep your fruit safe as well as being able to carry a bottle of water/can of drink, you’ll find this eminently suitable.
Price: $9.99 + shipping
[walmart.com](http://www.walmart.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 3. A Must if You Want a Professional Look

![A Must if You Want a Professional Look](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nt/cd/pjs17gwq5571ab7303784343432296_600x469.jpg)

It comes in a range of glorious colors from black to candy pink and blazing yellow. You can even have it personalized with your initials or name (fee applies.) And how cute is that furry gorilla?

## 4. Love Me, Love My Lunch Bag

![Love Me, Love My Lunch Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lr/d3/gl6m6l9v5571ab70f353b075216866_600x550.jpg)

If you’re enamored by anything with a heart pattern, this will set you all a-flutter. Isn’t it a cutey? Nice shape. Nice colors. Nice size. Nice price. Easy to clean. Lightweight. What more could you want from a lunch bag? And it comes with a bonus – a free stylus.
Price: $8.99 + free shipping
[amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 7. An Apple a Day Keeps the Doctor Away

![An Apple a Day Keeps the Doctor Away](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dr/ax/s5s718pe5571ab9c57da4371315215_450x450.jpg)

## 8. Bugs You Won’t Mind Joining You for Lunch
