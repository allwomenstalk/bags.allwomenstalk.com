# 7 Fun Facts about the Worlds Most Famous Bags ...

Enjoy 7 fun facts about some of the world's most recognizable and coveted handbags: Chanel, Louis Vuitton and Hermes! These interesting and shocking facts are brought to you by Lollipuff.com, the site that provides the best deals on designer goods.

## 1. The Chanel 2.55 Bag Increases in Price Each Year!

![The Chanel 2.55 Bag Increases in Price Each Year!](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/es/lj/vmcauy54_600x399.jpg)

Extremely classic and effortlessly versatile, the Chanel double flap bag is the most famous bag in the world.

The classic Chanel double flap bag, oftentimes referred to as the 2.55, experiences an average of 15% price increase each year due to continued heavy demand. Better buy that gorgeous Chanel bag before it gets even more expensive! The classic medium size is currently retailing at $4900.

## 2. 2.55 Stands for February 1955 Which is when the Orginal Classic Flap Was Created

![2.55 Stands for February 1955 Which is when the Orginal Classic Flap Was Created](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xj/f0/rovyhgs4_600x583.jpg)

The classic Chanel double flap is oftentimes referred to as the 2.55 bag. 2.55 stands for February 1955, when the original classic flap was invented! Though the bag has had a few changes over the year, the overall structure and shape has proven to be timeless.

## 3. Hermes Birkin Bags Are the Most Expensive Designer Bags

![Hermes Birkin Bags Are the Most Expensive Designer Bags](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wr/a7/e7qyibbj_408x480.jpg)

Hermes Birkin bags are the most expensive line of bags out there! The price normally ranges from $10,000 to $120,000! Hermes utilizes an array of finishes, from canvas fabric to calfskin to even ostrich and alligator!

## 4. A French Actress Was the Inspiration for the Hermes Birkin Bag

![A French Actress Was the Inspiration for the Hermes Birkin Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nr/ar/gxowm85k_600x750.jpg)

The beautiful French actress, Jane Birkin, is the inspiration behind the Birkin bag. Bumping into Jean-Louis Dumas, the president of Hermes at the time, on a flight, the actress lamented about the poor handbag selection available. Shortly thereafter, the much celebrated Birkin bag was born.

## 5. Louis Vuitton is the Most Counterfeited Designer in the World

![Louis Vuitton is the Most Counterfeited Designer in the World](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ax/rx/mav9ppq3_600x400.jpg)

Of all of the designer handbags, Louis Vuitton is the most common. Louis Vuitton is also the most counterfeited designer brand in the world!

## 6. The Louis Vuitton Speedy Bag is Made Mostly of Canvas, Not Leather

![The Louis Vuitton Speedy Bag is Made Mostly of Canvas, Not Leather](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xt/ud/zf2215y4_500x397.jpg)

Unlike most high-end bags, the famous Louis Vuitton Speedy bag is actually predominantly made in a super-durable canvas material. Only the handles and trim are leather.

## 7. The Louis Vuitton Speedy Bag Was One of Audrey Hepburn's Favorite Bags

![The Louis Vuitton Speedy Bag Was One of Audrey Hepburn's Favorite Bags](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cx/z1/nuhghp5m_400x333.jpg)

Designer handbags have always gained immediate popularity with celebrities. The Louis Vuitton Speedy bag was also one of Audrey Hepburn's favorite bags.
