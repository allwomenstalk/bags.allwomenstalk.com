# 9 Affordable Yet Gorgeous Everyday Totes ...

**Everyday tote** bags are ideal for carrying around your daily essentials. Everyday totes can range from a simple leather handbag to a fabric bag for carrying around your shopping or gym gear. Personally, I like my handbags with clean, simple designs, so tote bags are always at the top of my list. Take a look at some stylish tote bags for everyday below.

## 1. Modcloth Wandering Westward Shoulder Bag

![Modcloth Wandering Westward Shoulder Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/02/1_modcloth-wandering-westward-shoulder-bag_341x360.jpg)

**Price:** $54.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This faux leather **everyday tote** has a wonderfully earthy design. It features contrasting tan hues and decorative stitching around a V-shaped motif. It’s also roomy enough to carry all your day to day needs. Keep the boho vibe going by teaming this bag with flared jeans, a slouchy white t-shirt, and beaded jewellery.

## 2. Topshop Black Leather Shopper

![Topshop Black Leather Shopper](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/02/2_topshop-black-leather-shopper_350x524.jpg)

**Price:** $95.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/pJ422x)
This simple black tote would make an excellent everyday bag. It is made from real leather and features scallop details around the edges. The scallop details are a really feminine, on-trend touch.

## 3. Marla Bella Bow Tote Bag

![Marla Bella Bow Tote Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/02/3_marla-bella-bow-tote-bag_350x398.jpg)

If you like your **everyday totes** to have more of an emphasis on design details, then take a look at this stylish bow tote. It is made from leather-look materials and features contrast stitching, a fold over fastening, engraved logo black, and bow design to the front. It also comes with a detachable shoulder strap.

## 4. Topshop Ring Trim Fabric Tote

![Topshop Ring Trim Fabric Tote](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/02/4_topshop-ring-trim-fabric-tote_350x525.jpg)

## 5. Modcloth Owl Carry Your Things Bag

![Modcloth Owl Carry Your Things Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/02/5_modcloth-owl-carry-your-things-bag_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $49.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you’re after something more playful or youthful when it comes to an **everyday tote**, then check out this quirky number. It has a canvas main and thick leather trims. The front of the tote features an owl motif and button embellishments. This tote would be perfect for students carrying books to school.

## 6. Forever 21 LG Woven Tote

_ **Price:** $26.80 at forever21.com_
The woven finish on this tote gives it an interesting texture. It is made from leather-look materials and features buckles accents and antique-finish hardware. A black leather tote like this is perfect for wearing on an everyday basis.

## 7. Topshop Pink Snake Insert Holdall

![Topshop Pink Snake Insert Holdall](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/02/7_topshop-pink-snake-insert-holdall_350x431.jpg)

## 8. Forever 21 Ikat Print Tote

_ **Price:** $19.80 at forever21.com_
Bold, multicoloured prints are a favourite for designers lately. Work this look into your wardrobe on a budget with this wonderful **everyday tote** bag. It features a fun ikat print and contrasting trims. If you’re after a statement making accessory, then this is definitely it.

## 9. ASOS Pocket Tote Bag

Tan and cream is a classic colour combination. This gorgeous **everyday tote** bag features a leather-look main, front flap pocket, and curved stud detailing. It also features a handy interior pocket.

These are all examples of stylish yet affordable **everyday tote** bags. You can’t go past a timeless black tote, but colourful or patterned options can be fun ways to liven up an outfit. What do you think of these everyday totes?
