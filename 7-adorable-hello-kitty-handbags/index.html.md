# 7 Adorable Hello Kitty Handbags ...

As I get older, I thought I’d get over my slight obsession with Hello Kitty, but, though I’ve been a fan since my childhood, I haven’t stop adoring her… though my obsession has matured, in a way. I no longer pine for Hello Kitty stickers or pencil sets… not I covet handbags and wallet with Hello Kitty on them! Can you blame me? She’s absolutely adorable, a fashion icon… and if you’re as Kitty-obsessed as I am, you’ll want to keep reading! Here are 7 adorable Hello Kitty handbags.

## 1. Wild Cat Bag

![Wild Cat Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wp/vy/pxhq52wq54c8f4a8cd69f012495578_350x273.jpg)

**Price:** $39.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This chic, sleek bag is a must-have for every Hello Kitty fan, for it’s mature enough for everyday use. Kitty is there, but with subtlety, for the bag is shaped like her sweet face, complete with her trademark bow. The bag has a pretty quilted pattern with a stud at each corner, and the bow is bedecked with Kitty-worthy rhinestones. Pair with a floral-print dress (or an LBD) and patent heels.

## 2. Hello, Sporty Bag in Bowling

![Hello, Sporty Bag in Bowling](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bl/fg/yi8dblro54c8f4a9e5ec4523784335_350x439.jpg)

**Price:** $27.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Wouldn’t this make a darling gym bag… or a tennis bag, since that’s Kitty’s favorite sport? The bag is a bright blue PVC with white trim, shaped like a classic bowling bag, roomy enough for storing all of your workout of sports gear - or just carrying as a bright handbag!

## 3. Hello, Sweet Tooth Wallet

![Hello, Sweet Tooth Wallet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n0/c0/h8r9u5hy54c8f4aaddb32329580900_350x256.jpg)

**Price:** $34.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Well, fabulous! This wallet appeals to both my Kitty obsession and my sweet tooth - how can I possibly resist? It’s a darling wallet, with Hello Kitty herself offering a pink donut with oodles of rainbow sprinkles. Lots of the details are a metallic pink, which makes them even more desirable… this wallet is a must-have!

## 4. Hello Kowgirl Wallet

![Hello Kowgirl Wallet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uz/q4/qjejj48854c8f4abddb67098631231_350x265.jpg)

**Price:** $34.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Hello Kitty tries her hand at the western trend, wearing a bright yellow cowgirl (or cow-kitty?) hat and a red bandanna-print bow with her initials in a western-motif brown pleather. I’d say she’s has the trend just right, though the wallet itself is handy enough to stand on its own, with plenty of slots for ID and debit cards.

## 5. Hello Sporty Bag

![Hello Sporty Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yq/bn/eb15vqeu54c8f4ad2b955809737323_350x476.jpg)

**Price:** $27.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you loved the bright blue bowling bag but thought it was a little too big, give this little shoulder bag a go instead! It has the same bright motif, the same tennis-loving Kitty, but it’s smaller, more like an everyday shoulder bag or tote. I love it!

## 6. Cat in the Bag

![Cat in the Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/py/xr/gqbu8gok54c8f4ae0fbab194147833_350x472.jpg)

**Price:** $24.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
The cat’s out of the bag… well, actually, the cat IS the bag! This darling shoulder bag is a plushie Hello Kitty, with a perky pink bow and pink polka-dot arms and legs. There’s a zipper at the top, so all of your treasures stay secure, and a single black shoulder strap for wearing the back cross body or over one shoulder.

## 7. Hello Klutch Wristlet

![Hello Klutch Wristlet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b2/w8/nduniym254c8f4aedf48b217074043_350x212.jpg)

**Price:** $11.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This is the Hello Kitty I recall from my childhood, with a bright red bow, white face, black eyes and whiskers, and a sweet yellow nose. And this is a classic versatile wristlet, for use as a clutch or wallet (or both). Use it to hold all of your necessities, then carry it as a clutch or stash it in a bigger bag.

See? How am I ever supposed to get over my Hello Kitty obsession with these darling, versatile bags to tempt me? I adore those bright blue sporty bags… which of these Hello Kitty bags and wallets do you like best, and why? Please share, so I know I’m not alone!
