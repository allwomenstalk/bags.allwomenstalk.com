# 10 Fantastic Pastel Bags ...

**Pastel bags are a big** trend for spring. Think soft pinks, mints, lilacs and butter yellows. If you’re hesitant about fully embracing the pastel trend, then a pastel bag is a great way to incorporate this colour trend into your look. Take a look at a few pretty pastel bags below.

## 1. Topshop Pink Colour Drench Holdall

![Topshop Pink Colour Drench Holdall](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/03/1_topshop-pink-colour-drench-holdall_400x429.jpg)

**Price:** $72.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/fKIGKB)
If you’re looking **at larger sized pastel bags**, then go for ones with fairly minimal designs. Anything too fussy can be overkill! This pastel pink bag has a simple rectangular design and features a top zip fastening and twin grab top handles. It would look great worn with a white shirtdress and nude ballet pumps.

## 2. Modcloth Pistachio Pastel Bag

![Modcloth Pistachio Pastel Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/03/2_modcloth-pistachio-pastel-bag_350x216.jpg)

**Price:** $39.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This sweet side bag comes in a wonderful shade of pistachio green. It has a long shoulder strap, press lock closure, and zip compartment at the back. Try some subtle pastel colour blocking and team this bag with faded pink or orange clothing.

## 3. ASOS Pyramid Metal Tip Clutch

![ASOS Pyramid Metal Tip Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/03/3_asos-pyramid-metal-tip-clutch_400x243.jpg)

**Price:** $21.49 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/FEg6Hv).
Add this pretty **pastel bag to your** evening wear arsenal this spring. This envelope shaped clutch comes in a lovely lilac colour and has a textured finish. Wear it at night with a slinky top, cropped cigarette style pants, pointed pumps, and metallic jewellery.

## 4. Modcloth Blueberry Juice Bag

![Modcloth Blueberry Juice Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/03/4_modcloth-blueberry-juice-bag_330x304.jpg)

**Price:** $72.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This light blue bag is great for everyday use. Like most things on the Modcloth site, this bag has a vintage inspired design. It’s made from leather-look materials and features a colourful patterned cloth lining. Team this bag with your favourite spring florals.

## 5. Topshop Bowling Bag

![Topshop Bowling Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/03/5_topshop-bowling-bag_400x473.jpg)

**Price:** $72.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/D9vbUU)
Mint colours are bang on trend for spring. I also love this style of bag – it’s simple, functional, and fashionable. Wear this pastel bag with a white pleated maxi skirt, neutral singlet, and leather sandals for an effortlessly chic look.

## 6. Topshop Lemon Snake Twist Lock Bag

![Topshop Lemon Snake Twist Lock Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/03/6_topshop-lemon-snake-twist-lock-bag_400x340.jpg)

**Price:** $50.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/EbNYa0)
A small, boxy handbag will be your saviour this season. Extra points if it comes in colour. This lemon coloured bag also has a textured snake finish. Work an off-duty look and team this bag with a denim shirt, coloured shorts, and lace-up shoes.

## 7. ASOS Oversized Bowler

![ASOS Oversized Bowler](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/03/7_asos-oversized-bowler_400x514.jpg)

**Price:** $64.46 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/szgLQM).
This oversized pastel bag will definitely make a statement. It has a structured design and is made from leather-look materials. The butter yellow colour is ideal for pulling off this season’s pastel trend.

## 8. Topshop Pink Double Handle Chain Bag

![Topshop Pink Double Handle Chain Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/03/8_topshop-pink-double-handle-chain-bag_400x422.jpg)

**Price:** $76.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/pPgcx5)
This two-tone bag features pastel pink panels and neutral sides. It has a front pocket, grab top handles, and shoulder strap. This is quite a ladylike bag and would look great with an equally ladylike outfit.

## 9. ASOS Weave Metal Bar Clutch

![ASOS Weave Metal Bar Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/03/9_asos-weave-metal-bar-clutch_400x271.jpg)

**Price:** $53.72 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/SNZ0fK)
This pastel bag has an interesting woven leather-look main. It has an envelope design and features a fold-over flap front with gold metal detailing. Team this oversized clutch with a neutral dress and tan wedge sandals.

## 10. Mulberry Pink Pastel Bag

![Mulberry Pink Pastel Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/03/10_mulberry-pink-pastel-bag_288x563.jpg)

**Price:** $775.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If designer bags are you’re thing then you can’t go past this pastel number by Mulberry. Known as ‘The Lily’, this bag has a crinkle patent leather finish and features a looped chain link strap. It’s quite an elegant, timeless design.

The great thing about **pastel bags is that they’re** an easy way to incorporate this season’s must have colour palette into your wardrobe. A pastel bag can add just the right amount of colour to an outfit. What do you think of these pastel bags? Any favourites?
