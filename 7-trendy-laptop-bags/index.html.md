# 7 Trendy Laptop Bags ...

Most laptop bags feature unsightly amounts of padding and nylon material. While practical, it’s also nice to have something a little more fashionable. Rest assured that it is possible to keep your laptop safe and sound, and in a stylish bag to boot. Just take a look at the following couple of bags!

## 1. Mulberry Bayswater Patent Leather Laptop Bag

![Mulberry Bayswater Patent Leather Laptop Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d1/rh/i7qk8uop54c8f4ba2ba70451053743_400x512.jpg)

**Price:** $1395.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/6ibAmu)
The iconic Bayswater bag is now available in a specially designed laptop form. As part of the Mulberry for Apple line, this bag features an internal padded pouch pocket for your laptop. I love the high-shine patent finish. It’s the perfect combination of tradition and trend!

## 2. Ally Capellino Oil Tanned Leather 15” Document and Laptop Holder

![Ally Capellino Oil Tanned Leather 15” Document and Laptop Holder](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e8/pw/y3fdl65854c8f4bb14f62893394208_400x256.jpg)

**Price:** $313.78 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/tTgxgL)
I love the oiled leather finish of this bag. The tan leather is expertly worn down. The bag features a fold-over design, pin stud fastening, and raw edges. This bag would be such a wonderful way to carry your laptop from one place to the next.

## 3. Fiorelli Hayworth Slim Laptop Bag

![Fiorelli Hayworth Slim Laptop Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lv/ru/b1e7onk754c8f4bc3f2d0377492254_400x343.jpg)

**Price:** $141.65 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/q63SeQ)
This leather laptop bag is sleek and sophisticated. The slim design of this bag is what really won me over. I also really like the faux crocodile skin finish. This bag looks just like any other day bag, and would be well suited for those working in a corporate environment.

## 4. Topshop Leather Laptop Case

![Topshop Leather Laptop Case](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ea/l5/rwj46bbm54c8f4bd632cb869260778_400x304.jpg)

**Price:** $100.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/jdk00F)
This bag has such a vintage feel about it. It comes with a removable strap, so you can carry it over the shoulder or in your hands. I love the fuss free design. This bag would be well suited for someone who prefers a minimalist aesthetic.

## 5. Want L.E.V. Kansai Computer Portfolio

![Want L.E.V. Kansai Computer Portfolio](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/le/gi/nx3wgflb54c8f4be6fd8f182770584_400x339.jpg)

**Price:** $500.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This bag features an interior organiser, removable handle, and zip around closure. There are also a bunch of handy features such as pen holder and interior zipped pouch. I love the tan leather finish, but it also comes in an equally stylish black colour. This bag is definitely a worthwhile investment.

## 6. Bodhi Double Handle Canvas Bag

**Price:** $148.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This canvas bag is casually stylish. I really like the contrast of the brown canvas and tan trim. It comes with a padded laptop compartment, interior zip pockets, and outer pouch compartments. This is a great option for those who like to carry their life (or work, at least) in their bags.

## 7. Marc by Marc Jacobs Pretty Nylon – Computer Commuter Bag

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Pretty Nylon – Computer Commuter Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yp/oz/qehmaqrb54c8f4c08a62f709473612_400x295.jpg)

**Price:** $98.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This nylon bag is simple and understated. The nylon is embroidered with oversized logo letters, which I think adds another dimension to the overall design. It features casually knotted top handles and an optional adjustable shoulder strap. This bag will fit most 15” laptops.
