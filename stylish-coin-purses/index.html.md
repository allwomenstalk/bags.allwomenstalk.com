# 8 Stylish Coin Purses ...

Coin Purses are functional, but that doesn’t mean that they can’t be fashionable as well. Too many coins can weigh down your wallet, so keep a coin purse handy to carry all your shrapnel. Clip-frame coin purses are traditionally ladylike, but there are also a range of modern designs that you may want to keep an eye out for. Take a look at a few stylish coin purses below.

## 7. Gucci Moby Dick Leather and Canvas Coin Purse

From zip-top coin purses to classic clasp frames, the options are endless. You don’t have to fork out for designer styles, as there are a range of stylish and affordable options available.
