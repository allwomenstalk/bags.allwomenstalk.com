# 8 Stylish Leather Satchels ...

The leather satchel is a well worn classic and never goes out of style. Satchel shaped bags can complement a range of outfits and come in a range of sizes. They are versatile and in most cases can be worn over the shoulder or carried by the handle. Take a look at a few traditional and modern stylish leather satchels below.

## 1. Asos Leather Top Handle Buckle Satchel

![Asos Leather Top Handle Buckle Satchel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q4/bw/zt0zngch54c8f94d0c77c912177936_400x305.jpg)

**Price:** $93.21 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/HLSg90)
This large leather satchel would be a great bag to have on high rotation. It features contrasting straps, turn lock fastening, and silver tone buckle embellishments. The long shoulder strap is also removable. This satchel would be well suited to a student or someone always on the go.

## 2. Asos Leather Vintage Style Satchel

![Asos Leather Vintage Style Satchel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c3/ru/it0efuqz54c8f94e04227450569231_400x270.jpg)

**Price:** $64.55 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/ms3r4I)
The most stylish leather satchels often look vintage in design. This understated satchel features a fold over flap, twist-clasp fastening, and short grab handle. The gold tone hardware offsets the black leather quite nicely. Black goes with pretty much anything, so this bag can easily be worn with a variety of outfits.

## 3. J.W. Hulme Fairmount Satchel

![J.W. Hulme Fairmount Satchel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r5/ju/tykyqqwx54c8f94edd80f456105294_400x424.jpg)

**Price:** $600.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Most of the stylish leather satchels in this list feature a fold over flap design. However, this tan leather satchel features a top zip closer. The detachable shoulder strap also means this satchel can be worn as a normal bag, possibly carried in the crook of your elbow. The [tan leather](https://allwomenstalk.com/the-secrets-of-french-chic-fashion-and-materials/) is soft and supple, and neutral enough to be worn throughout the day.

## 4. Topshop Premium Nude Leather Flower Stamped Satchel

![Topshop Premium Nude Leather Flower Stamped Satchel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dy/pv/noa2vhgm54c8f94fddc98784549699_400x448.jpg)

**Price:** $170.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/CKLkQK)
If you identify as being a bit more free-spirited, then take a look at this flower stamped satchel. Most leather satchels conjure up images of a strict, scholarly aesthetic. The light nude leather is soft and feminine, while the flower stamped cutout detail gives the satchel a slight bohemian air. This satchel would look stylish paired with flowing maxi length skirts and dresses.

## 5. Cambridge Satchel Company 15” Batchel

![Cambridge Satchel Company 15” Batchel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z0/zo/z19trlhd54c8f950e3bb2258780465_400x310.jpg)

**Price:** $200.82 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/eqChn5)
Leather satchels are the speciality of the Cambridge Satchel Company. Once relegated to the realms of school uniforms, these satchels are becoming increasingly fashionable. Their satchels come in a range of colours from traditional black, to eye-popping yellow. This tan and black contrasting satchel has quite a directional and modern design.

## 6. Miu Miu Leather Satchel

![Miu Miu Leather Satchel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vy/n9/u2969vre54c8f95209758794884778_400x360.jpg)

**Price:** $1695.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/R5rhNo)
This pastel blue Miu Miu satchel is a chic designer option. This bag comes with a short top handle, detachable shoulder strap, and gold hardware. The pastel blue leather is a great way to incorporate the current pastel trend into your look. Experiment with colour and pair this bag with an equally sweet dress.

## 7. Asos Envelope Front Leather Satchel

![Asos Envelope Front Leather Satchel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ot/m0/lc4xybrs54c8f9531a36c413731733_400x248.jpg)

This small leather satchel is would make a playful addition to your wardrobe. This satchel is made from red textured leather and features and envelope-shaped front flap. It has a push lock opening, concealed front pocket, adjustable shoulder strap. This bag is perfect for storing your bare essentials!

## 8. Topshop 2 Tone Colour Satchel

![Topshop 2 Tone Colour Satchel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/so/n7/c5xs3h6h54c8f95436697932471786_400x464.jpg)
