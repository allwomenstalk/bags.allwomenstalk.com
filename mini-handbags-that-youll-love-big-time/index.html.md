# 9 Mini Handbags That You'll Love Big Time ...

Mini handbags are the must-have item of the season. Bag trends are being downsized and we’re seeing a definite drift towards teeny tiny handbags with just enough room to house the bare essentials. Luxury labels are releasing miniature versions of their signature designs while affordable chain store brands are selling pint-sized bags for your quick fashion fix. If you like the look this accessories trend, check out these mini handbags you’ll want to get your hands on.

## 1. ASOS Mini Crossbody Bag

![ASOS Mini Crossbody Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ll/br/i6iwo3ou_600x331.jpg)

Really pack a punch with your mini handbags by choosing one in a statement-making colour. This mini crossbody bag from ASOS comes in a radiant orchid colour, which style spotters will notice is right on trend. You can wear this stylish mini bag slung on your shoulder or remove the strap to use it as a chic clutch.

## 2. Topshop Leather Chain Strap Pouch

![Topshop Leather Chain Strap Pouch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u6/04/d6xb00o6_600x338.jpg)

This pouch-like handbag from Topshop is perfect for pulling off the monochrome trend. This pint-sized handbag has a white snake-effect finish and comes with a contrasting slim black strap. Team it with an all-white outfit for a striking monochrome look or dress it down with denim.

## 3. Warehouse Twistlock Mini Bag

![Warehouse Twistlock Mini Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kx/cw/p5vpz544_600x337.jpg)

Style speaks volumes and this tiny handbag has an elegant, vintage-inspired design. It has a classic, structured shape with a flap front design and gold tone twist-lock closure. This elegant handbag is perfect for teaming with the most feminine of outfits.

## 4. Forever 21 Structured Crossbody Bag

![Forever 21 Structured Crossbody Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sm/qk/dkmbdjbp_600x331.jpg)

This gem of a handbag from Forever 21 comes in a classic design with a fold over top and twist-lock closure. Mini handbags in bold colours, like this one, are perfect for adding a pop of colour to your outfits this season.

## 5. ASOS Mini Festival Bag

![ASOS Mini Festival Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ms/em/j9r7hffn_600x274.jpg)

With only enough room for the bare essentials – we’re talking a small phone, keys, and maybe a stick of lip balm – this tiny bag from ASOS is really taking downsizing to the next level. It comes in a pastel pink colour with a boxy structured shape. It’s been dubbed the festival bag with good reason as it will keep your essentials safe and secure but won’t get in the way of a few dance moves.

## 6. Furla Candy Mini Crossbody Bag

![Furla Candy Mini Crossbody Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k5/g9/nj530q1w_600x368.jpg)

Spring and pastel colours go hand in hand. This mini handbag by Furla is made from a colourful PVC material, which gives it that ‘candy’ finish. Work a fresh spring look and team it with a crisp white dress and nude shoes.

## 7. Sole Society ‘Natalia’ Mini Bag

![Sole Society ‘Natalia’ Mini Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xe/pr/gszyyo8j_452x352.jpg)

Dress up your outfits with this stylish tan mini bag from Sole Society. It has a classic flap top design with gold tone hardware and a removable chain link strap. Wear it with an outfit of a breezy blouse tucked into skinny jeans for an effortlessly stylish everyday look.

## 8. Diane Von Furstenberg ‘440’ Metallic Bag

![Diane Von Furstenberg ‘440’ Metallic Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xq/0x/poxciwp0_600x386.jpg)

Make your mark in metallics with this stylish miniature bag by Diane von Furstenberg. The compact ‘440’ bag comes in an elegantly futuristic-looking metallic design and would be perfect for dressing up an evening outfit.

## 9. Saint Laurent Classic Duffle Mini Bag

![Saint Laurent Classic Duffle Mini Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mz/pm/e46grz7o_600x556.jpg)

This timeless looking handbag is a miniature version of Saint Laurent’s classic duffle bag. It has a perfectly fuss-free design and comes with top handles and a long shoulder strap. It’s a stylish way to tote around the bare essentials.

It’s all about the mini handbag this season, and there are plenty of styles out there to suit all budgets. What do you think of these mini handbags? Will you be embracing the trend for pint-sized handbags this season?
