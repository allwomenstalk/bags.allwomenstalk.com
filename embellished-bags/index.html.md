# 7 Embellished Bags ...

If you want to add an extra element to your outfit, try doing so in the form of an embellished handbag. Embellishments can come in a range of different styles and materials. From studs to sequins, designers are using embellishments in a manner of creative ways. Take a look at a range of embellished bags below.

## 1. Topshop Teal Fabric Rose Chain Handle Bag

![Topshop Teal Fabric Rose Chain Handle Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ug/gd/pa7o70kg54c8fd7144a3d663325107_350x297.jpg)

**Price:** $60.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/ltMOtI)
This bag resembles a bouquet of roses. This bouquet-style appearance has been created through the use of all over fabric rose embellishments. This pouch bag has a chain strap and magdot fastening. This delicate bag would look great against softly flowing evening dresses.

## 2. Antik Batik ‘Amit’ Embellished Shoulder Bag

![Antik Batik ‘Amit’ Embellished Shoulder Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yq/tu/cxjltldh54c8fd724c6f9203702825_350x267.jpg)

**Price:** $195.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/jCSsYA)
The overlapping sequin embellishments add an extra textural element to this bag. It has a grey leather body, tasselled zip fastening, and chain shoulder strap. The front of the bag features the distinctive triangle-shaped sequin flaps. This casually elegant bag would work well against a [simple black dress](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/how-to-style-your-little-black-dress-different-ways/).

## 3. Modcloth ‘Cave Cadence’ Shoulder Bag

![Modcloth ‘Cave Cadence’ Shoulder Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rs/yc/r3zdcd9w54c8fd7368374857190355_350x285.jpg)

**Price:** $54.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Carry your essentials in style with this embellished shoulder bag. The bag has a military style design, but the stud embellishments give it an edgy twist. It is made from leather-look material and has a flap front, buckle closure, and adjustable shoulder strap. This bag would work well with an outfit of jeans, loose t-shirt, and lace-up ankle boots.

## 4. Forever21 Embellished Satin Clutch

![Forever21 Embellished Satin Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bj/xb/fe1ze9m854c8fd74655d0408163489_350x400.jpg)

**Price:** $22.80 at [forever21.com](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Embellished bags come in a range of designs and sizes. This satin clutch would make an elegant addition to an evening outfit. It has a lace and satin composition and round sequins throughout. It has a push lock closure and is fully lined. Sling the chain strap over your shoulder or hide the strap inside and use as a clutch.

## 5. Tasha Jewel Box Clutch

![Tasha Jewel Box Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lu/rb/e8nugljs54c8fd7544130222875811_350x253.jpg)

**Price:** $120.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
An embellished clutch is a great way of adding added glam to an evening look. It has a crinkled metallic finish and is adorned with an array of faceted gems. It also has a gem-encrusted clasp closure. This beautiful bag would be a great way to accessorise a sleek cocktail dress.

## 6. 3.1 Phillip Lim ‘Edie’ Bow Embellished Suede Bag

![3.1 Phillip Lim ‘Edie’ Bow Embellished Suede Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tm/nf/x1jn4mcr54c8fd7629285180608399_350x187.jpg)

**Price:** $695.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/8WRr82)
Bow embellished bags can add a touch of feminine elegance to an outfit. This Phillip Lim bag gives femininity a punk edge by adding studs to the bow embellishment. It comes in a greyish suede leather and features a fold over flap front, shoulder strap, and silver hardware. This bag is of a medium size and can be worn over the shoulder or across the body.

## 7. Topshop Chain Handle Studded Bag

![Topshop Chain Handle Studded Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ka/wt/tmozz15f54c8fd77207c8546917601_350x339.jpg)

**Price:** $60.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/HcpCiT)
Studs are a popular choice for embellished bags. This bag features different sized studs in a circular pattern. The pattern also complements the circular design of the bag. This bag has a top zip fastening and comes with a chain link shoulder strap. Wear this bag with a uniform of t-shirts and jeans for a street smart look.
