# Heres What Your Bag Says about Your Personality ...

People love to assess personality by the things we do, what we wear, our hair color, zodiac sign … it all comes down to trying to better understand the complex creatures we are. It’s often hardly scientific but it can be fun seeing if you match up to the conclusions reached. Shall we see what our handbags say about us?

## 1. Tote

![Tote](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bp/ir/k2nygki155c5614010161978060674_554x610.jpg)

When thinking about what your bag says about you, the tote signifies the kind of woman who is constantly on the go and needs an accessory that will help her fit everything she needs to go about her life. Whether it’s an extra pair of shoes for a quick change after work or even a whole new outfit, the tote has got your back and you’ll never run out of much needed space.

## 2. Hobo

![Hobo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ja/ul/rvs65fcb55c561571f443456658234_499x750.jpg)

The hobo bag is perfect for that special kind of woman for whom not even a tote is big enough to satisfy their every day needs. The vast space available in a hobo means that you are an aspirational and confident woman with a have-it-all attitude. Why should you have to leave any accessories at home when your bag can accommodate them all!?

## 3. Backpack

![Backpack](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v9/xz/kdoxb9hl55c561808f034281622319_600x636.jpg)

The backpack is a perfect choice for those who are still playful and young at heart. A stylish backpack signifies a love of adventure and willingness to try new experiences, and this could pertain to wanting to explore a new city or having the confidence and conviction to leap head first in to a brand new career.

## 4. Clutch

![Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eu/fj/rpuqxqko55c5619b249ae070052187_500x750.jpg)

The clutch is the epitome of classic, simple glamour, perhaps best suited to a woman who doesn’t need a big statement to look stylish. Clutches aren’t famous for their capacity, so if you are the kind of girl who only leaves the house with her phone, lipstick and some cash, then you may have found your bag soul mate!

## 5. Cross Body

![Cross Body](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ln/ah/sehjpsqj55c56a234b472767922481_500x667.jpg)

The cross body is the bag of choice for the modern style conscious and essentials only type of girl. No room for a change of shoes or new outfit here, so a cross body bag wearer is usually a practical kind of girl who is already wearing exactly what she needs without having to carry alternatives.

## 6. Satchel

![Satchel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ma/iz/sj235hq355c56a35c38e8483950184_466x700.jpg)

The satchel is the iconic symbol of the focused career woman. Such an organized bag belongs in the hands of an organized owner, somebody who is perfectly adept at balancing their personal and professional lives and achieving great success in both.

## 7. Shopper

![Shopper](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rk/tx/lo1k3yr755c56a55ec6cb287998460_600x903.jpg)

The shopper is the kind of bag that is perfect for a woman who absolutely lives for the weekend. Whether you are popping down to the local farmer’s market, doing a spot of window browsing in town, or running important errands, the shopper bag allows you to complete all of these tasks whilst still looking effortlessly stylish.

## 8. The Bowling Bag

![The Bowling Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xx/b9/ww9ecy6d55c56a8a8f84b142123129_465x696.jpg)

The bowling bag is the epitome of fashionable structure, so therefore is a great choice for people who like order and simplicity in their lives. A solid and structured bag for a girl who adheres strictly to the appointments written in her planner.

## 9. Fanny Pack

![Fanny Pack](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/el/m1/nqqaoqaz55c56a9fb29c7175483760_500x750.jpg)

The fanny pack is the perfect accessory for those of us who still think the early 90s were the best years of our lives! Why carry the burden on your bag or shoulders when you can have all your essentials resting right there on your hips!

So what’s your main bag type of choice? Have “they” got you right in what your handbag says about you?
