# 9 Stunning Evening Bags Fit for the Party Season ...

**Evening Bags** can make or break your party outfits. When it comes to party dressing, I like to think it’s a time to be a bit more frivolous with our outfit choices. Decadent embellishments such as glitter, sequins, and jewels are all acceptable ways to add a bit of oomph to your outfit. **Evening bags** are an easy and versatile way of incorporating this into your outfit. Take a look at a few stunning **evening bags** below.

## 1. ASOS Mirrored Bow Clutch

![ASOS Mirrored Bow Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e3/hi/c5uulvgo54c93621e314e441738426_400x315.jpg)

**Price:** $50.90 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/uNtjSe)
This oversized clutch comes with a large mirrored bow to the front. You can’t go past a good bow detail, and the mirrored finish on this particular one gives it a more modern edge. This clutch would look great teamed with a leather shift dress and matching metallic cuff bracelets.

## 2. Jimmy Choo Caro Glitter Mini Bag

![Jimmy Choo Caro Glitter Mini Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ky/x0/zyxdiuln54c93622ef213323111795_400x299.jpg)

**Price:** $525.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/X5Y3tX)
Glitter evening bags are one way of incorporating some glitz and glamour into your outfit. This Jimmy Choo bag has a gunmetal leather body covered in multicoloured glitter. It also comes in an optional gold chain strap. It would look fabulous with simple neutral outfit.

## 3. Modcloth Shine Bright, Shine Far Clutch

![Modcloth Shine Bright, Shine Far Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tn/b0/bk11pwcq54c93624ab670457195405_336x304.jpg)

**Price:** $47.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Stand out from the crowd with this star-shaped evening bag. It has a hard, structured body and features a rhinestone closure. Wear it with a classic LBD for a simple yet stunning night time look. Remember, the best evening bags are original and unique.

## 4. Michael Kors Gia Leather Clutch

![Michael Kors Gia Leather Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nl/ku/lbzpcz1u54c93625a296f705850136_400x281.jpg)

**Price:** $450.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/RgrCuv)
Add a splash of colour to your evening look with this bright pink clutch. It has a simple fold-over flap design with an ostrich-effect leather finish. You could either play it safe b teaming this clutch with a neutral outfit, or try your hand at colour blocking with turquoise for something more daring.

## 5. Topshop Camelia Cross Body Bag

**Price:** $60.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/66AsQw)
Simple black evening bags are a timeless choice. For a modern touch, choose one with a high-shine patent finish. This particular bag also has a stylish flower-shaped body and comes with a long shoulder strap.

## 6. ASOS Half Moon Clutch

![ASOS Half Moon Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fg/hl/ygrzswrw54c93627baa2a669932892_400x240.jpg)

**Price:** $63.63 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/lKs51r)
This half-moon clutch has a quirky, almost vintage-inspired design. It comes in a gold metallic finish with contrasting blue fold over tab closure. This clutch may not be able to fit a lot in, but it’ll definite add an immeasurable amount of charm to your outfit.

## 7. ASOS Oversized Mirror Hardcase

![ASOS Oversized Mirror Hardcase](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ct/t3/fwcws5ds54c93628a5300779723718_400x272.jpg)

**Price:** $81.81 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/TtVIGX)
High-shine metallic finishes are oh-so-hot this season, especially when it comes to evening bags. This hardcase clutch has a curved rectangular body with a secure clasp fastening. It also features a small chain handle. Balance out the reflectiveness of the clutch by teaming it with block colours.

## 8. ASOS Nude Feather Clutch

![ASOS Nude Feather Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ea/p6/puzju7lv54c9362985138357158453_400x218.jpg)

**Price:** $45.45 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/2717wY)
Evening bags with feather details are one way to add a froufrou touch to your outfit. This bag features nude feather trims all over the body and a silver snake-effect fastening. This would look great with a neutral coloured mini-dress and glitter heels.

## 9. Valentino Rossette Mini Leather Pouch

![Valentino Rossette Mini Leather Pouch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s7/q8/bvhdn2k754c9362a87cc1893890934_400x385.jpg)

**Price:** $325.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/e18XCX)
A daring red bag is a simple way to update a tired party outfit. This bag has a rosette detail to the front and comes with a detachable chain strap. Channel a romantic aesthetic and pair this bag with a white lace blouse and black jacquard skirt.

When it comes to picking out **evening bags** this party season, don’t be afraid of colour or a bit of glitz and glamour. Heavily embellished **evening bags** and clutches are instant outfit updaters. Which of these **evening bags** would you want to take to your next party?
