# 8 Brilliant Box Clutches ...

Box clutches are a stylish way to accessorise an outfit. Compact bags are a fashionable choice right now, and box clutches fit right into this trend. Box clutches usually have a hard, durable shell. Since they’re quite structured, they don’t allow room for much give. Be prepared to carry only the bare essentials when choosing to accessorise with a box clutch. Take a look at a few fashionable box clutches below.

## 1. Diane Von Furstenberg Box Clutch

![Diane Von Furstenberg Box Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/05/1_diane-von-furstenberg-box-clutch_460x416.jpg)

**Price:** $325.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/Y51owr)
This beautiful compact clutch comes in the loveliest mint green colour. It features all over sequin embellishments and a gold twig-shaped clasp fastening. This clutch also comes with a detachable gold chain so you could wear it over your shoulder as well.

## 2. ASOS Leather Faux Stingray Clutch

![ASOS Leather Faux Stingray Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/05/2_asos-leather-faux-stingray-clutch_600x395.jpg)

**Price:** $66.35 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/M8KDYp)
The ‘stingray’ effect finish on this clutch creates an eye-catching mottled pattern. Like most box clutches, this clutch has a structured frame and features a clasp fastening at the top. Pair it with an LBD, silver heels, and silver accessories for a stylish night time look.

## 3. Alexander McQueen Shark Teeth Skull Box Clutch

![Alexander McQueen Shark Teeth Skull Box Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/05/3_alexander-mcqueen-shark-teeth-skull-box-clutch_460x402.jpg)

**Price:** $1595.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/EgaXxp)
Alexander McQueen makes the ultimate box clutches. They’re a cult fashion favourite and combine the perfect mixture of elegance and edginess. This one has a panelled black leather finish with skull clasp at the top. Work gothic inspired look by teaming this clutch with plenty of black lace.

## 4. Tasha Rose Box Clutch

![Tasha Rose Box Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/05/4_tasha-rose-box-clutch_592x344.jpg)

**Price:** $98.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you like your accessories extremely sweet and girly, this rose-embellished clutch has you covered. It has more of a rectangular shape, which is a great alternative to the squarer styles of box clutches available. Pair this clutch with a crisp white dress or cool neutral colours.

## 5. Forever21 Glittered Clutch

_ **Price:** $9.00 at forever21.com_
This glittery number is a bargain at just under $10. It comes in a hot pink colour and has a slimline design. It also comes with a detachable chain link strap should you choose to wear it on your shoulder.

## 6. Topshop Hard Phone Case with Purse

![Topshop Hard Phone Case with Purse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/05/6_topshop-hard-phone-case-with-purse_600x386.jpg)

**Price:** $28.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/KlnscX)
Box clutches usually accommodate for the bare essentials. This rose metallic one has only enough room for a phone, cards, and money. The design is slim and structured, and would look great teamed with a sophisticated evening outfit.

## 7. Nordstrom Woven Box Clutch

![Nordstrom Woven Box Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/05/7_nordstrom-woven-box-clutch_586x408.jpg)

**Price:** $78.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
You can’t go wrong with a brightly coloured clutch this season. This box clutch has a wonderful woven finish with a simple kiss-lock closure at the top. It also comes with a detachable shoulder strap. Let this clutch be the focal point of your outfit by teaming it with a simple black and white outfit.

## 8. French Connection Perspex Clutch Bag

![French Connection Perspex Clutch Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/05/8_french-connection-perspex-clutch-bag_600x408.jpg)

**Price:** $218.15 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/eZ3Q4U)
Keep an eye out for box clutches made from Perspex materials this season. The look was huge on the runways and is trickling down into the mainstream. This particular box clutch comes in a vibrant yellow colour and has a marbled finish. Wear it bright coloured dresses or bold patterns for a fun look.

You can pick up a box clutch to suit both your budget and taste. Make a statement with embellished or metallic box clutches to really give your evening outfit some edge. Which, if any, of these box clutches do you like best?
