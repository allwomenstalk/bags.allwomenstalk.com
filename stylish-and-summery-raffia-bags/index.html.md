# 9 Stylish and Summery Raffia Bags ...

Raffia bags make for stylish accessories in the summer months. From clutches to beach bags, choosing one with a raffia woven straw finish can give your outfit an on-trend touch. Adding a raffia bag to your outfit can also give it a laidback, beachy feel, no matter where you are in the world! If this is a trend you’d like to try out, take a look at some stylish raffia bags below.
