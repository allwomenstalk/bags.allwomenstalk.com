# 10 Fabulous Travel Bags ...

**Travel Bags** come in handy in all sorts of situations. You can carry them when you travel, of course – that's what **travel bags** are essentially for, after all. However, you can also carry them to the beach, while you're walking around the city, or to work. Any time you need to take a lot of things with you, these come in handy, especially since they come in all shapes and sizes. Some look like suitcases, others look like large purses – it all depends on what you need. Here are several total luxe **travel bags** that I would kill to have!

## 1. Gucci Speedy Leather-Trimmed Canvas Weekend Bag

![Gucci Speedy Leather-Trimmed Canvas Weekend Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/01/1_gucci-speedy-leather-trimmed-canvas-weekend-bag_460x394.jpg)

**Price:** $950.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/EOH9NV)
Gucci makes great **travel bags**. They come in so many sizes! I love this one because it's so much like a messenger bag. You can easily fit books, tablets, cell phones, a change of clothes, and who knows what else. Of course, for that prices, you should also be able to fit the kitchen sink!

## 2. Proenza Schouler Medium PS1 Travel Leather and Felt Tote

![Proenza Schouler Medium PS1 Travel Leather and Felt Tote](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/01/2_proenza-schouler-medium-ps1-travel-leather-and-felt-tote_423x500.jpg)

**Price:** $1,825.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/y1bUO3)
I adore the color scheme of this bag! The black leather with the felt just looks rich, sleek, and kind of sexy. It's incredibly chic, and understated enough to be used with the brightest, boldest outfits. Like net-a-porter suggests, I could see it with some gorgeous Pucci threads.

## 3. Michael Kors Leather Weekend Bag

![Michael Kors Leather Weekend Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/01/3_michael-kors-leather-weekend-bag_460x352.jpg)

**Price:** $400.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/PWObDg)
If you prefer much more understated **travel bags**, brown makes an excellent color. This bag from Michael Kors is rich and even a little sporty. It's large but it doesn't look oversized, so it could be used day to day as well.

## 4. Mulberry Fold up Medium Clipper Carryall

![Mulberry Fold up Medium Clipper Carryall](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/01/4_mulberry-fold-up-medium-clipper-carryall_460x372.jpg)

**Price:** $420.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/B55baZ)
This is one of my favorite **travel bags**, from the shape to the color to the pattern. I love that shade of poison green. This bag is also easy to carry anywhere, especially since it folds up to fit into a suitcase. I would carry this every day, all the time.

## 5. Jason Wu Weekender Patent-Trimmed Leather Tote

![Jason Wu Weekender Patent-Trimmed Leather Tote](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/01/5_jason-wu-weekender-patent-trimmed-leather-tote_460x498.jpg)

**Price:** $1,895.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/D8otcs)
You've got to love patent leather! If you want something simple but sleek, sophisticated but luxurious, this is the perfect choice. It's totally minimalist but still incredibly spacious. Any time you need a little shine, it's a perfect choice!

## 6. Bottega Veneta Intreciato Leather Weekend Bag

![Bottega Veneta Intreciato Leather Weekend Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/01/6_bottega-veneta-intreciato-leather-weekend-bag_411x500.jpg)

**Price:** $2,580.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/Tmsxg9)
Here's another minimalist choice, but completely luxe for all of that. The leather is absolutely sumptuous, giving a rich appeal to this otherwise boxy bag. It makes a serious statement, and lends itself well to elegantly understated outfits.

## 7. Lanvin Amalia Voyage Leather Suitcase

![Lanvin Amalia Voyage Leather Suitcase](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/01/7_lanvin-amalia-voyage-leather-suitcase_302x500.jpg)

**Price:** $3,475.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/eSTne6)
As mentioned, some **travel bags** are specifically for travel. This is a very, very expensive suitcase, but you'll likely never need a replacement. It's simple but still somehow glam, and it will definitely cause heads to turn as you stroll through the airport.

## 8. Victoria Beckham Oversized Textured Leather Duffle Bag

![Victoria Beckham Oversized Textured Leather Duffle Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/01/8_victoria-beckham-oversized-textured-leather-duffle-bag_460x491.jpg)

**Price:** $3,495.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/1EpDTK)
This one's expensive too, but did you ever see such a luxurious duffle bag? It's got a lot of room for anything you need and can be carried anywhere, with any kind of outfit. I recommend super bold colors and lots of knee high boots.

## 9. Bottega Veneta Intrecciato Leather Travel Trolley

![Bottega Veneta Intrecciato Leather Travel Trolley](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/01/9_bottega-veneta-intrecciato-leather-travel-trolley_342x500.jpg)

**Price:** $4,900.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/5Zq4P6)
If you like Bottega Veneta **travel bags** like the one seen above but need something better suited to airport travel, here you go! Yes, that is a lot of money – but wow, that's a cute trolley. I'd be afraid to actually travel with it, though – what if it got scuffed?!

## 10. Mulberry Leather Weekend Bag

![Mulberry Leather Weekend Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/01/10_mulberry-leather-weekend-bag_460x336.jpg)

**Price:** $1,750.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/DxLOcM)
Last but definitely not least, here's another Mulberry! Rich in color, the oak shade mixes well with chocolates, creams, and greens. You can carry anything you need quite easily, and you'll definitely look luxe yourself while you do so.

The great thing about these **travel bags** is that you can always find a knock-off or cheaper version in the same shape and design. I always think designer **travel bags** are basically a prototype for the affordable bag I really want to buy, you know? I would, however, love to have one of those Mulberry **travel bags**!
