# 9 Chic Colored Shoulder Bags ...

**Colored shoulder bags** are just one way to work the season’s latest hues into your wardrobe. While black and tan handbags are undoubtedly a staple of any wardrobe, why not shake things up with a colored shoulder bag? You can choose between brightly colored shoulder bags or pastels, but as long as it’s a color that you like, you can’t really go wrong. Why not take inspiration from the following few colored shoulder bags?

## 1. Topshop Snake Twist Lock Bag

![Topshop Snake Twist Lock Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/02/1_topshop-snake-twist-lock-bag_350x275.jpg)

**Price:** $50.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/p1awZY)
This coral colored bag is perfect for wearing during spring. It has a simple, slightly curved box design and features a fold over flap front with a twist lock closure. It also has a long shoulder strap and can be worn across the body or on one shoulder.

## 2. Proenza Schouler PS1 Leather Pouch

_ **Price:** $1345.00 at mytheresa.com_
As you can see, coral is quite a popular color this season. However, unlike the previous bag, this designer option is quite the budget breaker. It comes in the instantly recognisable PS1 design and has a modern, utilitarian feel to it.

## 3. Mulberry Lily Green Colored Shoulder Bag

_ **Price:** $875.00 at mytheresa.com_
All the luxury designers seem to have embraced **colored shoulder bags** this season. The usually refined Mulberry brand has gone for a distinctly modern look with this bright green bag. A bright green bag like this would look great teamed with a neutral color palette.

## 4. Tory Burch Patent Leather Shoulder Bag

![Tory Burch Patent Leather Shoulder Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/02/4_tory-burch-patent-leather-shoulder-bag_350x241.jpg)

**Price:** $395.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/2K5r30)
This patent-leather orange bag will add just the right amount of high intensity color to an outfit. It comes with a detachable chain-trimmed shoulder strap, so can be worn on the shoulder or carried as a clutch.

## 5. Modcloth Red Colored Shoulder Bag

![Modcloth Red Colored Shoulder Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/02/5_modcloth-red-colored-shoulder-bag_350x226.jpg)

**Price:** $64.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This messenger style bag comes in a brilliant red colour. This mini satchel also features cheetah print details at the sides. Wear it with a cream knit sweater, dark denim jeans and loafers for a casual weekend look.

## 6. Salvatore Ferragamo Leather Shoulder Bag

![Salvatore Ferragamo Leather Shoulder Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/02/6_salvatore-ferragamo-leather-shoulder-bag_340x228.jpg)

**Price:** $839.00 at [mytheresa.com](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.mytheresa.com/us_en/fancy-textured-leather-shoulder-bag.html)
A **colored shoulder bag** like this lovely purple number is ideal for the upcoming spring/summer months. It has a simple and elegant design, and features gold-toned hardware. It would look great worn with a printed sundress and strappy sandals.

## 7. Michael Michael Kors Ostrich Effect Shoulder Bag

![Michael Michael Kors Ostrich Effect Shoulder Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/02/7_michael-michael-kors-ostrich-effect-shoulder-bag_350x325.jpg)

**Price:** $270.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/UVnd5X)
This pink shoulder bag features an interesting ostrich effect finish. It has a simple box silhouette and features a fold over flap with a turn lock closure. It also has foldable handle that can be extended to shoulder strap length.

## 8. Forever 21 Structured Cross Body Bag

_ **Price:** $19.80 at forever21.com_
This structured bag has a slightly vintage inspired design. It features a front flap closure, grab top handle, and comes in a bright orange color. This bag would look great teamed with a navy and cream color palette.

## 9. Anya Hindmarch Mini Gracie Leather Shoulder Bag

![Anya Hindmarch Mini Gracie Leather Shoulder Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/02/9_anya-hindmarch-mini-gracie-leather-shoulder-bag_350x222.jpg)

**Price:** $650.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/JqH9Bf)
Blue hues are a key trend this season, so get in on the action with a blue **colored shoulder bag**. This bag features functional interior compartments such as card holders and zip pockets. You can wear this bag over the shoulder or remove the strap and use it as a clutch.

There are a range of **colored shoulder bags** to suit all styles and budgets. If you’re worried about choosing colors that are too trend-based, then stick to affordable bags that you won’t feel so guilty about casting away at the end of the season.
