# 7 Cool Colorful Satchels ...

Colorful satchels are all the rage right now. They’re a fresh and fun way of adding a dash of color to your outfit. From the cult Cambridge Satchel Company bag to chain store alternatives, there’s a colorful satchel to suit all tastes and budgets. Take a look at a few cool colored satchels below.

## 1. Coral Snake Trim Satchel

![Coral Snake Trim Satchel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/05/1_coral-snake-trim-satchel_350x342.jpg)

**Price:** $64.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/KIad5L)
Coral is one of the freshest colors for the spring-summer season so keep this in mind when choosing your colorful satchels. This satchel has a contemporary design with straps, buckles, and snake-effect trims. This satchel would look great teamed with white dress and tan sandals.

## 2. Scallop Edge Satchel

![Scallop Edge Satchel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/05/2_scallop-edge-satchel_350x315.jpg)

**Price:** $35.00 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/x2xW9f)
This lilac satchel has a structured, vintage inspired design. The scallop edging on this bag gives it a sweet touch. Wear it slung across the body or off one shoulder, and team it with a full-skirted dress and ballet flats for a perfectly feminine look.

## 3. Cherry Red Mini Satchel

![Cherry Red Mini Satchel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/05/3_cherry-red-mini-satchel_350x226.jpg)

**Price:** $64.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Not only does this colorful satchel come in a bold red hue, it also features leopard print detailing at the sides. It comes in a mini design which is perfect for when you want to travel light. This satchel would work well with an outfit of classic colors such as navy, cream, and red.

## 4. Large Leather Push Lock Satchel

![Large Leather Push Lock Satchel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/05/4_large-leather-push-lock-satchel_350x364.jpg)

**Price:** $170.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/A6L22O)
This large leather satchel comes in a brilliant shade of green. It has a simple design with a fold over flap, push lock fastening, and adjustable shoulder strap. Make this satchel the standout piece of your outfit. Team it with monochrome basics for a cool, effortless look.

## 5. PS1 Medium Suede Satchel

![PS1 Medium Suede Satchel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/05/5_ps1-medium-suede-satchel_350x312.jpg)

**Price:** $1595.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/ThM6yj)
If you want to burn a hole in your wallet, look no further than this Proenza Schouler satchel. The PS1 design has quickly become a cult favourite, and is now available in a range of colors, prints, and sizes. At over $1000, I’d be wearing this colorful satchel with anything and everything to get my money’s worth.

## 6. Blue Briefcase Satchel

![Blue Briefcase Satchel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/05/6_blue-briefcase-satchel_350x245.jpg)

**Price:** $49.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Bold blue hues are on-trend for this season. It looks especially chic when worn in the form of accessories like colorful satchels. This brilliant blue satchel would look great worn with a fun printed top, tailored shorts, and patent shoes.

## 7. Cambridge Satchel Company Purple Satchel

![Cambridge Satchel Company Purple Satchel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/05/7_cambridge-satchel-company-purple-satchel_350x252.jpg)

**Price:** $166.00 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/zHxYZT)
The Cambridge Satchel Company satchel is another cult fashion favourite. The once schoolboy inspired design is now sought after by trendsetters worldwide and is available in multiple colors, finishes, and sizes. As far as colorful satchels go, these are some of the most popular.

When it comes to choosing a colorful satchel, simply start by picking out your favourite color. When worn with basics, a colored satchel is an easy way to update your outfit. It’s all about the statement bag in a statement color, so don’t be left out this season. Which of these colorful satchels do you like best?
