# 9 Fun Colorful Handbags for Summer ...

The hottest handbags for summer are colorful, loads of fun, and sure to draw the eye. They're ideal for drawing attention to your outfit or making the most of the seasons trendiest colors, like tangerine, coral, and indigo. If you can't work these colors into your wardrobe, make your accessories work for you by choosing summer handbags in vibrant hues. Don't worry, I've got a score of absolutely gorgeous summer bags from which you can choose – just keep reading!

## 1. Dolce & Gabbana Printed Canvas & Leather Mini Shoulder Bag

![Dolce & Gabbana Printed Canvas & Leather Mini Shoulder Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/04/1_dolce-gabbana-printed-canvas-leather-mini-shoulder-bag_460x332.jpg)

**Price:** $725.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/w83Xmo)
Neons are big this season and a lot of handbags for summer incorporate the trend. It's a perfect draw, because it can be difficult to work neon into your wardrobe. So, if you want something sizzling bright, this D&G bag with its snazzy leopard print is perfect! It's both glamorous and unique, so you're sure to draw lots of comments.

The tomato hue makes a perfect spark of color with bold outfits, so your ensembles will really shine.

## 9. MICHAEL Michael Kors Margo Ostrich Effect Leather Shoulder Bag

![MICHAEL Michael Kors Margo Ostrich Effect Leather Shoulder Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/04/9_michael-michael-kors-margo-ostrich-effect-leather-shoulder-bag_460x455.jpg)

I love shopping for handbags for summer, because they're so often bright, vivacious, and fun.
