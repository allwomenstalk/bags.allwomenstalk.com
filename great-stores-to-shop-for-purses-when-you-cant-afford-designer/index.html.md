# 10 Great Stores to Shop for Purses when You Can't Afford Designer ...

There’re some great little **stores to shop for purses** that I want to tell you about. If you’re a gal like me, who loves designer purses but can’t always afford them, then this article is for you. These **stores to shop for purses** offer choices that’re stylish, decent quality and sometimes even remind you of designer choices. These shops definitely have cute choices when it comes to bags.

## 1. Lulu’s

‌﻿[lulus.com](https://www.lulus.com/‌)
Lulu’s is a lovely little online boutique that I check out frequently. They offer a decent number of purses and they’re all very fashionable, making this one of the great stores to shop for purses. Their prices are certainly better than the price of designer purses. Every once in a while you’ll come across a purse on this site that reminds you of a designer style but not so much that it appears that’s the goal. It’s more that they capture the flair of designer purses for a fraction of the cost.

## 2. Modcloth

_modcloth.com_
If you want a unique bag, shop at Modcloth. This store is all about capturing the styles of years gone by in a vintage made new sort of way. They’ve got many different sorts of styles of purses in all different shapes and sizes. Some are very vintage-y while others are more modern but with a style that almost verges on a preppy look. I’ve seen many gorgeous purses on this site.

## 3. Charming Charlie

_charmingcharlie.com_
Charming Charlie is a store that I could just lose myself in all day long. If you love accessories, this store will be a dream come true for you. This is true when it comes to purses, too. Charming Charlie offers a lot of basic styles in their bags that don’t get too far out there. Whether you want a crossbody, satchel or a hobo, you can trust Charming Charlie will have it.

## 4. Target

[target.com](https://www.target.com.au/)
Of course you can count on Target to have cute bags. Don’t they have cute everything? I’ve seen many cute bags that I would’ve guessed to be high dollar but they actually came from Target. I’ve personally carried purses from there. Next time you drop by your local Target, stop by the handbags section and see if something catches your eye.

## 5. Etsy

[etsy.com](https://www.etsy.com/)
If you love the look of one of a kind, unique handbags, then you should be shopping on Etsy. There’re many handmade bags that are simply beautiful. There are also other choices that you can choose from. When you buy a purse from Etsy you aren’t going to get a purse like everyone else is carrying. Don’t be surprised if you get a ton of questions on where your purse came from if you purchase one from here.

## 6. Amazon

_amazon.com_
Yes, Amazon carries absolutely everything. And while they do carry designer purses, they also carry many other, more budget-friendly options in handbags. In fact, I’m currently waiting on a purse to arrive that I ordered from Amazon that isn’t designer but is really cute. Amazon is a huge online store that has tons of options in handbags. You’re bound to find a purse you like.

## 7. Maurices

[maurices.com](#)﻿‌
Maurices is just an all-around great store. They have really cute tops and dresses that are perfect for when you’re watching your spending. They also have adorable purses. I’ve carried many purses from Maurices. I always receive tons of compliments on them, too.

## 8. ASOS

[asos.com](https://www.asos.com/)
If you can't afford designer purses but love the look of them, you'll find plenty of affordable dupes on ASOS! This is a great place to start looking for anything designer-inspired, plus it's guaranteed to be at a fraction of the price and just as stylish!

## 9. AMI Clubwear

_amiclubwear.com_
AMI Clubwear is also another must-see website for girls looking for great purses. With these affordable price, you're bound to see a purse, bag or wallet that'll leave you wanting to buy! It's also great for shopping for affordable clothes and other accessories.

## 10. Boohoo

[boohoo.com](https://us.boohoo.com/‌)﻿‌
You've probably heard of Boohoo by now, simply because of their already-affordable prices on everything a girl loves to wear! No doubt this applies to their bag and purse range, too, so forget designer and see what bargains you can find on Boohoo instead!
