# The 11 Best Stylish Backpacks ...

If you must schlep a bag, why not make it one of the most stylish backpacks? They combine trendy looks with function and you can take them to work, on a hike or just to and from the store. You’ve probably heard that carrying a heavy handbag is murder on your posture, so the obvious answer is to buy yourself one of these stylish backpacks to use any day.

## 1. Alexander Wang Marti Backpack

![Alexander Wang Marti Backpack](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/09040844_4740_336x420.jpg)

Alexander Wang's Marti Backpack is definitely one of the most stylish backpacks. It’s made from luxe lambskin that's totally soft when you touch it. It’s got adjustable straps, shiny hardware and it’s just the right sized stylish backpack for all the stuff you need to carry around.

**Price:** $895 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 2. Marc by Marc Jacobs Pretty Nylon Backpack

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Pretty Nylon Backpack](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/09040812_3831_349x495.jpg)

If pretty is what you desire, this Marc Jacobs backpack is the answer. Not only does it look good, but it’s also made from durable materials that will hold up no matter what life throws your way. A two-way zipper and a logo print make this a backpack you’ll love for years.

**Price:** $198 at [marcjacobs.com](http://www.marcjacobs.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 3. Cambridge Satchel 15”Satchel Backpack

![Cambridge Satchel 15”Satchel Backpack](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/09041059_4147_335x313.jpg)

Cambridge's 15" Satchel Backpack looks less like a backpack and more like a stylish briefcase. You can carry it on your back with two straps and it’s constructed from beautiful materials that will match all your clothes.

**Price:** $210 at [cambridgesatchel.com](https://www.cambridgesatchel.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 4. Steve Madden Canvas Backpack

![Steve Madden Canvas Backpack](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/09040812_2506_350x537.jpg)

The Steve Madden Canvas Backpack is a great combination of awesome good looks and an affordable price. It’s got pockets inside and out and comes in a variety of colors and prints.

**Price:** $48 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 5. Elizabeth and James Sling Backpack

![Elizabeth and James Sling Backpack](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/10045759_5372_522x522.jpg)

More satchel in appearance, the Elizabeth and James' Sling Backpack is a slouchy style bag that you can still strap on your back for easy carrying. It’s fully lined and has a handy drawstring closure that makes it easy to get in your bag for anything you need to grab.

**Price:** $445 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 6. Will Leather Goods Lennon Backpack

_ Will Leather Goods 'Lennon' Backpack is a pack for all your rugged outfits. It combines brown leather with a lovely green color, so you can pair it with any fashionable hiking ensemble. It’s also great for any other time you want a unique bag that every other girl isn’t carrying on her back.

**Price:** $275 at willleathergoods.com_

## 7. Herschel Supply Co. Settlement Backpack

![Herschel Supply Co. Settlement Backpack](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/09040813_5615_350x537.jpg)

This backpack is stylish, but it’s also less expensive than many of the other options out there. Available in three colors and featuring a canvas shell and pockets both inside and out, the bag is a great choice for any activity. It even fits a standard sized laptop computer.

**Price:** $54.99 at [shop.herschelsupply.com](https://herschel.com/?utm_source=CJ&utm_content=6147293&utm_medium=affiliate&PID=6147293&cjevent=53ffddfe0adb11ed8032aee60a82b836)

## 8. MCM Six Stud Stark Backpack

![MCM Six Stud Stark Backpack](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/10045800_6693_499x465.jpg)

With studs scattered over the body of the backpack and featuring a fancy logo design, the MCM Stud Stark backpack is a great choice for rocker girls who don’t want to sacrifice their edgy look just because they need to carry a bag. This awesome choice is available in a rainbow of fun colors. Good luck choosing just one!

**Price:** $620 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 9. Longchamp Le Pliage Backpack

_ This bag from Longchamp comes in four great colors, so it’s easy to find the one that works best in your wardrobe. The bag is a combination of nylon and leather, so you know it’ll hold up to all the demands you place on it.

**Price:** $125 at us.longchamp.com_

## 10. Baggu Backpack

![Baggu Backpack](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/09040813_3407_350x537.jpg)

For the price, Baggu's Backpack is a bag you can’t go wrong with! Made from canvas and available in three colors, the bag is one you’ll never regret buying. Heck, at this price, you can even get one in each color.

**Price:** $20 at [fab.com](http://fab.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 11. Poketo Striped Fleece Backpack

_ Not only is this backpack soft and cuddly, but it’s also one you won’t be embarrassed to carry. The light gray and white color of the bag makes it simple to pair with all your clothes. It’s made with lots of pockets and is spacious enough for all your necessities.

**Price:** $28 at poketo.com_

Are backpacks something you haven’t even considered since high school? You’re doing yourself a disservice by not getting one of these grown-up versions. Which one is your favorite?
