### Quick Tips for Styling Winter Clutches

- **Mix Textures:** Pair a smooth leather clutch with a chunky knit sweater for a sophisticated contrast.

- **Go Bold with Color:** Use a bright or printed clutch to break up a dark, monochromatic winter outfit.

- **Size Matters:** For evening events, stick to smaller envelope styles. For daytime, a slightly larger zippered clutch can hold more essentials.
