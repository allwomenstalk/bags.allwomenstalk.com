# 7 Furry Bags That Will Warm up Your Winter Outfits ...

Winter is the perfect time to experiment with texture and this season furry bags are the way to go. The drop in temperature has seen a raft of warm and woolly clothing and accessories hitting the shop shelves. Most notably, faux fur bags seem to be at the forefront of trends. They come in all sorts of designs and are suited to a range of occasions. The following are a few furry bags that will add some warmth to your outfits this winter.

## 1. Faux Fur Carryall Bag

![Faux Fur Carryall Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j3/vk/e6k0ujgm54c8e36425733693470869_600x534.jpg)

Incorporate furry bags into your everyday outfit with this carryall from Topshop. It’s roomy enough to hold all of your bits and bobs and is versatile enough to be worn with a variety of outfits. It has a slightly slouchy shape and features a glossy black faux fur finish. Team this furry bag with a simple outfit of a t-shirt, jeans, trench coat, and flats for a classic kind of look.

## 2. Faux Fur Roll-Top Clutch

![Faux Fur Roll-Top Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v4/ac/em4onyy454c8e3658776e734380572_600x430.jpg)

The key to making furry bags work is to stick to sleek, streamlined designs. This faux fur clutch from Topshop is a great example an understated way to work this trend into your outfit. It has a simple, roll-top design and comes in basic black. Pair it with a chunky knit sweater, skinny jeans, and sneakers for an on-trend daytime look. If you’re feeling a bit experimental, this clutch is also available in a range of candy bright colours.

## 3. Two-Tone Faux Fur Clutch

![Two-Tone Faux Fur Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rq/lj/mrlomb3f54c8e3825d7d3276365170_474x296.jpg)

Add a pop of colour to your outfit with this orange and pale blue clutch by Shrimps. The vibrant colour combination will add an element of fun to any outfit. Try being a bit cheeky with your styling and team this clutch with an equally colourful pastel coat. Otherwise, opt for a neutral colour palette to really let this accessory shine.

## 4. Faux Fur Backpack

![Faux Fur Backpack](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q1/rp/s0vohnv254c8e3647ab61471490159_600x525.jpg)

For the more practical among us, you can’t beat a classic backpack. This Topshop one, however, comes with a faux fur twist. It has a slouchy design, with a drawstring closure, fold-over flap, and two side pockets. It’s perfect for pulling off a sporty, street-inspired look.

## 5. Faux Fur Satchel

![Faux Fur Satchel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ul/sj/sf8wm9jr54c8e3644e821982038022_600x488.jpg)

This DKNY faux fur satchel has an elegant, structured design. It has faux fur detailing at the front and back, with glossy croc trims to the sides. Work a ladylike look and team this bag with a midi dress, structured coat, and a classic pair of heels.

## 6. Multicolour Faux Fur Bag

![Multicolour Faux Fur Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rb/xs/c0uxfens54c8e364a6d21838970508_600x858.jpg)

This furry bag by Monki features a blue, black and grey camouflage print. It has a sleek rectangular design and comes with a detachable strap so it can be worn on the shoulder or used as a clutch. Use the colours of the clutch as a starting point for your outfit and team it with grey basics, dark blue denim, and black accessories.

## 7. Faux Fur Blush Bag

![Faux Fur Blush Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sd/rs/ux7zzgqm54c8e365efd15056931882_600x883.jpg)

Add a Clueless-inspired touch to your outfits with this blush bag by Nasty Gal x Nila Anthony. It has a compact design and comes with a long chain strap and zip compartments to the interior. This blush furry bag would add a playful touch to a plain party dress.

These bags are a guaranteed to add some statement-making texture to your outfits this season. What do you think of this furry trend? Are you a fan of these furry looking bags?
