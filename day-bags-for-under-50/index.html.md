# 8 Day Bags for under $50 ...

**Day Bags** under $50 can be hard to come by. If you’re on a budget, stylish **day bags** do exist. Look to chain stores and the high street for your fashion fix. From messenger style satchels, to slouchy hobo bags, there are a range to suit all styles and tastes. Take a look at a few budget** day bags** below.

## 1. ASOS Traditional Colour Briefcase

![ASOS Traditional Colour Briefcase](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r9/sx/arewjta454c935c27ee46406284075_350x254.jpg)

**Price:** $49.32 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/cn8p8M)
Coloured day bags make excellent outfit additions. This bag has a briefcase design which makes it ideal for everyday use. It has a textured finish, grab top handle, and detachable shoulder strap. It also has twin push-through clasp fastenings at the front. Wear this with a striped t-shirt, jeans, and ballet flats for a simply stylish look.

## 2. Forever21 Distressed Leatherette Handbag

_ **Price:** $28.80 at forever21.com_
If you’re after a basic black bag then check out this affordable number from Forever21. This handbag has a front snap design with twin pouch compartments and plenty of room. It also has an adjustable shoulder strap on a cross body bias. This versatile bag can easily fit all your daily basics.

## 3. Out of Print Scott Tote

![Out of Print Scott Tote](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q2/mf/xhejoo5854c935c36f426148753826_188x349.jpg)

**Price:** $17.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Canvas totes make great day bags. This one from Modcloth features a print of the original cover of The Great Gatsby. Canvas totes like this are perfect for using every day or for keeping in your handbag as a shopper bag. The best thing about this bag is that for every purchase, a book is donated to a community in need.

## 4. Forever21 Buckled Leatherette Handbag

_ **Price:** $24.80 at forever21.com_
A subtly colored handbag is perfect for wearing on a daily basis. This leatherette handbag as a stylish shopper design and features a sleep handle strap as well as an adjustable shoulder strap. This handbag would look great teamed with mustard coloured pants, a cream jumper, and lace up brogues.

## 5. Modcloth Day at the River Backpack

**Price:** $23.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
For a truly casual day bag, you can’t go past this backpack. It has a delightful striped design with a drawstring closure and adjustable straps. It also has an external zipper pocket. The best thing about this bag is that it folds into its own pocket for travel or storage.

## 6. ASOS Metallic Colour Block Shopper

**Price:** $32.88 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/t9CBs1)
Large day bags are great for women who have a lot to carry. This oversized shopper bag is perfect for carrying during the day. It has a simple design and comes in a neutral black colour. The metallic strip along the bottom of the bag adds extra interest to the design. This bag is an ideal size for students wanting to carry textbooks or laptops.

## 7. Forever21 Fair Isle Handbag

_ **Price:** $27.80 at forever21.com_
Printed day bags are a great way of adding extra detail to an outfit. This bag has a barrel design and features a multi coloured knit Fair Isle pattern. It has an adjustable shoulder strap, zippered side pockets, and two top handle straps. This would look great again a tan wool cape and indigo jeans.

## 8. ASOS Zip Base Handheld Bag

![ASOS Zip Base Handheld Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ef/a5/mg6mb94454c935c62c301489494432_242x350.jpg)

**Price:** $49.32 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/HQMj0U)
