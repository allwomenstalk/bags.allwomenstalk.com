# 7 Chic Designer Wallets ...

Designer Wallets come in all sorts of shapes and sizes. Good quality designer wallets would make a great investment pieces. A classic black slimline wallet is perfect for those who prefer timeless style, whereas more contemporary designs are great for those who like edgier styles. If you want to add a bit of luxury to your day, take a look at the following designer wallets.

## 1. DKNY Textured Leather Wallet

_ **Price:** $95.00 at net-a-porter.com_
A black wallet is timeless. This DKNY wallet has a textured leather finish and has a gold lock-embellished plaque at the front. This wallet has a front flap design and features four internal compartments as well as fourteen card slots. This slimline wallet is great for those who prefer minimal designs.

## 2. Mulberry Polly Push Lock Wallet

_ **Price:** $300.00 at lagarconne.com_
Compact designer wallets are a chic choice. This Mulberry wallet comes in a light brown ‘pumpkin’ colour and has a full grain leather finish. It’s a duo-fold wallet with compartments at the front and back. This compact wallet still manages to hold a lot, with eight card slots and two bill sleeves.

## 3. Alexander Wang Prisma Compact Leather Wallet

_ **Price:** $235.00 at net-a-porter.com_
This wallet has a great compact design. It is made from black leather and features rhodium hardware. The triangle rhodium tipped edges are a contemporary design addition. This wallet holds five card slots, one bill slot, and a snap fastening coin purse. It also has an oversized zipper fastening.

## 4. Marc by Marc Jacobs Slim Leather Wallet

_ **Price:** $226.00 at matchesfashion.com_
This elegant wallet has a chic streamlined design. It has a cool face plate and hardware fastening. This zip-up wallet also has card-holders, slit pockets, and a zipped pocket. The neutral colour is a timeless choice, which is a quality you want from designer wallets.

## 5. Lanvin Happy Long Wallet

_ **Price:** $630.00 at barneys.com_
A cream wallet is a sophisticated and elegant choice. This long wallet has a smooth finish and features a turn lock closure at the front. The interior of the wallet features multiple compartments as well as card slots and a zipped coin compartment.

## 6. Michael Michael Kors Jet Set Designer Wallet

_ **Price:** $120.00 at net-a-porter.com_
This sleek wallet has a framed body. If you’re after more structure from your designer wallets, then this is a great one to check out. It has a gold frame, fourteen card slots, an ID compartment, and a clasp fastening at the top. It’s ideal for those who like to carry an array of items in their wallets.

## 7. Mulberry Postman’s Lock Continental Leather Wallet

_ **Price:** $450.00 at net-a-porter.com_
A classic Mulberry design has been given a contemporary touch in the form of a colourful leather finish. This wallet comes in a striking green colour and has a front flap closure with turn-lock fastening. It features several interior compartments as well as a zip-fastening pocket at the back.

Designer wallets are destined to set you back a pretty penny. Shop smart and keep an eye out for designer wallets on sale and in timeless designs. That way you’ll know you’ll be in fashion year in and year out. Which of these designer wallets do you like best?
