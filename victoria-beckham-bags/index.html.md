# 8 Victoria Beckham Bags ...

**Victoria Beckham Bags** are amazingly stylish. Her handbags are incredibly successful, too, especially for being part of a celebrity line. That's no surprise, considering the woman herself. Victoria Beckham bags incorporate all the sleek style of Victoria herself. They're elegant, sophisticated, and absolutely fabulous. They're simple without being boring and chic without being ostentatious. As a result, most Victoria Beckham bags are instant classics – but don't take my word for it, see for yourself!

## 1. Mini Satchel Textured-Leather Shoulder Bag

![Mini Satchel Textured-Leather Shoulder Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/02/1_mini-satchel-textured-leather-shoulder-bag_400x600.jpg)

**Price:** $1,600.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/pTa2K1)
This is one of my favorite **Victoria Beckham bags** because it will go with anything. It's small enough that it could double as an evening bag but versatile enough you can use it every day as well. From a distance it looks like a simple black bag, but the textured level and the subtle sparkle from the gold hardware combine to make it perfect.

## 2. Victoria Leather Tote

![Victoria Leather Tote](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/02/2_victoria-leather-tote_400x433.jpg)

**Price:** $3,250.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/ZqgtFn)
Although I usually don't like orange, I think it looks striking on this tote. With the black contrast, it could look like a pumpkin, but several things keep it cool. The boxy shape helps, and the shade of orange does wonders. It's a pumpkin hue, but soft, so it's almost like a darker shade of tangerine. I think it's bright and colorful, a perfect match for colors like chocolate, cream, toffee, and camel.

## 3. Victoria Small Leather Tote

![Victoria Small Leather Tote](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/02/3_victoria-small-leather-tote_400x430.jpg)

**Price:** $2,995.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/3g9Psr)
If you prefer a smaller tote, there are several **Victoria Beckham bags** from which to choose. I especially like this one, because it's simple but elegant. With a bag in this color, you can easily wear bright, vibrant colors. You can also don bold fashion choices that will mesh well with the bag's sleek, clean lines.

## 4. Oversized Textured-Leather Duffle Bag

![Oversized Textured-Leather Duffle Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/02/4_oversized-textured-leather-duffle-bag_400x427.jpg)

**Price:** $3,495.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/1EpDTK)
This duffle is just too cute for words, but I'll try to do it justice! I love everything about this bag, from the thin strap to the clasp to its overall shape. Again, the texture of the leather gives the bag an innovative look, and it's classic enough to pair well with any kind of ensemble.

## 5. Two-Tone Leather and Suede Shoulder Bag

![Two-Tone Leather and Suede Shoulder Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/02/5_two-tone-leather-and-suede-shoulder-bag_400x600.jpg)

**Price:** $3,125.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/C54IyE)
I love color contrasts, especially with handbags. This is one of my favorite **Victoria Beckham bags** for just that reason. The leather is the shade of a fine merlot, and it looks beautiful against the rich vermillion of the suede leather body. The decadent colors are perfectly complemented by the bag's sleek lines and angles. The best part is its versatility: you can detach the strap to use this as an evening bag.

## 6. Victoria Structured Leather Tote

![Victoria Structured Leather Tote](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/02/6_victoria-structured-leather-tote_400x485.jpg)

**Price:** $3,750.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/McI8iD)
I think the shape of this bag is fantastic. It's incredibly geometric, which makes it a great choice for chic, modern business outfits and more feminine ensembles. Again, you get that luscious shade of merlot, so you can add subtle color to your daily palette.

## 7. Victoria Leather-Trimmed Suede Tote

![Victoria Leather-Trimmed Suede Tote](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/02/7_victoria-leather-trimmed-suede-tote_400x435.jpg)

**Price:** $3,250.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/U4aSdW)
This is one of the most popular **Victoria Beckham bags** because of its finish. I adore it – the way the suede looks scuffed creates a really stunning texture. The taupe shade adds to the bag's versatility, and its large and roomy without being ostentatious and overbearing.

## 8. Shopper Leather Tote

![Shopper Leather Tote](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/02/8_shopper-leather-tote_400x391.jpg)

**Price:** $2,700.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/Zo5vLF)
This is a great weekend bag, but you can use it from day to day as well. This one is sleek with a smooth finish, and it, too, lends itself well to vivid, vibrant fashion choices. Red looks great with this bag, and highlight it with either gold or silver jewelry – they're both highly complementary.

**Victoria Beckham bags** are expensive, there's no doubt about that, but if you're saving up for a stylish bag that will last a long time and will go with any outfit, one of these might be your best bet. Most Victoria Beckham bags are neutral but still fashionable, which means they're easy to pair with any kind of outfit.
