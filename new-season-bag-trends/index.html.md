# 7 New Season Bag Trends ...

**Bag Trends** vary from season to season. One season it might be all about the oversized tote, while the next may see a shift in focus onto smaller silhouettes. This season, bag trends range from a new take on animal print to a focus on minimalist designs. A trend-based bag is a simple way of updating your look. Take note of some new season bag trends below.

## 1. Colourful Animal Print

Animal prints have always been a firm favourite when it comes to accessorising. This season, take it up a notch by investing in some colourful animal print pieces. Steer towards citrus shades of orange and yellow. Snakeskin and croc effect bags will be hot favourites.

## 2. Monochrome Patterns

A black and white patterned clutch is a simple yet effective way to update your look. From houndstooth to zebra print to geometric patterns, the monochrome bag can take on a range of forms. The look is all about creating a graphic effect. A monochrome clutch will look great with an evening outfit.

## 3. Frosted Finishes

Bags with frosted finishes are a fun trend to take note of. These plastic-looking bags are often see-through, but the frosted finish obscures the contents of your bag ever so slightly. Stick to fuss-free clutches and sporty utility style bags when experimenting with this **bag trend**.

## 4. Sporty Silhouettes

Sporty, utility style bags will also be on-trend this season. Bucket bags and duffel bags with slouchy silhouettes are perfect for creating a casual off-duty look. Keep an eye out for finishes such as exposed zippers and drawstrings.

## 5. Block Colours

Brightly, block colours are another big **bag trend** for this season. A brightly coloured bag is a simple way to add a block of colour to an outfit. Primary colours such as red, yellow and blue are all on-trend choices. Because block colours can be quite busy, choose a bag with a fairly simple design.

## 6. Fuss-Free Totes

**Bag trends** can be quite dated, so keep it timeless with a fuss-free tote. A minimalist tote bag with a streamlined design would make the ideal everyday bag this season. A neutral coloured bag in a smooth square or rectangular design is a versatile option and can be easily incorporated into a range of outfits.

## 7. Laser Cut Outs

This is one of the more modern and directional **bag trends** around. Laser cut out clutches were initially made popular by contemporary designer Christopher Kane. The look has now started filtering down into high street stores. A neon laser-cut clutch is a fun addition to any outfit, and would look great worn with crisp whites or pale neutrals.

There will be a focus on minimal designs and bright colours when it comes to **bag trends** this season. When looking to incorporate these bag trends into your wardrobe, look to stores such as Zara and ASOS.com for affordable options. What do you think of these new season bag trends?
