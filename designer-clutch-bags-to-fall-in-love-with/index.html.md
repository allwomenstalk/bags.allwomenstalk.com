# 9 Designer Clutch Bags to Fall in Love with ...

Designer Clutch Bags make great statement pieces. The right clutch will set off any outfit, day or night. As these designer clutch bags come with the designer price tags to match, they definitely come under the category of investment pieces. Take a look at a few lovely designer clutch bags below.

## 1. Yves Saint Laurent Gold Shimmer Clutch

_ **Price:** $705.00 at mytheresa.com_
This stunning gold clutch is a real attention grabber. It is made from leather and has a metallic finish. It has a fold-over flap with a gold-tone signature logo closure. The interior features dark brown satin lining and a single slot pocket. Team this with a black cocktail dress and strappy stiletto heels for a fuss-free evening look.

## 2. Anya Hindmarch Valorie Glitter Finish Clutch

![Anya Hindmarch Valorie Glitter Finish Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yq/rb/ri29c8mr54c92ef015ca2736343109_300x229.jpg)

**Price:** $395.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/8p25Mv)
Designers seem to be having a love affair with glitter at the moment. This glitter finish clutch would make a fun and flirty addition to any outfit. It has a multicoloured glitter finish and light gold hardware. Team it with a block coloured dress for a simple party outfit.

## 3. Miu Miu Matelasse Leather Clutch

![Miu Miu Matelasse Leather Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n3/ru/nci0phz454c92ef100907568523992_300x216.jpg)

**Price:** $470.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/bGf9Nv)
A black clutch is a must in any capsule wardrobe. Designer clutch bags such as this Miu Miu bag are ideal investment pieces. It is made from black matelasse leather and features a designer stamped plaque at the front. Carry it during the day to transport your essentials, or team it with a glamorous outfit at night.

## 4. Marc Jacobs Large Eugenie Clutch

_ **Price:** $459.00 at mytheresa.com_
Indulge your feminine side with this sweet pastel pink clutch. It is made from quilted rosewood-toned leather and has a fold-over flap front. Go for a smart casual look and pair this clutch with tailored horts, a tank top, and white blazer.

## 5. Marni Gusset Clutch

_ **Price:** $1540.00 at barneys.com_
This framed clutch would make an elegant addition to any bag collection. It comes in a soft pink leather and features a gold-tone frame with metal push-lock closure. Team designer clutch bags such as this with a white floaty dress for a fresh summer look.

## 6. Chloe Large Leather Wristlet Bag

_ **Price:** $895.00 at mytheresa.com_
Larger designer clutch bags are great for carrying during the day. This leather clutch bag is comparable to a medium sized handbag and could easily fit your day to day essentials. It is made from white leather and has a dual zip-around closure. It also has a wristlet attachment at one end.

## 7. Burberry Metallic Leather Wristlet Clutch

![Burberry Metallic Leather Wristlet Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eu/jw/t2trh21x54c92ef42c855177816029_300x218.jpg)

**Price:** $650.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/jvMi17)
This small clutch bag is perfect for a night out on the town. It is made from gold leather and has a quilted finish. It has two compartments and a chain wristlet strap. Work a contemporary look and team this clutch with an oversized top, leather look leggings, and wedge ankle boots.

## 8. Coach Chelsea Patent Clutch

![Coach Chelsea Patent Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p5/o5/lrefk7h254c92ef50a0dc811510492_300x172.jpg)

## 9. Matthew Williamson Sequinned Suede Clutch

![Matthew Williamson Sequinned Suede Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kq/v3/vyum1bnf54c92ef5cf881877442078_300x200.jpg)
