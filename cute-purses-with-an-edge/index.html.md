# 9 Cute Purses with an Edge ...

I like cute purses with an edge, you know? I want something a little funky, a little out of the ordinary, a little one of a kind. Who wants to carry a handbag that everyone else has, too? I’m always on the lookout for bags that look different from any others I’ve ever seen. To that end, I think I’ve come up with some pretty cute purses with an edge for you to check out today!

## 1. Mar Y Sol “Guadalupe” Straw Shopper

![Mar Y Sol “Guadalupe” Straw Shopper](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ow/yx/b0s389jk54c9295c33cd3263670285_300x325.jpg)

**Price:** $88.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This is definitely a cute purse with an edge, primarily because it’s a different sort of edgy than you typically expect. I love a number of things about this bag, from the handle to that pretty flower on the front, which blooms in dazzling 3D style. It also shows off the weaving technique traditional in Madagascar!

## 2. Ted Baker Tennison Dragonfly Bag

![Ted Baker Tennison Dragonfly Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nt/ex/xvfpzvga54c9295d10163195988845_300x193.jpg)

**Price:** $170.67 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/HByvdD)
There is absolutely nothing I don’t love about this handbag. It’s the best definition I’ve found of how a bag can be both cute and edgy. Actually, it’s rather drop dead gorgeous. It’s a hard case, and somehow, the firm, oblong shape, which makes everything a little sharp and angular, is a perfect complement to the feminine beauty of the dragonfly print. It features appliques, not to mention touches of jersey fabric among the rest of the embellishments, so even the construction is edgy!

## 3. Dandy Candy Shoulder Bag

![Dandy Candy Shoulder Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w4/uj/yva4v8fv54c9295dd5913492900746_300x203.jpg)

**Price:** $42.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Nobody does cute and edgy better than Mod Cloth. Everything about this bag is original. I even love Mod Cloth’s description of it – and it happens to be true! This is more than a cute purse with an edge, it’s absolutely delicious! Chocolate, candy apples, strawberries – I can see all those scrumptious treats in the design of this wonderfully vintage bag! And that chain link strap is just the icing on the perfect concoction.

## 4. Wayuu Taya Mochilla Knitted Cotton Cross-Body Bag

**Price:** $175.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/JmBRd9)
I really love the colors in this bag, as well as the pattern. It’s literally edgy, full of angles and sharp edges, thanks to those gorgeous, stark, hand knitted geometric shapes. The tassels are too cute, and lends a softness to the bag – as does that bright shade of pink!

## 5. Trouve Slouchy Faux Leather Hobo

![Trouve Slouchy Faux Leather Hobo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g2/lj/wr6arkz154c9295f9b058176540810_300x309.jpg)

**Price:** $98.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I like this purse for several reasons. For one thing, I’m generally a fan of hobo bags. I love the shape, and I love the extra slouch to this one. For me, the ruffle combined with this particular color really works. However, you can get it in other shades, as well!

## 6. Paul’s Boutique Betty Black Skull Shopper

![Paul’s Boutique Betty Black Skull Shopper](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wk/l0/xewxntm354c92960889f7481832516_300x308.jpg)

**Price:** $70.68 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/Ps9a6G)
Paul’s boutique produces a lot of cute purses with an edge, but this one is definitely among my favorites. Skulls just rock. I just got some beach towels with skulls all over them, and I feel like this would make the perfect, matching beach bag!

## 7. Shell We? Shoulder Bag

![Shell We? Shoulder Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ut/d6/umrf9rat54c929615f945384725550_300x237.jpg)

**Price:** $74.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Speaking of beach bags, how appropriate is this? This is just too cute for words! It’s evocative of the beach, for that matter. It’s not just the seashell tape, it’s the gold color as well – so bright!

## 8. Fossil “Key-per” Printed Coated Canvas Shopper

![Fossil “Key-per” Printed Coated Canvas Shopper](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yc/wp/zogni8xo54c9296256f91922487753_300x444.jpg)

**Price:** $68.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Fossil has a lot of cute purses with an edge, too, and this is the one I like best. You can get it in black or aqua, and honestly, I like both. I like the bright color of the aqua, and the pattern of the print. The black, however, has a key in a contrasting color – the black and white combined with a kind of dark magenta is just gorgeous!

## 9. Double Handled Ruffled Hobo Bag

![Double Handled Ruffled Hobo Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s8/cf/fing4c8i54c929636a193606101978_300x288.jpg)

**Price:** $29.99 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This bag is absolutely adorable. It’s also like nothing I’ve ever seen before, so it definitely fits the bill for being cute and edgy. It looks like a huge, exotic blossom. Plus, there’s something so sensual about the way the ruffles curve!

I hate to say it, because the Better Half is already running out of closet space, but I think it’s time to go shopping for some handbags. I can safely say I’d love to own every single one of these cute purses with an edge. What about you, do you prefer funky handbags or simple, elegant ones?
