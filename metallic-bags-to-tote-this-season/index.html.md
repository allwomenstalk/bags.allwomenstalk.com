# 8 Metallic Bags to Tote This Season ...

Shine on with the lustrous metallic bags to tote this season. Metallics glimmer in the best molten hot shades like gold, silver, bronze and pewter to create brilliant bag beauties that keep you radiating style all season long. From liquid luster and gleaming gloss finishes to sparkling crackle, these are the metallic bags to tote this season.

## 1. Metallic Tote Bag

![Metallic Tote Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/11/1_metallic-tote-bag_554x447.jpg)

The metallic tote bag is the perfect carryall option to tote around this season for when you need to keep all your unmentionables in tow. For an ultra-luxe option select a metallic tote bag in a burnished bronze to keep your style cool and sophisticated instead of loud or over the top. Whether it’s running weekend errands or rushing off to meetings, a metallic tote is the go-to bag to grab at a moment’s notice. The ideal never full option from Reed Krakoff above will set you back $1,290, but other metallic bag styles are found in high fashion designs from Michael Kors, Emilio Pucci and Rafe New York. More affordable options can be seen at Dorothy Perkins, BCBGMaxAzria, ASOS and Cambridge Satchel Company. A good quality metallic bag from any of these designers or retailers can cost anywhere from $30 to $700 for styles that suit the budget babe and runway ready fashionista.

## 2. Metallic Shoulder Bag

![Metallic Shoulder Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/11/2_metallic-shoulder-bag_600x481.jpg)

Sling a small and simple shoulder bag over your arm this season for big impact in a small statement. Mini metallic shoulder bags are practical and versatile style choices for all lifestyles. Adjust the straps on a metallic shoulder bag to create a cross body look when movement and comfort is of importance, or throw over your shoulder for easy ladylike sophistication.

## 3. Metallic Clutch Bag

![Metallic Clutch Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/11/3_metallic-clutch-bag_593x390.jpg)

For a small showstopper, choose a metallic clutch bag in gleaming gold. Keep your look sleek and streamlined by selecting a metallic clutch bag with minimal detail and clean lines. A high liquid gloss finish and brilliant shine will be sure to turn heads everywhere you go.

## 4. Metallic Box Bag

![Metallic Box Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/11/4_metallic-box-bag_600x502.jpg)

Bags take form in all shapes and sizes this season, but no style is favored by the fashionista more than the box bag. A box bag in a metallic finish adds sparkle and dazzling dimension to this geometric shape. Although on the small side, a metallic box bag packs a powerful punch during day or night.

## 5. Metallic Satchel Bag

![Metallic Satchel Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/11/5_metallic-satchel-bag_254x488.jpg)

For everyday use a satchel bag is the perfect go to option for the woman on the move. Satchel bags are casual enough for everyday wear yet still chic and fashionable in an effortless sort of way. To update your satchel for high fashion drama, opt for a cool, metallic silver to give that edge to your practical and functional style.

## 6. Metallic Hobo Bag

![Metallic Hobo Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/11/6_metallic-hobo-bag_320x400.jpg)

To maintain your urban, downtown aesthetic sport a metallic hobo bag this season. Named after its slouchy and relaxed shape, a hobo bag screams city cool with a touch of bohemian flair. A brilliant metallic finish makes this cool and casual bag chic and easy to wear throughout the season.

## 7. Metallic Evening Bag

![Metallic Evening Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/11/7_metallic-evening-bag_500x289.jpg)

Dazzling metallic bags were made for evening wear. To add a high polished shine to your nighttime outfit add a metallic evening bag into the mix. Whether it’s a night out on the town or Holiday soiree, a metallic evening clutch is the perfect finishing touch for superb style elegance.

## 8. Metallic Bowler Bag

![Metallic Bowler Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/11/8_metallic-bowler-bag_568x755.jpg)

New and reinvented shapes were introduced this season and nothing received a modern makeover quite like the bowler bag. It’s hip to be square for fall as boxy shapes lead the bag pack. Follow fall’s bag directive by opting for a modern bowler bag in a luminous and glossy metallic like pewter for a subdued shine this season.

For when a little shine goes a long way, keep these metallic bags in tow this season and create super sheen style. Whether it’s traditional heavy metal in gold or silver, or modern metallics in pewter and bronze, stick to lustrous finishes for a brilliant bag update this season. What’s your favorite way to wear metallic bags this season? Will you be toting around a metallic bag for fall?
