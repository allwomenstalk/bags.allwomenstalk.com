# 10 Gorgeous Satchels for School ...

As the holidays come close to an end, it’s time to start thinking about the new semester, and among the things you're likely to need is a list of the prettiest and most functional satchels for school. You have your textbooks, your notebooks, your pens, and then your ratty old backpack - the one that’s tearing at the seams and covered in ink. Why not trade it in for a glamorous new satchel and start the new semester in style? Here are some adorable and practical satchels for school.

## 1. Clean ID Satchel

![Clean ID Satchel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2013/01/1_clean-id-satchel_600x498.jpg)

This beautiful forest green satchel will put the spotlight on you as you stroll through campus. Like its name says, this bag is cleanly cut and looking fresh. It shows your peers that you’re stylish without being too over the top, as the solid color doesn’t make it too ‘busy.’ Pair it with a sheer blouse and a nice skirt for an easy schoolgirl chic look. This is definitely one of the most ideal satchels for school.

## 2. ANTONIA SATCHEL by PAUL'S BOUTIQUE

![ANTONIA SATCHEL by PAUL'S BOUTIQUE](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2013/01/2_antonia-satchel-by-pauls-boutique_600x584.jpg)

This one is a bit more glamorous, and probably more fit as a purse for days when you don’t have classes. The nice addition of the royal blue make this satchel different from many of the generic satchels you see around the mall. You could try to stuff some of your smaller notebooks into this one, but why not pair it with a nice contrasting pea coat and some boots for a great day out in town.

## 3. Leather School Messenger Bag

![Leather School Messenger Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2013/01/3_leather-school-messenger-bag_600x450.jpg)

This is certainly the ideal satchel for school if you’re used to a more rugged knapsack that can hold all your things. You can fling this one around your shoulder, throw it on the ground, and it’ll only add to its weathered, rustic look. Pair it with some combat boots and skinnies or tights and a colorful skirt because with this bag, your outfit really can’t go wrong.

## 4. Double Buckle Retro Satchel

![Double Buckle Retro Satchel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2013/01/4_double-buckle-retro-satchel_400x294.jpg)

This satchel for school is similar to the previous messenger bag in the sense that it looks a bit more weathered than a bright and shiny one. The double buckle, however, gives this bag attitude and flair. Big enough to fit a laptop, you can easily carry everything you’ll need for school. Try for a ‘private boarding school’ look with a nice wool coat, knee-high socks and patent leather shoes.

## 5. Canvas Satchel

![Canvas Satchel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2013/01/5_canvas-satchel_600x369.jpg)

This satchel is certainly for the techy-savvy student in you. Looking a lot like a laptop bag, the double buckles on this canvas satchel says ‘I like technology but I’m also chic and stylish.’ This too is large enough to store your laptop, and maybe a tablet and a smartphone. There’s no reason you can’t be glamorous and versatile as you make your way to campus.

## 6. Smart Retro Satchel

![Smart Retro Satchel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2013/01/6_smart-retro-satchel_600x437.jpg)

This satchel is sleek- a perfect fit for those that love simplicity and originality. There’s no need for all those belts and buckles when this large practical satchel easily knocks them out of the park. The wide dimensions of this one makes it perfect for students with lots of textbooks/notebooks, and adjustable straps makes it easy on your shoulders. Wear pops of color to contrast the solid wood color of this gorgeous satchel.

## 7. Clean Seam Satchel

![Clean Seam Satchel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2013/01/3513043050-373x560_373x560.jpg)

This satchel for school just screams fashionable. People know you’re a fashionista as you skip around class with this bag around your shoulders. A little too small for notebooks or a laptop, use this satchel on days where all you need is a pen and a keen ear. Why go to school empty-handed when you can walk into class with this gem? Pair with darker neutrals to make this gorgeous satchel pop.

## 8. Satchel with Aztec Panel

![Satchel with Aztec Panel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2013/01/8_satchel-with-aztec-panel_600x428.jpg)

Geometric and Aztec prints will probably never go out of fashion, so why not get your hands on this timeless piece with the Aztec panel. This gorgeous satchel for school is the perfect blend of style and versatility. The large size makes it pragmatic, and the Aztec panel shows your peers you’re not afraid to mix fashion with education. Avoid wearing more patterns or you may risk looking too matchy-matchy, or worse: too busy.

## 9. Contrast Smart Retro Satchel

![Contrast Smart Retro Satchel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2013/01/9_contrast-smart-retro-satchel_600x545.jpg)

This satchel is a classic. There are no bells and whistles, just two simple panels of black and leather. This is for the modest student in you- one that wants to look nicely put together as you walk to class. A classic like this one can be paired with almost anything whether you’re going for the preppy schoolgirl, a retro/vintage feel or even a cute modern look.

## 10. Cambridge Satchel Company 13" Leather Satchel

![Cambridge Satchel Company 13" Leather Satchel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2013/01/10_cambridge-satchel-company-13-leather-satchel_600x381.jpg)

No satchel list is complete without a gorgeous one from the Cambridge Satchel Company. You can almost never go wrong with these magnificent, handmade creations. Skip the neon ones, and go straight for a bright solid color like this teal piece. With clean cut lines like these, it’s certainly worth the splurge. Make sure to dress in darker colors, or light neutrals to make the teal stand out.

As the new semester approaches, you deserve to treat yourself to a nice satchel. Which one of these gorgeous satchels for school best suits you? Which one are you going to show up to class with?
