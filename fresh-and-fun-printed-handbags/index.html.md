# 8 Fresh and Fun Printed Handbags ...

**Printed handbags** are a fun way to update your look. From fresh florals to quirky geometric patterns, the printed handbag is a must have addition for your spring-summer wardrobe. It’s a simple and effective way to get in on the bold print trend without having to commit to an entire wardrobe full of printed clothing. Take some inspiration from the following print handbags.

## 1. Topshop Tropical Print Holdall

![Topshop Tropical Print Holdall](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/04/1_topshop-tropical-print-holdall_400x460.jpg)

**Price:** $92.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/z1tdld)
Fun tropical prints are all the rage at the moment, it seems. This handbag features a colourful tropical print but is anchored with black trims. It’s a roomier style of bag, with a zip top closure, grab handles, and a detachable long shoulder strap. **This printed handbag** is perfect for those days where you need some extra room.

## 2. ASOS Bowler Bag in Anchor Print

![ASOS Bowler Bag in Anchor Print](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/04/2_asos-bowler-bag-in-anchor-print_400x404.jpg)

**Price:** $39.39 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/n1gVkz).
This handbag has a super cute anchor print on it. It’s made from a strong cotton fabric and comes in a cool cream and orange colourway. This bag would look great worn with a Peter Pan collared blouse and dark denim jeans.

## 3. Milly Printed Canvas Tote

![Milly Printed Canvas Tote](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/04/3_milly-printed-canvas-tote_372x560.jpg)

**Price:** $165.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/fEHCuG)
This tote handbag features a cool abstract botanical print. This simple printed handbag comes with a detachable pouch, grosgrain top handles, and an internal patch pocket. It’d be a great back for teaming with a casual weekend outfit.

## 4. Michael Michael Kors Cotton-Canvas Tote

![Michael Michael Kors Cotton-Canvas Tote](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/04/4_michael-michael-kors-cotton-canvas-tote_346x560.jpg)

**Price:** $80.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/1xJ8ZO)
This bold red bag is quite quirky. It features a print in the style of more expensive designer handbags, but is made from a much more affordable cotton-canvas. Use it as your every day bag or as a backup tote bag.

## 5. River Island Aztec Print Shopper

![River Island Aztec Print Shopper](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/04/5_river-island-aztec-print-shopper_400x448.jpg)

**Price:** $44.76 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/klhz61)
Still not sick of the tribal/Aztec trend? **Then this printed handbag** is just for you. It features an eye catching lime Aztec inspired print and comes in a functional shopper design. The roomier size makes it the perfect bag for taking to school or the beach.

## 6. ASOS Print Weave Clutch

![ASOS Print Weave Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/04/6_asos-print-weave-clutch_400x275.jpg)

**Price:** $31.33 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/70RUKG).
If you prefer clutches, then there are still plenty of printed options available. This woven clutch bag is an excellent choice for summer and comes in a cute floral print. This clutch has an oversized design (so very ‘in’ right now) and features envelope styling. It’d look cute teamed with a contrasting sundress.

## 7. Kate Sheridan Printed Handbag

![Kate Sheridan Printed Handbag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/04/7_kate-sheridan-printed-handbag_330x320.jpg)

**Price:** $89.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This **printed handbag comes in a** unique circular design. If also features a stylish geometric print. When empty it sits more slouchy and when full it keeps its circular shape. It’d look great worn with denim shorts and a t-shirt for a fun, off-duty look.

## 8. Marni Printed PVC Tote

![Marni Printed PVC Tote](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/04/8_marni-printed-pvc-tote_324x560.jpg)

**Price:** $635.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/NQpgu7)
Marni are well known for their graphic prints. They also seem to do printed handbags quite well. One side of this bag features a bright green and pink pattern while the other side features a contrasting circular pattern. Team it with a basic white or black dress and you’re good to go.

**There’s a printed handbag** to suit all tastes and budgets. If you’re worried about this being a fleeting trend, then there are plenty of affordable options to see you through the season. What do you think of these printed handbags?
