The Best New Designer Handbags You'll Want to Spend Money on This Summer ...

Rebecca Minkoff Midnighter Top Handle Feed Bag • Alice + Olivia Drew Watermelon Box Bag • Dolce&amp;Gabbana Miss Sicily Pineapple Leather Satchel • Elizabeth and James Eloise Mini Shoulder Bag • Henri Bendel Crosby Small Zip Tote Bag • More ...

By Eliza

Designer bags for summer are a must-have for any girl who wants to bring her hot weather wardrobe up to date. The perfect bag is roomy enough for all of your essentials, but trendy enough to go with your entire closet. Fortunately, BestProducts.com, has helped us compile a list of the best designer bags for summer. Whether you're heading to the office, strolling through the park or having lunch with friends, there's definitely a stylish designer bag on this list that suits your tastes and your needs. So, if perfect designer bags for summer top your list right now, keep reading for everything you've ever wanted.

1. Rebecca Minkoff Midnighter Top Handle Feed Bag

$295 BUY NOW

Studs up the cool factor on this crossbody bag. The hands-free design is already ready for days on the move, and a magnetic closure creates easy access. It also comes in colors like white and tan.

2. Alice + Olivia Drew Watermelon Box Bag

$395 BUY NOW

How picnic-perfect is this watermelon bag? The framed silhouette has a bit of a vintage look, and is embellished with black crystal "seeds." Be prepared to get stopped everywhere you go.

3. Dolce&Gabbana Miss Sicily Pineapple Leather Satchel

$2,495 BUY NOW

Dolce & Gabbana's signature Miss Sicily bag goes tropical for summer. The pineapple print makes every day feel like vacation, and it'll be the best companion to all your summer parties.

4. Elizabeth and James Eloise Mini Shoulder Bag

$395 BUY NOW

Bring an exotic element to your outfits with this bitty crossbody. It's made from crocodile-embossed leather, and the ladylike shape has an Hermés Kelly bag vibe.

5. Henri Bendel Crosby Small Zip Tote Bag

$328 BUY NOW

Spring for green! This seafoam color is a surefire way to wake up your wardrobe. The zipped tote silhouette makes a great choice for daytime, and multiple pockets can store handfuls of lip gloss.

6. Givenchy Mini Horizon Grained Leather Tote

$1,990 BUY NOW

Givenchy shrunk their Horizon bag down to mini size for 2017. The dreamy orange color still makes a big impact, and it's now more mobile with a crossbody design.

7. Loeffler Randall Pompom Crossbody Bag

$295 BUY NOW

We can't get enough of Loeffler Randall's handbags this season. This one is an easy favorite with a half-moon shape and pompom-dotted strap. What more could a girl want?! The clutch version comes in at a close second.

8. Kate Spade New York Hayes Street Small Isobel Satchel

$328 BUY NOW

Add a splash of color with this coral handbag by Kate Spade. The structured shape is perfect for work or play, and a bow brings a sweet touch to the front. It also comes in black and cognac leather if you need something more classic.

9. Rebecca Minkoff Sofia Top Handle Bucket Bag

$245 BUY NOW

We're predicting pale pink as the it color this summer. This bucket bag does it in the loveliest shade, and is sized right for taking everywhere you go. A top handle and shoulder strap offer two ways to carry.

10. Alexander Wang Roxy Mini Studded Bucket Bag

$595 neimanmarcus.com

The Roxy bags ruled the runway at Alexander Wang's Spring 2017 show. This mini bucket bag makes a more interesting replacement for your black clutch, and keeps practicality in mind with an interior card case.

11. Milly Rainbow Half Moon Clutch

$295 BUY NOW

The world really is all rainbows and butterflies with this clutch bag in hand. It'll take any dress from standard to standout, and there's even an optional shoulder strap tucked inside.

12. Prada Saffiano Garden Small Double-Zip Tote

$3,080 BUY NOW

This Prada bag is the ultimate splurge for summer. Their popular double-zip tote is blooming with flower appliqués (that are insanely detailed BTW), and might be the most beautiful bag we've ever seen. Opt for the black version to get year-round use.
