# 17 Pocketbook Essentials to Keep in Your Bag All Throughout the Season ...

Don't fill your bag up with useless items you're never going to use. Stick stuff in there that's actually going to come in handy. That way, you'll always be prepared in any given situation. Here are a few pocketbook essentials you should keep on you at all times:

## 1. Hand Sanitizer

![Hand Sanitizer](https://img.allw.mn/content/mm/ys/um0jcuw9563a0d7c2de1d685644058_500x282.gif)

If you don't have time to wash your hands before a meal, you can at least use hand sanitizer. The miniature bottles from Bath and Body Works are easy to fit in the side pocket of your bag, so they won't take up much space.

## 2. Umbrella

![Umbrella](https://img.allw.mn/content/o2/ri/uy3ktd6u563a0d7ca5229519359234_500x220.gif)

Buy a little, fold-up umbrella to carry around with you. You won't always have a hood on your jacket, and you don't want the rain to ruin your gorgeous hair.

## 3. Hand Cream

![Hand Cream](https://img.allw.mn/content/sh/eh/icaeeed9563a0d7cd6c8c527303967_400x318.gif)

Now that it's getting colder, your hands will be prone to cracking. If you want soft skin, you need lotion.

## 4. Lip Balm

![Lip Balm](https://img.allw.mn/content/ln/b6/peey3k67563a0d7e9db62675356002_500x281.gif)

Nobody likes flaky lips. That's why you need some Chapstick with you 24/7, just in case.

## 5. ID

![ID](https://img.allw.mn/content/nj/n6/l5xjrzfa563a0d7d41553997992416_500x273.gif)

It doesn't matter if you're driving your car or walking to work. You need your license on you.

## 6. Blotting Sheets

![Blotting Sheets](https://img.allw.mn/content/r0/q1/ybum0lc0563a0d7d675b8526045445_500x273.gif)

If your skin looks a little iffy, you can use these to freshen up. It's a quick fix.

## 7. Perfume

![Perfume](https://img.allw.mn/content/i8/bj/wskrb5yi563a0d7e263ed377904654_500x281.gif)

If you're not a fan of perfume, at least carry around a faint body spray. That way, you'll smell delicious all day.

## 8. Mirror

![Mirror](https://img.allw.mn/content/cy/fp/g49tnewj563a0d80108c5977094270_700x394.gif)

You should check your mirror every once in a while to make sure that there isn't any food stuck in your teeth. You don't want to embarrass yourself.

## 9. Ballet Flats

![Ballet Flats](https://img.allw.mn/content/xv/gi/u8pww73v563a0d80d911d191990076_700x467.gif)

If you're wearing heels, or any other uncomfortable shoes, keep some ballet flats in your bag. Then you can change into them on your way home when your feet are killing you.

## 10. Miniature Deodorant

![Miniature Deodorant](https://img.allw.mn/content/m1/gg/hx2cymst563a0d7f3bc1b445099510_606x512.gif)

If your pits get sweaty, you'll need deodorant to save the day. The product comes in a miniature version, so you can easily slip it into your purse.

## 11. Cash

![Cash](https://img.allw.mn/content/o4/rq/ff50uej0563a0d7f5deff018435259_500x282.gif)

You should never leave the house without money. You might need it.

## 12. Phone

![Phone](https://img.allw.mn/content/gk/n7/tpmlwulw563a0d80b4b63299248628_500x279.gif)

You never leave the house without your phone, which is why you should keep it safe in your pocketbook. Just make sure it stays on silent when you're in an important meeting.

## 13. Small Notebook

![Small Notebook](https://img.allw.mn/content/yw/in/arzwstv5563a0d80512ac310357964_500x378.gif)

If you're the artsy type, you can jot down all of your ideas in a little notebook. Then you'll never forget about the inspiration that struck you in the middle of the day.

## 14. Snack

![Snack](https://img.allw.mn/content/ss/wd/jgkdcby6563a0d808af60857534132_338x232.gif)

You won't always have time for a lunch break on a busy day. That's why you should keep a granola bar on you at all times, so you don't end up starving yourself.

## 15. Band Aids

![Band Aids](https://img.allw.mn/content/m4/o1/gktpdp2j563a0d8153509788118887_500x282.gif)

If you get hurt, you don't want your wound exposed to the world. You need a bandage to keep it safe.

## 16. Bobby Pins

![Bobby Pins](https://img.allw.mn/content/xh/nx/pgn3wifk563a0d82ead26585130794_720x480.gif)

If your hair falls out of place throughout the day, you don't have to redo the entire style. Just stick some bobby pins in it to fix it.

## 17. Tissues

![Tissues](https://img.allw.mn/content/im/kp/ytv745qk563a0d839bad6882579503_400x226.gif)

Maybe you'll have to blow your nose. Maybe you'll have to wipe off some of your makeup. Maybe you'll have to kill a bug.

If you keep these things in your bag, you'll never have to worry again. What do you always keep in your pocketbook, no matter what?
