# 8 Cool Cobalt Blue Fashion Must\-Haves of 2012 ...

Cobalt blue fashion 2012 – this colour is one of the must-haves this year. Pastels and neons are still going strong but bold block colours like cobalt blue are having a trending moment. This shade of blue is perfect for making a statement and will have you standing out from the crowd, without having to resort to acid bright neons. Take a look at a few cool cobalt blue fashions for 2012, below.

## 1. Topshop Pocket Shift Dress

![Topshop Pocket Shift Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/05/1_topshop-pocket-shift-dress_400x599.jpg)

**Price:** $80.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/X7kHm1)
A simple shift dress is a great choice for any wardrobe. It can be styled up or down, depending on the occasion. This one from Topshop is ideal if you want to take part in the cobalt blue fashion 2012 trend. It has a flattering v-neckline and two front patch pockets. Style it up with a crisp white blazer and black pumps for a polished daytime look.

## 2. Carven Suede Slingbacks

![Carven Suede Slingbacks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/05/2_carven-suede-slingbacks_400x351.jpg)

**Price:** $595.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/O08SLb)
These Carven slingback shoes are quickly become a must-have piece for 2012. They have a feminine design with large bow detail at the back. They’re available in a range of colours and cobalt blue is just one super stylish option. These shoes would look great with anything from a neutral outfit to a colour blocked ensemble.

## 3. Topshop Skinny Patent Belt

![Topshop Skinny Patent Belt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/05/3_topshop-skinny-patent-belt_400x181.jpg)

**Price:** $24.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/HFnRqC)
If you only want to add a hint of colour to your look then accessories are your best bet. This skinny patent belt is one way to subtly incorporate the cobalt blue fashion 2012 trend into your wardrobes. Use it to cinch in a monochrome dress or loop it into the waistband of some pale coloured pants.

## 4. Kain Washed-Silk Tank

![Kain Washed-Silk Tank](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/05/4_kain-washed-silk-tank_400x600.jpg)

**Price:** $155.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/e8ykfb)
A basic tank top is a must-have for any capsule wardrobe, and this one just happens to come in an on-trend cobalt blue colour. It’s quite a luxe option, being made from washed-silk. Work a contemporary look by teaming it with printed pants, strappy heels, and a patent clutch.

## 5. Alexander Wang Prisma Leather Tote

![Alexander Wang Prisma Leather Tote](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/05/5_alexander-wang-prisma-leather-tote_400x600.jpg)

**Price:** $685.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/1lVWOQ)
The Prisma tote by Alexander Wang is fast becoming one of those must-have items for 2012. It comes in a roomy design and features the designers’ signature metal detailing at the corners. Statement bags like this cobalt blue fashion for 2012 are a simple way to update an outfit.

## 6. Chinti and Parker Contrast-Elbow Cashmere Sweater

![Chinti and Parker Contrast-Elbow Cashmere Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/05/6_chinti-and-parker-contrast-elbow-cashmere-sweater_400x510.jpg)

**Price:** $430.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/5LNpQr)
Elbow patches are a quirky detail that you might notice on more and more sweaters this year. This sweater comes in a brilliant blue colour and features grey patches at the elbow. At over $400 it is a lot to drop on one item of clothing so keep an eye out for more affordable alternatives being stocked in high street stores.

## 7. ASOS Contrast Buckle Clutch

**Price:** $33.00 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/bqhjGg)
Coloured clutch bags are a trend that’s enjoying popularity this year. If you’re on board for cobalt blue fashion for 2012, then this clutch is a stylish and affordable option. It has an envelope design and features a contrasting buckle strap at the centre. Team it with a geometric printed dress for a fun look.

## 8. Moschino Cheap and Chic Dress

![Moschino Cheap and Chic Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/05/8_moschino-cheap-and-chic-dress_400x600.jpg)

**Price:** $695.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/MhIfXZ)
Cut a stylish figure in this fitted cobalt blue dress. The cut is simple and timeless, making this dress a worthy investment piece. Dress it up with jewels for a cool cocktail look, or pare it down with a blazer and ballet flats for a polished daytime look.

The cobalt blue fashion 2012 trend is one to definitely try out this year. It’s a bit more timeless than other colour trends and can have more longevity in your wardrobe. What do you think of these cobalt blue fashions for 2012?
