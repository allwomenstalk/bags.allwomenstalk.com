# 7 Classic Envelope Clutches You Should Own ...

The mail is finally in this season and the trend delivery says these are the classic envelope clutches you should own. The envelope clutch is a great investment handbag with its classic silhouette, minimal shape and clean lines. An envelope clutch’s structured frame and geometrical design offers a versatile and sleek option for handheld bags you can keep in tow all year round. It’s time to check your mailboxes and see what’s in store with the classic envelope clutches you should own right now.

## 1. Leather Envelope Clutch

![Leather Envelope Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/11/1_leather-envelope-clutch_600x419.jpg)

A classic leather envelope clutch is the perfect go-to bag for all occasions. When you are looking for a quick and easy bag to tote around all day, a neutral toned leather envelope clutch is a great everyday option for when you need something lighter and smaller. For a hands-free option tuck a leather envelope clutch effortlessly under you arm for sophisticated ease and elegance.There are envelope clutch bag choices from ASOS like above priced at $28 or other styles in the $12 to $900 range from H&M, J.Crew, Aldo, Intermix, BCBGMaxAzria and ModCloth.

## 2. Colorblock Envelope Clutch

![Colorblock Envelope Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/11/2_colorblock-envelope-clutch_512x306.jpg)

Trendy colorblock seems to be going nowhere fast so it’s time to invest in a colorblock envelope clutch. To maximize this trend's longevity opt for an envelope clutch in a timeless color combination like black and white for a sleek and minimal style that’s still chic and on-trend. Accessorize any look with a colorblock envelope bag for when a handheld colorful option seems just right.

## 3. Metallic Envelope Clutch

![Metallic Envelope Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/11/3_metallic-envelope-clutch_597x471.jpg)

Metallics are scorching hot this season with their liquid like shine and luster. Tote around metallic envelope clutches for when a little elegant gloss is required. Lustrous and reflective metals in gold and silver offer a sleek and sexy alternative to basic clutches for evening. Remember that metallic tones work as a neutral so don’t be afraid to switch out your basic black and boring brown with a molten hot metallic.

## 4. Neon Envelope Clutch

![Neon Envelope Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/11/4_neon-envelope-clutch_350x211.jpg)

Neon bags are no longer reserved for those stuck in an 80s time warp as this color trend rocked the fashion world for the past few seasons. A neon envelope clutch is a cool and contemporary way to rock this highlighter hue for a bright and bold pop of color.

## 5. Animal Print Envelope Clutch

![Animal Print Envelope Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/11/5_animal-print-envelope-clutch_345x233.jpg)

An animal print accent polishes off any look and what better way to complete your outfit than to finish with an animal print envelope clutch. Whether or not the rest of your wardrobe has received the call of the wild, you should embrace an animal print accessory as they are super chic and always in style. From little black dresses to casual denim and sweaters, animal print offers an edgy finishing touch to your outfit.

## 6. Patent Envelope Clutch

![Patent Envelope Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/11/6_patent-envelope-clutch_600x426.jpg)

For a dressier leather envelope clutch alternative, a patent finish offers an elegant shine and dramatic touch. Select a patent envelope clutch in black leather for a sleek and sexy finish that you can sport for day or night. Its glossy texture and small shape is usually best reserved for evening-wear but when worn in a neutral or muted shade patent can be worn with ease all season long.

## 7. Glitter Envelope Clutch

![Glitter Envelope Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/11/7_glitter-envelope-clutch_600x434.jpg)

When a little sparkle is in order a glitter envelope clutch goes a long way. Perfect for nights out on the town or holiday soirees. Glitter and shimmer sets a fun and playful tone to your outfit, which will make you life of the party this season.

While handheld bags may come in a small and pretty package, these classic envelope clutches you should own deliver high-fashion style and functionality for the practical girl on the go. Whether in supple leather or exotic animal skins, every woman should tow around a classic envelope clutch for an effortlessly cool and chic accessory that’s more than just a pretty package. Which of these classic envelope clutches do you want to own?
