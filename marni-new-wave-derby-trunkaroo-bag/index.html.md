# The Story Behind Marni's New Wave Derby and Trunkaroo Bag...

When fashion becomes art, you discover silhouettes that move beyond mere existence to create sensations and stories. _Marni_, a brand synonymous with bold exploration and innovative design, has always had the ability to capture this magic. With their new releases, the New Wave Derby and the Trunkaroo bag, they invite us on a journey through form and function. Let’s explore the essence of these creations and the brand’s commitment to redefining style...

Imagine stepping into a world where fashion meets fantasy. A world where the New Wave Derby shoes and the Trunkaroo bag create a melody of styles, harmoniously dancing in our daily narratives. **Marni** (follow them on [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/marni/)) invites us to be part of their Spring/Summer 2025 Vol.2 collection, promising an unexpected voyage into design mastery.

## The New Wave Derby: Echoes of Elegance and Edginess

There’s something undeniably striking about Marni's New Wave Derby Shoes. They tell a story-a tale of contrast and craftsmanship, captured within every stitch and curve. Priced at **$1,195**, these shoes, made from polished calfskin leather, are both timeless and avant-garde. The [lace-up Derby shoe](https://www.marni.com/en-us/lace-ups-8059030481350.html) redefines elegance with its two-tone design and exaggerated bubble toe.

_The soft insole carries the promise of comfort and showcases Marni's logo as a mark of perfection_.

It’s more than just footwear; it's a statement piece, encouraging you to walk with both purpose and poise.

## A Glimpse of Modern Luxury: The Trunkaroo Bag

In the universe of Marni, bags are not mere accessories. They are companions, destined to accompany their owners on many adventures. The Trunkaroo bag is no exception, available for **$1,450**. It’s a beautiful harmony of practical design and artistic flair, crafted from calfskin leather. _
Photo: @marni

This rectangular mini shoulder bag features a front flap with magnetic closure, etched Marni lettering, and oversized stud details. The engaging combination of **functionality** and style_ ensures it stands as a beacon of modern luxury. You’ll adore how easily it can be transformed-from a shoulder bag to a chic clutch.

## The Creative Vision: Behind the Scenes at Marni

Francesco Risso, Marni's Creative Director, continues to push boundaries, leaving a distinct fingerprint on every piece. Under his guidance, the Spring/Summer 2025 Vol.2 collection blooms with fresh perspectives. Risso's unique touch is deeply woven into each creation, ensuring that every design embodies Marni’s ethos of innovation and individuality.

## The Art of Dressing: Jackets and Trousers That Echo Style

Marni’s clothing line seamlessly complements its accessories. The Baby Jacket, priced at **$2,200**, crafted from cotton poplin, offers a compact feel. Each layer of the Aurora Wispy Rose pattern is a canvas of scraped wet paint, echoing Marni’s daring spirit. Pair it with the **$995** high-waisted Capri Trousers, showcasing the captivating Cybele Maxi Rose pattern.

The trousers' heavy cotton poplin speaks of intricate development and contrast, while the pleats and printed designs enhance their narrative. The inclusion of invisible zips at the inner cuffs offers both style and practicality, ensuring they capture attention while remaining timeless.
