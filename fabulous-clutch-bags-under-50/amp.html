9 Fabulous Clutch Bags under $50 ...

ALDO Multicolored Clutch • GUESS Retro Slim Croc Clutch • LA REGALE Box Clutch • Jessica Simpson Leana Clutch • River Island Snakeskin Envelope Clutch • More ...

By Jelena

Clutch Bags are a necessity, a fashion statement, a must have, a way to turn a relatively simple outfit into something really special! Big or small, neutral or colorful, funky or classy, for day or evening – clutch bags are as necessary as clothes, jewelry, shoes or makeup. So, for all of you clutch-lovers/collectors and all of you interested getting a new accessory for the season – here are the clutch bags I absolutely adore!

1. ALDO Multicolored Clutch

Price: $45.00 at zappos.com
Oh-la-la! Now this is what a call a cute bag! I like the glossy finish, love the envelope design and absolutely adore both color combos! These two clutch bags could actually replace at least 10 others, not only because they are super-stylish, but also because you can start picking your shoes with a blindfold on and they will still match these bags!

2. GUESS Retro Slim Croc Clutch

Price: $38.00 at zappos.com
Oh, I absolutely love Guess clutch bags and I find this one totally adorable. This fab find is available in three colors – black, grey and rust and, since you probably have all the neutrals bags you could wish for, my advice would be to update your fall style with this accessory in the always welcome, easy to match eye-catching rust!

3. LA REGALE Box Clutch

Price: $48.00 at shop.nordstrom.com
Available in champagne and black – this luxurious-looking box clutch could be your beloved evening bag or piece of your bridal attire! I could actually use both black and champagne because I always need a nice black bag plus I still haven’t got a clue on where to start my search for a bridal clutch (all I know is that it has to be box clutch and have gold hardware).

4. Jessica Simpson Leana Clutch

Price: $45.00 at zappos.com
Retro kiss-lock closures, quilted details and shiny gold-tone accents are trendier than ever so, if you can’t afford or don’t feel like spending your cash on a luxurious, expensive evening bag, treat yourself to a budget-friendly Jessica Simpson wallet-clutch available in three classic, neutral colors.

5. River Island Snakeskin Envelope Clutch

Price: $49.32 at asos.com
Delicious plum shade, croc effect and beautiful gold-tone accents really make this clutch a must have! Over sized clutch bags are still very stylish (not to mention useful) and this baby even has a metal frame to keep it from sagging!

6. ASOS Feather Clutch

Price: $44.93 at us.asos.com.
Don’t ask me why but this feather clutch definitely reminds me of Paris Hilton! Not that I think you should adopt her attitude but hey… girl does have a great style! Definitely a piece to consider in case you have an important event coming up! Now, this is not one of those clutch bags you’ll get to wear a lot but hey, black clutch is never a waste so I’m giving it thumbs up.

7. ASOS Portfolio Clutch

Price: $35.94 at us.asos.com.
This stylish yellow number will certainly make your style is never plain or boring this fall and, since it’s big and kind of classic-y, you can even wear it with your chocolate brown, black or jade work ensembles. Dressing up to go to work has never been more fun!

8. ASOS Pyramid Lock Metal Tip Clutch

Price: $35.94 at us.asos.com
Awesome! Some would say 80’s and 90’s have been one of the worst fashion moments in history but I just have to disagree! Massive jewelry, colors, prints and reasonably-sized shoulder pads can look rather cool even in 2011! That’s why I’m suggesting this amazing, retro-looking piece! Yup, definitely go for metallic clutch bags!

9. ASOS Lightning Bolt Clutch

Price: $44.93 at us.asos.com.
Wanna jump into the time machine with me again? Well, here we gooooo! Coming to you live, from time in which Madonna wore massive earrings and MC Hammer was the only one who could touch that. LOL! I absolutely love this period and, if you’re looking at this clutch thinking “Whoa, I’d love to have it in my collection”, then I guess you don’t absolutely hate it either.

I know you love these clutch bags so I won’t even ask you what do you think about them. What I want to know is this - Which one of these clutch bags do you feel like you NEED to own ASAP?

Frequently Asked Questions

Are clutch bags only for fancy events or can I use them daily?
Oh, totally! While clutch bags are fab for fancy events, you can definitely rock them daily. They add a chic touch to any casual outfit. Who doesn't want a bit of glam, right?
Can I really find stylish clutch bags under $50?
Absolutely, you can! There are so many gorgeous options out there that look expensive but are super budget-friendly. It's like finding a little fashion gem!
How do I choose the right clutch bag to match my outfit?
Great question! I usually pick a clutch that complements the colors in my outfit. Or sometimes, go bold with a contrasting color. Honestly, it's all about what makes you feel fabulous!
Is it just celebrity styles that make clutch bags trendy?
Celebs definitely give bags that spotlight moment, but honestly, your personal style makes it trendy. Get what you're drawn to, and you'll be the fashion icon in no time!
Are there any brands that offer affordable yet stylish clutch bags?
Oh, for sure! Brands like ASOS have some killer styles that won't break the bank. And you can always find gems in unexpected places. It's all about keeping your eyes open!
