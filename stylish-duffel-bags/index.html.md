# 8 Stylish Duffel Bags ...

Duffel Bags are often thought of as being very sporty and generic. However, duffel bags are enjoying a burst of renewed popularity. The term ‘duffel’ can cover a range of bags styles, from those with drawstring closures to more generic hold-all or travel style bags. The drawstring, or pouch, bag is great for teaming with a casual dressed-down outfit. Duffel bags can also come in quite streamlined and upmarket designs. Below are just some stylish duffel bags for you to check out.

**Price:** $875.00 at [barneys.com](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.barneys.com/Rocco-Duffel/00505011274049,default,pd.html?cgid=WOMEN02)
This designer duffel bag is a modern cult favourite on the fashion scene. Incorporated the popular studded trend into the design, this bag also manages to be sleek and stylish. It has a cylindrical design with a top zip fastening and grab-top handles. It also features rhodium plated hardware and a studded base. This tan colour would make be great for every day dressing.

## 2. Dorothy Perkins Camel Tassel Duffel Bag

![Dorothy Perkins Camel Tassel Duffel Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tt/nu/e3v966ic54c932fa9be82982005190_400x470.jpg)

**Price:** $62.00 at [dorothyperkins.com](http://www.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Duffel bags can also refer to those with a pouch design, such as this one. This bag features a drawstring opening with a gold trim and tassel detailing. It is made from synthetic materials and has a camel coloured finish. The long shoulder strap makes it ideal for wearing across the body.

## 3. Chloé Aurore Duffel Leather Bag

![Chloé Aurore Duffel Leather Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ey/w1/aib4utzz54c932fbc0773662548290_400x434.jpg)

## 4. Givenchy Medium Antigona Duffel Bag

![Givenchy Medium Antigona Duffel Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nj/qh/o9avw5zk54c932fcb10ae773874864_400x342.jpg)

## 5. Calvin Klein Vienna Duffel

![Calvin Klein Vienna Duffel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s0/nk/vjvw4f8454c932fdaeca1746863157_400x459.jpg)

## 6. Fendi Chameleon Duffel Bag

_ **Price:** $2420.00 at barneys.com_
This duffel bag has a more rotund, square shape than its counterparts. It has a clean design and features dual grab-top handles, a detachable shoulder strap, and gold-tone hardware. It also features metal studs at the base. At over $2000, this bag is definitely for someone who means business.

## 7. Dorothy Perkins Black Zip Front Duffle Bag

## 8. Meredith Wendell Leather Duffel Bag

![Meredith Wendell Leather Duffel Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nj/jl/heiygjsq54c933010bc81658020299_400x351.jpg)
