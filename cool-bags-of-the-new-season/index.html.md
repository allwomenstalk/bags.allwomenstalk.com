# 8 Cool Bags of the New Season ...

A women’s outfit is never complete without her handbag, which is why these cool bags of the new season are the top finishing touch for all looks and outfit choices this fall. The cool new bags of the season begin to take form when size, shape and detail speak volumes and result in the perfect accent piece to tote around all season long.

## 1. Handheld Minaudiere Bag

![Handheld Minaudiere Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/10/1_handheld-minaudiere-bag_470x297.jpg)

When nothing else seems to suit your look, the handheld minaudiere bag is always just right. Sleek shapes and small sizes allow you to carry your minaudiere bag in tow for all occasions. Boxy structures and architectural designs are elevated with cool finishes and luxe materials like crocodile skins for an edgy way to maximize your minaudiere’s small but chic style. Don’t forget big things come in small packages!

## 2. Oversized Clutch

![Oversized Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/10/2_oversized-clutch_599x409.jpg)

This season clutches go from small compact bags to practical and appropriate for the girl on the go. Oversized clutches are utilitarian alternatives to handheld minaudiere’s reserved for evening occasions. Their oversized proportions make them the perfect travel companion to hold everything a woman about town needs and more. A large oversized clutch can be held in hand or tucked under your arm for sensible and glamorous wear with ease throughout the day.

## 3. Square Shaped Bag

![Square Shaped Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/10/3_square-shaped-bag_600x711.jpg)

It’s hip to be square this fall with square shaped bags taking over as the style of the season. Square bags have a ladylike and feminine quality about them that offers a fuss-free and sensible design for the modern woman. The structure and solid framework of a square shaped bag is understated and classically cool for the fashion forward minimalist.

## 4. Fur Pelt Bag

![Fur Pelt Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/10/4_fur-pelt-bag_600x560.jpg)

Whether faux or real, the fur pelt bag is the go-to accessory for urban fashionista’s everywhere. Fall’s biggest and most lust-worthy texture is and will always be fur so prepare to get your hands on this plush accessory. From short animal hairs to shaggy shearlings, fur is a classic piece for fall that never goes out of style. Stick to traditional fur colors for a luxe look that will last throughout the season.

## 5. Gold Embroidered Bag

![Gold Embroidered Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/10/5_gold-embroidered-bag_400x261.jpg)

Get in touch with the baroque trend of the season with gilded embroidered bags. Intricate embroidery offers a regal finishing touch to all looks, which will have you looking and feeling like royalty this fall. For a serious decadent edge don a gold embroidered bag with brocade prints and patterns for added old world flair, dripping in drama and elegance.

## 6. Embellished Bag

![Embellished Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/10/6_embellished-bag_300x300.jpg)

Be jeweled this season with an embellished bag for ultra high glamour and opulent decadence. From oversized gems and crystals to exaggerated trims, fall gets its rocks off with statement adornments on superstar bags. Throw caution to the wind this season and sport an over the top and ornate embellished bag for fall.

## 7. Metallic Leather Tote Bag

![Metallic Leather Tote Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/10/7_metallic-leather-tote-bag_600x659.jpg)

For high-octane shine and gilded treasure, a metallic tote bag is the ideal way to sparkle this season. Molten like metals in gold, bronze and silver set the runways ablaze and glimmered in every shape and form imaginable. To make the right lustrous impact, let your metallic bag be the star of the show as too many metallic pieces can cheapen your outfit and look. Instead, pair your metallic bag with basic neutrals like a little black dress for a fresh way to dazzle this season.

## 8. Purple Bag

_ Purple is the must-have jewel tone for fall. The lush opulent purple hues of the season range from perfect plums to demure mauve's and vivid violets. Rich purples reign supreme this fall in the form of luxe bags and add just the right amount of bold pop needed during the cool season for a majestic and glamorous finish to any outfit fit for a queen.

This fall, the cool bags of the new season are all about making style statements that soar to new high-fashion heights with unique sizes, shapes and details. From opulent jewel tones to over the top embellishments and luxurious furs, fall’s cool bags of the new season are welcomed in an arrival of fresh silhouettes and playful designs. Which one of these cool bags of the new season are you hoping to add to your collection this fall? What’s your fall go-to bag?
