# 8 Colourful Wallets ...

While most fashion ‘rules’ suggest that a neutral handbag is best for daily activities, why not inject some colour in other ways? A brightly hued wallet would be a fun way to add some colour into your day. It’s guaranteed to add some liveliness to the contents of your handbag. Take a look at a few colourful wallets below.

## 1. Mulberry Postman’s Lock Continental Wallet

![Mulberry Postman’s Lock Continental Wallet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nd/ze/p01z52wa54c8fcd8b6ce3504381310_400x238.jpg)

**Price:** $350.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/wlUHFD)
The classic Mulberry wallet is given a modern twist in this watermelon pink leather. It features an envelope design, Mulberry’s iconic postman’s lock fastening, and zip fastening slit pocket at the back. It is lined in dark brown twill and features both interior slit and zip pockets.

## 2. Comme Des Garcons Long Zip Wallet

![Comme Des Garcons Long Zip Wallet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uw/ze/c0e69nbw54c8fcd99954c008499795_366x400.jpg)

**Price:** $290.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This long zip wallet has a simple and streamlined design. As far as colourful wallets go, this green leather one is one of the brightest. It features an exposed gold zip around three sides. The interior of the wallet houses six card slots, two bill pockets, and a snapped coin pocket.

## 3. Topshop Leather Ruched Twist Purse

![Topshop Leather Ruched Twist Purse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cj/y8/vmkn7au254c8fcda74e08686995285_400x216.jpg)

**Price:** $32.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/8nkdYp)
This leather wallet comes in a burnt orange colour. It has a fold over flap design, and features twisted detailing at the front. The interior of the wallet houses both slit and zip pockets. This wallet is an affordable trend-based accessory.

## 4. Forever21 Leopard Print Leatherette Wallet

![Forever21 Leopard Print Leatherette Wallet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/br/cx/i8xg7eki54c8fcdb71dda957867184_400x281.jpg)

**Price:** $7.80 at [forever21.com](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Stay pretty in pink with this leopard print wallet from Forever21. It is made from leatherette materials and features an all over leopard print pattern. It has a matching zip closure and comes with a removable wrist strap. It is fully lined and has eight credit card slots and a zip pocket.

## 5. Comme Des Garcons Embossed Classic Wallet

_ **Price:** $370.00 at openingceremony.us_
Comme des Garcons have a range of colourful wallets on offer. This one comes in bright blue leather and features an all over embossed pattern. It has six card slots, a pen holder, and press stud pouch. It also features a gold embossed designer logo on the interior.

## 6. Marni Leather Wallet

![Marni Leather Wallet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lx/kk/s9bu6hds54c8fcdd45919984514948_400x255.jpg)

**Price:** $245.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/ZvaVqv)
Colourful wallets don’t get much brighter than this fluorescent orange number. It has a rectangular fold-over design and features two silver press-stud fastenings at the front. It also has an interior zip fastening and coin and card compartments. This wallet will add an instant hit of colour to your handbag.

## 7. Ameka Leather Zip around Wallet

![Ameka Leather Zip around Wallet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sq/q2/tfcgpez654c8fcde62602796050506_400x251.jpg)

**Price:** $51.00 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/I243DM)
This lime green wallet has a washed out finish. This wallet would be a great option for those after a more subdued colour. The wallet has a contrasting zip around closure. It also has internal zip and side pockets, as well as plenty of card slots.

## 8. Forever21 Shiny Clutch Wallet

![Forever21 Shiny Clutch Wallet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wg/yn/vyqtiabr54c8fcdf6689a890386523_400x179.jpg)

**Price:** $7.80 at [forever21.com](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
The graphic print on this wallet is quite eye catching. It comes in a black and red pattern and has a glossy finish. The interior of the wallet houses 12 card slots. This slim wallet is also very structured, so it doesn’t allow much room to give.
