# 7 Luxe Embellished Clutches ...

It’s all in the details for Fall and the perfect way to add that extra element to your fall wardrobe is in the form of a luxe embellished clutch. Smaller, hand held bags are making their return in fashion, and naturally the clutch was a designer favourite. Designers dotted their best bags with a variety of embellishments, from delicate beaded embroidery to large all over studs. Making your own style statement this season is as easy as choosing a luxe embellished clutch to show off your own personality - whether you’re super feminine or have an avant-garde edge there’s a type of embellishment for you!

## 1. Dorothy Perkins Grey Pearl and Diamond Clutch

![Dorothy Perkins Grey Pearl and Diamond Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/08/1_dorothy-perkins-grey-pearl-and-diamond-clutch_600x513.jpg)

**Price:** $57 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

All over embellishment makes for an eye-catching statement when paired with any outfit. The all over 3D texture adds visual interest and the large rhinestones give off just enough sparkle suitable for daytime or nighttime dressing. This small clutch comes with tiny silver chain perfect to sling over your shoulder when you get tired of holding it.

## 2. ASOS Jewel Lace Clutch

![ASOS Jewel Lace Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/08/2_asos-jewel-lace-clutch_600x363.jpg)

**Price:** $51 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/P7TWTA)

This fold-over clutch from ASOS features a lace overlay with jeweled embellishment, stud detailing and a sequined floral design. As a larger wristlet clutch, this bag will still hold all your unmentionables and then some! You can fit in with the brocade baroque trend this fall by donning this elegantly chic clutch.

## 3. Sam Edelman Holmby Embellished Clutch Bag

![Sam Edelman Holmby Embellished Clutch Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/08/edelman_398x296.jpg)

**Price:** $298 at [lordandtaylor.com](http://www.lordandtaylor.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)For an edgier take on things, Sam Edelman’s Holmby clutch provides a city cool bag with pointy studs and sparkling stones. The neutral tones of the clutch make the embellished accents stand out even more, making it the ultimate versatile bag. Side zippers give this clutch a little added touch with a wristlet strap for easy carrying.

## 4. Flirt Catalogue Embellished Clutch

_ **Price:** $58 at flirtcatalog.com_

This clutch features a variety of embellishments. From multi-shaped silver beads to faceted diamonds and black rhinestones, this clutch has it all! Set against a creamy leather the additional stud detail at the clasp adds a little something extra to up the luxe ante.

## 5. Aftershock Paris Metal Embellished Clutch

_ **Price:** $45 at newlook.com_

Get your rocks on with this silver metallic clutch. The rectangular box style is super trendy and is a new take on an old favourite. A detachable chain gives you the option of hand-held or shoulder carrying. With large coloured diamante detail against a silvery backdrop, this clutch makes a standout statement.

## 6. ASOS Beaded Clutch

![ASOS Beaded Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bags/2012/08/6_asos-beaded-clutch_600x300.jpg)

**Price:** $85 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/byXrSX)

Made from a wool blend fabric, this ASOS clutch is the ultimate fall clutch you can still rock for winter. The black on black fabric and jewels create depth and dimension in this clutch with added faceted stones, sequin detailing and floral designs. All this adds up to equal the flawless mix of sparkle and shine.

## 7. Rebecca Minkoff Studded Fling Clutch

_ **Price:** $205 at endless.com_

For a toned down simpler embellished clutch, try this Rebecca Minkoff studded bag. This clutch perfectly suits the darker rock ‘n’ roll vibe on the runway for fall and its black and silver colour combination can be worn with any outfit for an instant add of edgy cool.

From ornamented minaudiere to beautiful box clutches and over the shoulder carryalls, the embellished clutch is making a huge style statement with a variety of beads, baubles and jewels for that flawless hint of sparkle and shine. What are your favourite bag embellishments?
