# 9 Outrageously Terrific Tips for Choosing a Leather Handbag ...

A Leather Handbag is an accessory that will often come in handy. A quality leather handbag should last you years. Whether you choose a timeless black or brown leather handbag, or something more colourful, it should be a design that reflects your personal style. Below are a few tips for choosing the perfect leather handbag.

## 1. Functionality

Before you purchase a leather handbag, ask yourself what you want out of it. Will it be an everyday bag or will it be for special occasions only? What kind of items will you be carrying in it? Will you need from for a laptop and books, or will you just be carrying the essentials. These are all things to take into account when looking for the right handbag for you.

## 2. Leather Quality

First of all, is what you’re buying really leather? There’s the age-old ‘flame’ trick, but this is not always a guarantee. Make sure you are buying from a reputable source. Full-grain and top-grain leathers are known to be of higher quality. Better quality leathers are also more likely to last longer.

## 3. Price Point

Leather handbags are usually more expensive than their non-leather counterparts. As such, you should be expecting to pay a little extra. Also, better quality leather usually comes at a much higher price. It pays to do your research before buying. That way you will know just how much money to save up.

## 4. Design Details

When it comes to handbags, design details often play a big part in the purchase-making decision. Some people prefer a minimal aesthetic while others welcome details such as decorative hardware and embellishments. Bags with streamlined, minimal silhouettes tend to be timeless, while decorative embellishments have the potential to date easily.

## 5. The Finish

The finish is an important factor to take into account when choosing a leather handbag. Patent leather is great for keeping clean, while supple leathers like suede have the potential to discolour easily. Also, leather has the potential to age and appear distressed. If you want a bag that will stay pristine, choose one with a structured silhouette and a glossier leather finish.

## 6. Test Run

Before buying a leather handbag, try it on in-store to see how it feels. Is it comfortable to wear? Does it give? Things like shoulder strap length, weight, and size are also things worth checking out.

## 7. Strength

Depending on how much you intend to carry in your leather handbag, you may need to find out the maximum weight that it will hold. Carrying more than a bag can handle can lead to breakage. In general, you shouldn’t be carrying more than a few kilos. But if you will be carrying a substantial wait, choose a bag with wider straps to ease the load.

## 8. Colour

What colour will work best in your wardrobe? Black and tan leathers are great for an everyday bag, while brightly coloured leathers are more statement-making. If you intend to buy a larger leather bag, stick to neutral colours. Bright colours work better on smaller clutch bags.

## 9. Care Guide

How much are you prepared to care for your handbag? With a leather handbag, upkeep is a must to keep it in top condition. Most will require a leather or all-weather protector, and this needs to be applied on a regular basis. Specialised storage is also something worth considering.

What you want out of your leather handbag will depend on your personal situation. A handbag is often a woman’s go-to accessory, so choosing the right one is an important decision. As you can see, there are a range of things to take into account when purchasing a leather handbag. What are your best tips for choosing a leather handbag?
