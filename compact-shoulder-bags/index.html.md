# 8 Compact Shoulder Bags ...

When it comes to handbags, the trend for the last couple of years has revolved around oversized bags. These large handbags, especially when packed to the brim, can put a strain on our backs and posture. There is a slight shift now towards smaller, more compact handbags - ones that provide just enough room for the essentials. Check out a few compact shoulder bags below.

## 1. Topshop Colour Block Cross Body Bag

![Topshop Colour Block Cross Body Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oc/mv/sd6lisio54c8fe14adeac801911775_350x272.jpg)

**Price:** $40.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/ESFBaA)
The combination of neutral and pastel colours is a favourite for this season. This trend will no doubt be filtering into the market of compact shoulder bags. This mini shoulder bag features a subtle colour block design. It has a fold over flap, gold-tone hardware, and long shoulder strap. Pink can sometimes be a hard colour to incorporate into an outfit, so try and keep your clothes as neutral as possible.
