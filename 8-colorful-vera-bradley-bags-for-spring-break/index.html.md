# 8 Colorful Vera Bradley Bags for Spring Break ...

It’s finally time for Spring Break, and I’ve already started setting aside all of the fun-in-the-sun goodies I’ll need for my holiday. I’ve got my sunglasses, swimsuit, sandals… now it’s time to choose a few cute new bags to carry! I love the new Vera Bradley Spring Break collection, so I’m sure to buy at least one of them, but which one? Here are 8 colorful Vera Bradley bags for Spring Break 2011.

## 1. Vera Bradley Rachel

![Vera Bradley Rachel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vs/oo/lvyfzj1r54c8f83c74fdf919032765_360x305.jpg)

**Price:** $54.00 at [verabradley.com](http://www.verabradley.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
What a great little bag for Spring Break! It’s small, but big enough for all of your essentials, with a magnetic close flap and adjustable strap. It’s available in 18 brightly-colored floral prints. My favorite is the Baroque… it’s gorgeous!

## 2. Vera Bradley Eloise

![Vera Bradley Eloise](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jv/j0/o1ycmqrm54c8f83d9bac1225796895_284x392.jpg)

**Price:** $74.00 at [verabradley.com](http://www.verabradley.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I absolutely must have this bag! It’s clearly vintage-inspired, right out of the 1940’s, with a top handle that slings right across your arm and a dainty kiss-lock closure. Choose your favorite from the 18 available sunshine-bright prints. This bag looks best in Buttercup… what a pretty bag for Spring Break!

## 3. Vera Bradley Mandy

![Vera Bradley Mandy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fg/bb/jab3q7y254c8f83e95c4b758575043_302x369.jpg)

**Price:** $64.00 at [verabradley.com](http://www.verabradley.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Are you mad about organization, happiest with as many pockets and compartments as you can get? Then this is the Vera Bradley Spring Break bag for you! It’s available in 16 lush floral prints, and of which have a recessed zipper that opens to reveal six handy interior pockets. I love it in the Dahlia print!

## 4. Vera Bradley Lizzy

![Vera Bradley Lizzy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e0/wc/nialy40254c8f83f79402082586000_264x370.jpg)

**Price:** $46.00 at [verabradley.com](http://www.verabradley.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Vera Bradley’s Lizzy bag may be small, but she’s just what you need for a day out… load your keys, lip gloss, cell phone, wallet and camera and you’re ready to go! Adjust the shoulder strap and carry this bag cross-body in whichever of the 12 brilliant floral prints you like best… I love it in Make Me Blush!

## 5. Vera Bradley Hotsy Totsy Hobo

![Vera Bradley Hotsy Totsy Hobo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k1/qb/ladthn7v54c8f8407c352646913007_306x352.jpg)

**Price:** $48.00 at [verabradley.com](http://www.verabradley.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
How very sassy! This bag is my favorite from the Vera Bradley Spring Break 201 collection. It’s a classic hobo bag, with a clever asymmetrical slip pocket on the front with a contrasting scalloped trim. It’s available in three pretty spring-time prints. Try it in Folkloric!

## 6. Vera Bradley Mailbag

![Vera Bradley Mailbag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ls/qx/pwnpvvhe54c8f84193687083000612_235x379.jpg)

**Price:** $51.00 at [verabradley.com](http://www.verabradley.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Form follows function with this ultra-organized mailbag, loaded with clever zippered pockets and plenty of room for all of your treasures. The single shoulder strap is adjustable for a perfect fit, and the bag is available in five sweet floral prints. I love it in Slate Blooms!

## 7. Vera Bradley Sugar and Spice Crossbody

![Vera Bradley Sugar and Spice Crossbody](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ro/ss/g3f9y0mg54c8f84264cbc831783545_231x347.jpg)

**Price:** $42.00 at [verabradley.com](http://www.verabradley.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
What’s made of sugar and spice and everything nice? Well, this Vera Bradley handbag, of course! It’s completely adorable, mini-sized with a kiss-lock frame and an outside slot for keeping close tabs on your cell phone. Choose from three cute spring time prints… I love the Boysenberry print best!

With so many sweet and sassy Vera Bradley Spring Break 2011 bags, how is a girl supposed to choose just one? I love the kiss-lock bags, but they’re all so cute! Which of these Vera Bradley handbags do you like best, and in which print? Please share!
